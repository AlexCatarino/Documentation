The organization homepage is located in the Algorithm Lab. At the top of the Algorithm Lab page, you can find which organizations your account is associated with. You can also change which organization your account is currently connected to allowing you to choose which resources you wish to use. From here, you can also create a new organization, for which you will need to provide a unique name.

The Organization tab, which is located in the Algorithm Lab, provides a central hub where you can easily manage your organization's members and resources.

Team Management

The members in your organization can collaborate on projects and also share the resources available to the organization. You can grant members various user-based permissions allowing manage resources and other aspects of the organization. Learn more in the Team Management documentation.

Support seats allow your members to submit support tickets to QuantConnect. You can lean more about support seats in the Support documentation.

Resources Management

Every organization comes with a free B-micro backtesting node and R-micro research node. However, you can also add additional nodes, allowing you to run faster backtests and more concurrent live algorithms. There is a variety of nodes with different specifications that can be added and given unique identifying names. Learn more in the Resource Management documentation.

The Object Store is a project specific data storage feature available in QuantConnect. Each organization receives a free 500 MB of storage. But, you can also subscribe to additional storage space for the Object Store from the research page.

Alpha Management

The Alpha Streams market place allows you to lisence alphas written by members our community of quants. Qualified organizations can apply to be clients of the market place, giving them licensing access to the available alphas. Learn more in the Alpha Management documentation.

Organization Support

QuantConnect's community is a great resource for project support. However, for more more personalized assistance, you can submit a request for support from a QuantConnect engineer. In order for a member of your organization to submit a support ticket, they must have a support seat. You can learn more about support seats in the Support documentation.

Billing

You can pay for resources as you require them, but you can also subscribe to one of the monthly subscription plans available.There are 4 tiers of subscriptions plans that provide various levels of resources and scalability. Find out which subscription plan is best suited for you in the Billing documentation.
