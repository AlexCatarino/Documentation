Educators are compensated in knowledge, commissions, and exposure. In terms of knowledge, as you contribute courses to the Learning Center, you deepen your understanding of the topics you teach. In terms of commissions, you can earn a $3,250 commission per course and a 70% revenue share for your courses. In terms of exposure, when your courses are listed in the Learning Center, your name and social media accounts are displayed to get you more exposure to the QuantConnect community.
