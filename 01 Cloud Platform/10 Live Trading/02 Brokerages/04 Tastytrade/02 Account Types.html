tastytrade supports cash and margin accounts. To set the account type in an algorithm, see the tastytrade brokerage model documentation.

Create an Account

Follow the account creation wizard on the tastytrade website to create an account.

Paper Trading

The tastytrade API doesn't support paper trading, but you can follow these steps to simulate it with QuantConnect:

  1. In the Initializeinitialize method of your algorithm, set the tastytrade brokerage model and your account type.
  2. Deploy your algorithm with the QuantConnect Paper Trading brokerage.
