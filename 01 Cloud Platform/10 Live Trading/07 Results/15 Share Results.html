You can share your live results page with anyone, even if they don't have a QuantConnect account. To share your results, follow these steps:

  1. In the Share Results section of the live results page, click Make Public.
  2. Click Live Stream.

    3. A URL to view the live results page is copied your clipboard.

  3. Share the link with a friend or colleague.

To stop sharing your live results page, in the Share Results section of the live results page, click Make Private.
