You can share your live results with anyone, even if they don't have a QuantConnect account. To share your results, follow these steps:

  1. In the Share Results section of the live results page, click Make Public.
  2. To get a URL that you can share with a friend or collegue, click Live Stream.
  3. To get an iframe that you can embed on a website, click Embed Code.

To stop sharing your live results page, in the Share Results section of the page, click Make Private.
