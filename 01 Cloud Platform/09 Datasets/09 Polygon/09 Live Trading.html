Follow these steps to use the Polygon data provider in live trading:

  1. Open the Brokerages page and then click the name of your brokerage.
  2. On the brokerage integration page, scroll down to the Deploy Live Algorithms section and start to follow the deployment instructions.
  3. In the Data Provider section of the deployment wizard, click Show.
  4. Click the Data Provider 1 field and then click Polygon from the drop-down menu.
  5. Enter your Polygon API Key.
  6. Click Save.
  7. Follow the remaining steps in the Deploy Live Algorithms tutorial for your brokerage.
