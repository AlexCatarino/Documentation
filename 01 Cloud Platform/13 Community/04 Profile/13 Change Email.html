Follow these steps to change your email:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. In the top navigation bar, click yourUsername > My Account.
  3. On your Account page, in the Security section, click Change Email.
  4. Enter your current email.
  5. Enter your new email.
  6. Enter your new email again to confirm.
  7. Click Change Email.
  8. Check your inbox and click Change Email to confirm.
