In order to enter your team into the competition, please fill out the following Google Form.

This will serve as your initial submission to the competition, which you can later update.

The team captain will have to enter the following in order to submit the Google form:

  • Team's University
  • Team Name
  • The email with which the captain is registered on the QuantConnect platform
  • The emails with which the team members are registered on the QuantConnect platform
  • A confirmation that all team members have updated their Names, Profile Picture, and LinkedIn Profiles on the QuantConnect platform
  • The link to the backtest of the team's submission
  • The team's investment thesis.

Further Information on the Investment Thesis:

Introduce your strategy concepts and philosophy to help investors and funds understand your investment idea. Communicating your thesis and attracting investment for your projects is critical to quantitative finance. You can encourage discussion and contributions by posting interesting ideas.

Once you submit the form, the QuantConnect team will review your submission and list it to the Quant League.
