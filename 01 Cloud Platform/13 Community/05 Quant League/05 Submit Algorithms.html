Before you submit an algorithm, you must run a backtest successfully. Your algorithm must observe the following rules:

  • Your algorithm is unique. Teams are penalized for submitting algorithms that are nearly identical to other competitors.
  • Your algorithm trades liquid securities. For equities, this includes the top 1000 stocks, crypto top 10 coins, forex top 20 pairs, and futures the top 30 contracts.
  • The maximum leverage is 2.

Follow these steps to submit your team's algorithm:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Open the page.
    1. In the top navigation bar, click Research.
    2. Click on League tab.
  3. On the league page, scroll down to find your team's submission. Newly registered organizations will be on the bottom of the page.
  4. Click on your team's View button.
  5. Click on the Update Competition button on the right side of the screen.
  6. Fill the Update League Submission form with the algorithm's name, description, and attach the backtest.
  7. Follow these steps to attach a backtest:
    1. Click Attach Backtest.
    2. Click the Project field and then select the project that contains the backtest that you want to attach from the drop-down menu.

      3. You can only attach projects that you own.

    3. Click the Backtest field and then select a backtest from the drop-down menu.
  8. Click on the Update League Submission button.

Your algorithm has been submitted and is competing in the league. Congratulations!
