The organization homepage is located in the Algorithm Lab. At the top of the Algorithm Lab page, you can find which organizations your account is associated with. You can also change which organization your account is currently connected to allowing you to choose which resources you wish to use. From here, you can also create a new organization, for which you will need to provide a unique name.

New organizations will be initialized with the default community plan, which includes 1 backtesting node and 1 research node. You can learn more about the subscription plans available in the Subscription Plans documentation. The Organization tab, which is located in the Algorithm Lab, provides a central hub where you can easily manage your organization's members and resources.

Team Management

The members page allows you to manage the members in the organization. The members in your organization can collaborate on projects and also share the resources available to the organization. Important information like the number of members and active members in your organization can be found on the dashboard. An active member is a member who has been online in the last hour. You can add an additional member using either their name or the email address associated with their account and also add support seats. Support seats allow your members to submit support tickets to QuantConnect. You can lean more about support seats in the Support documentation.

Resources Management

The research page displays the backtesting, research and live trading nodes that are available to your organization. Every organization comes with a free B-micro backtesting node and R-micro research node. However, you can also add additional nodes, allowing you to run faster backtests and more concurrent live algorithms. There is a variety of nodes with different specifications that can be added and given unique identifying names.

The Object Store is a project specific data storage feature available in QuantConnect. Each organization receives a free 500 MB of storage. But, you can also subscribe to additional storage space for the Object Store from the research page.

Alpha Management

From the alpha management tab, you can view and manage the alphas you have licensed from AlphaStreams. Your organization must be a client of Alpha Streams in order to lisence alphas from the market. Organizations that are not yet clients of Alpha Streams can apply to get access to the Alpha Streams market from the alpha management tab.

Organization Support

The support page allows you to submit a support ticket to QuantConnect. In order for a member to submit a support ticket they must have a support seat. You can learn more about support seats in the Support documentation. You can also find any open tickets that have been submitted by your organization and view closed tickets.

Billing

The billing page displays your billing history for subscription plans or resources and also lets manage your payment details.
