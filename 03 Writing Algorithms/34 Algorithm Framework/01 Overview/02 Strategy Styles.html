The algorithm framework works best for portfolio rebalancing, ranking, multi-factor style strategies. The portfolio construction models are designed to weigh and invest in the assets as the signals values shift.

Some strategies might find it difficult to fit this model. A technical entry signal might not have a defined hold period, but will follow the market to determine when to exit their positions. This style of strategy can be addressed in two ways:

  1. Short Term Signals - Emitting insights for short periods until they are no longer valid.
  2. Emitting Flat Signals - Emitting a flat insight when the exit signal is desired.
