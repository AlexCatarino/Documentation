Log statements are strings recorded during a backtest that are best to record key decision moments. Because backtests replay millions of data events, a log statement placed in the wrong place can generate gigabytes of logs which can be difficult to read. Take care when placing the log to keep them readable. 

Debug($"Signal triggered. Placing limit order: {limit}");
self.debug(f"Signal triggered. Placing limit order: {limit}")

For more information about log statements, see Logging.
