You can use Fundamental universes to select Stock CFD assets for trading based on corporate fundamental data. ETF Constituents Universes can also select the CFD versions of stocks in an exchange-traded fund. This feature is powerful for European clients seeking to trade US ETF products.

Only the Interactive Brokers CFD integration supports trading Stock-CFD products. QuantConnect does not have historical data for Interactive Brokers CFD products; however, you can use the LiveMode flag to swap to the CFD equivalents for live trading. You must select Interactive Brokers as a data provider in live-trading.
