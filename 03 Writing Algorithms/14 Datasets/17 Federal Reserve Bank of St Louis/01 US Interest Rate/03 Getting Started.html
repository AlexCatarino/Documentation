The following snippet demonstrates how to access data from the US Interest Rate dataset.

self.interest_rate = self.RiskFreeInterestRateModel.GetInterestRate(self.Time)
_interestRate = RiskFreeInterestRateModel.GetInterestRate(Time);

To find the average interest rate between two dates, call the GetRiskFreeRate method of RiskFreeInterestRateModel.

risk_free_rate = self.RiskFreeInterestRateModel.GetRiskFreeRate(self.Time - timedelta(365), self.Time)
var riskFreeRate = RiskFreeInterestRateModel.GetRiskFreeRate(Time.AddDays(-365), Time);
