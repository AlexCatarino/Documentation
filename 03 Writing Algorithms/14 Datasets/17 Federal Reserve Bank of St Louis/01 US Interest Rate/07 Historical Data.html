To get historical US Interest Rate data, call the static GetRiskFreeRate method of RiskFreeInterestRateModel. 

risk_free_rate = self.RiskFreeInterestRateModel.GetRiskFreeRate(self.Time - timedelta(365), self.Time)
var riskFreeRate = RiskFreeInterestRateModel.GetRiskFreeRate(Time.AddDays(-365), Time)

GetRiskFreeRate gives you the average value between two dates.
