The following snippet demonstrates how to request data from the FRED dataset:

self.dataset_symbol = self.add_data(Fred, "USARECDM", Resolution.DAILY).symbol
_datasetSymbol = AddData<Fred>("USARECDM", Resolution.Daily).Symbol;
QuantConnect provides helpers to find some of the available datasets from FRED: 
self.dataset_symbol = self.add_data(Fred, Fred.OECDRecessionIndicators.UNITED_STATES_FROM_PEAK_THROUGH_THE_TROUGH, Resolution.DAILY).symbol
_datasetSymbol = AddData<Fred>(Fred.OECDRecessionIndicators.UnitedStatesFromPeakThroughTheTrough, Resolution.Daily).Symbol;
