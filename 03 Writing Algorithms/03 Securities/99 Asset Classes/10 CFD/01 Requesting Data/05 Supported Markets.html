The following Market enumeration members are available for CFDs:

To set the market for a security, pass a market argument to the AddCfdadd_cfd method.

_symbol = AddCfd("XAUUSD", market: Market.Oanda).Symbol;
self._symbol = self.add_cfd("XAUUSD", market=Market.OANDA).symbol

The brokerage models have a default market for each asset class. If you set a brokerage model, you may not need to specify the market to use. In backtesting, set the market for CFDs to OANDA.
