Request US Equity data in your algorithm to receive a feed of asset prices in the OnDataon_data method.

Every stock traded in the US Securities Information Processors (SIP) CTA/UTP feed is available. Over-the-Counter (OTC) trades are not included. For more information about the specific dataset we use for backtests, see the US Equities dataset listing.

To trade US Equities live, you can use the QuantConnect data provider or one of the brokerage data providers.
