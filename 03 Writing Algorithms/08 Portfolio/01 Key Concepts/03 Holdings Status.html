You can identify a position as long- or short-biased based on the sign of the holding quantity. Long positions have a positive quantity and short positions have a negative quantity. The SecurityHolding object has four properties signing the investment status.

var holdings = Portfolio[_symbol];
var quantity = holdings.Quantity;
var invested = holdings.Invested;
var isLong = holdings.IsLong;
var isShort = holdings.IsShort;
holdings = self.portfolio[self._symbol]
quantity = holdings.quantity
invested = holdings.invested
is_long = holdings.is_long
is_short = holdings.is_short

The quantity of Cryptocurrencies and crypto futures can be lower than the minimum order size. The value of the Investedinvested property can trueTrue when quantity is not zero.
