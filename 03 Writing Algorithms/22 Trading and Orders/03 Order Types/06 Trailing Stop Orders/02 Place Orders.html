To send a trailing stop order, call the TrailingStopOrder method and provide a Symbol, quantity, trailing amount, and the trailing type. You can also provide a tag and order properties to the TrailingStopOrder method. If you do not have sufficient capital for the order, it is rejected.

TrailingStopOrder(symbol, quantity, trailingAmount, trailingAsPercentage, tag, orderProperties);
self.TrailingStopOrder(symbol, quantity, trailingAmount, trailingAsPercentage, tag, orderProperties)

LEAN calculates the initial stop price with the current security price.

To buy an asset with a trailing stop order, set the the trailing amount and trailing type.

// When Bitcoin trades up to its current price ($45,000) plus a $10,000 trailing amount, buy 1 Bitcoin
TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 10000, false);
// When Bitcoin trades up to its current price ($45,000) plus a 20% trailing amount, buy 1 Bitcoin
TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 0.2, true);
# When Bitcoin trades up to to its current price ($45,000) plus a $10,000 trailing amount, buy 1 Bitcoin
self.TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 10000, False)
# When Bitcoin trades up to to its current price ($45,000) plus a 20% trailing amount, buy 1 Bitcoin
self.TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 0.2, True)

To sell an asset with a trailing stop order, set the trailing amount.

// When Bitcoin trades down to its current price ($56,000) minus a $10,000 trailing amount, sell 1 Bitcoin
TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 10000, false);
// When Bitcoin trades down to its current price ($56,000) minus a 20% trailing amount, sell 1 Bitcoin
TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 0.2, true);
# When Bitcoin trades down to its current price ($56,000) minus a $10,000 trailing amount, sell 1 Bitcoin
self.TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 10000, False)
# When Bitcoin trades down to its current price ($56,000) minus a 20% trailing amount, sell 1 Bitcoin
self.TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 0.2, True)

To send a trailing stop order with an initial stop price, call the TrailingStopOrder method and provide a Symbol, quantity, stop price, trailing amount, and the trailing type. You can also provide a tag and order properties to the TrailingStopOrder method. If you do not have sufficient capital for the order, it is rejected.

TrailingStopOrder(symbol, quantity, stopPrice, trailingAmount, trailingAsPercentage, tag, orderProperties);
self.TrailingStopOrder(symbol, quantity, stopPrice, trailingAmount, trailingAsPercentage, tag, orderProperties)

To buy an asset with a trailing stop order, set the stop price above the current ask price and the trailing amount.

// When Bitcoin trades up to $60,000 with a $10000 trailing amount, buy 1 Bitcoin
TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 60000, 10000, false);
// When Bitcoin trades up to $60,000 with a 20% trailing amount, buy 1 Bitcoin
TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 60000, 0.2, true);
# When Bitcoin trades up to $60,000 with a $10000 trailing amount, buy 1 Bitcoin
self.TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 60000, 10000, False)
# When Bitcoin trades up to $60,000 with a 20% trailing amount, buy 1 Bitcoin
self.TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 60000, 0.2, True)

To sell an asset with a trailing stop order, set the stop price below the current bid price and the trailing amount.

// When Bitcoin trades down to $43,000 with a $10000 trailing amount, sell 1 Bitcoin
TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 43000, 10000, false);
// When Bitcoin trades down to $43,000 with a 20% trailing amount, sell 1 Bitcoin
TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 43000, 0.2, true);
# When Bitcoin trades down to $43,000 with a $10000 trailing amount, sell 1 Bitcoin
self.TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 43000, 10000, False)
# When Bitcoin trades down to $43,000 with a 20% trailing amount, sell 1 Bitcoin
self.TrailingStopOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 43000, 0.2, True)
