2015-12-24T09:31:00Z GOOG 160115C00747500 13.70 -1 Sell Filled -13.70

On December 24, 2015 at 9:31 AM Eastern Time (ET), the algorithm places a combo limit order to buy one GOOG 16011SC00745000 contract and sell one GOOG 160115C00747500 contracts. The limit price is 95% of the sum of both contract prices, which equals $29.59. The combo order doesn't fill immediately because the aggregate price of both contracts is $30.80 > $29.59. By 9:45 AM, the aggregate price of both contracts drops to $29.50, which is below the limit price, so the fill model fills the combo limit order.
