If you sell an Option in a backtest, LEAN can simulate an Option exercise order on behalf of the buyer. By Default, in every hour, LEAN scans your portfolio. It considers exercising American-style Options if they are within 4 days of their expiration and it considers exercising European-style Options on their day of expiration. If you have sold an Option that's 5% in-the-money and the Option exercise order is profitable after the cost of fees, LEAN exercises the Option.

Refer to Reality Modeling - Options Models - Assignment for details.
