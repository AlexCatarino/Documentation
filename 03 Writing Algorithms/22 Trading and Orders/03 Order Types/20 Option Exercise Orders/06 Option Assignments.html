If you sell an Option in a backtest, LEAN can simulate an Option exercise order on behalf of the buyer. By default, LEAN scans your portfolio ever hour. It considers exercising American-style Options if they are within 4 days of their expiration and it considers exercising European-style Options on their day of expiration. If you have sold an Option that's 5% in-the-money and the Option exercise order is profitable after the cost of fees, LEAN exercises the Option. For more information about how we simulate Option assignments, see the Assignment reality model.
