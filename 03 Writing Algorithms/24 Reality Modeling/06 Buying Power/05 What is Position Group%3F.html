Position group is the combination of holdings. A position group has lower margin requirement and maintenance margin than the sum of each position of the group. If you have a margin requirement of $29,150 to purchase a in-the-money option call contract, and a margin requirement of $101,499 to sell an out-of-the money option call contract, the total margin requirement would be $130,649. However, these positions compose a bull call spread with a margin requirement of $0.
