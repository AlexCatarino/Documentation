The default Option assignment model is the DefaultOptionAssignmentModel. The DefaultOptionAssignmentModel emulates exercising of short option positions in the portfolio by every hour: exercising American-style Options if they are within 4 days of their expiration, and exercising European-style Options on their day of expiration, if you have sold an Option that's 5% in-the-money and the Option exercise order is profitable after the cost of fees. To view the implementation of this model, see the LEAN GitHub repository.

If you wish to disable automatic assignment, use NullOptionAssignmentModel to have no assignment at all.
