The Alpha Streams marketplace efficiency is in part determined by the infrastructure in place to find the efficient price. Although we have plans for a more advanced marketplace, currently it is principally a manual process. An Alpha price can be changed manually by the Authors once per month. If there are funds actively licensing the strategy, they will have the choice to continue or stop their license.

Licenses are per month but billing is pro-rated per day; with a minimum of 1 days licensing revenue. This barrier is intended to be low enough funds can easily harness your strategy.
