The Alpha Streams marketplace efficiency is in part determined by the infrastructure in place to find the efficient price. Although we have plans for a more advanced marketplace, currently it is principally a manual process. An Alpha price can be changed manually by the Authors at anytime but the pricing update is delayed by one month if there are funds subscribed. If there are funds actively licensing the strategy, they will have the choice to continue or stop their license.

License pricing is locked in for a minimum of a month. Billing is pro-rated per day; with a minimum of 1 days licensing revenue. This barrier is intended to be low enough funds can easily harness your strategy.
