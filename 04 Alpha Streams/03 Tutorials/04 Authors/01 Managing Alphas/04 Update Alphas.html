Follow these steps to update an Alpha:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Strategies > My Listings.
  3. On the My Listings page, click the Alpha that you want to update.
  4. If you only want to update the family settings of the Alpha, follow these steps:
    1. Click Edit Alpha.
    2. Update any of the following fields:
      • Set suggested live server
      • Set family reserve price
      • Are you trading your own money?
    3. Click Save Changes.

      4. "Alpha updated successfully" displays.

  5. If you want to update more than just the family settings of an Alpha, click Submit Update and then follow the steps in the Submit New Alphas tutorial.
