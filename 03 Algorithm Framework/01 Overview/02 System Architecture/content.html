The Algorithm Framework is built on top of the QCAlgorithm class; giving your strategy access to all the normal methods you use for algorithm development. Algorithms which implement the framework extend the QCAlgorithmFramework class. The framework data output of each module flows into another predictably.

Assets selected by the Universe Selection Module are fed into your Alpha Module to generate trade signals. The trade signals ( Insights ) are converted into Portfolio Targets by the Portfolio Construction Module. The Portfolio Targets hold a target share quantity we'd like the algorithm to hold. To execute these targets efficiently the Execution module fills the targets efficiently over time. Finally; the Risk Management Module ensures our targets are still within safe risk parameters and adjusts the portfolio targets if required.

For simply strategies this may seem like overkill; however even simple strategies can benefit from reusing the ecosystem of modules available in QuantConnect. Imagine pairing your EMA-cross with a better execution system? Or simply plugging in an open sourced trailing stop risk management module?
