The Algorithm Framework is built on top of the QCAlgorithm class; giving your strategy access to all the normal methods you use for algorithm development. The data output of each module flows into another predictably.

Assets selected by the Universe Selection Module are fed into your Alpha Module to generate trade signals. The trade signals ( Insights ) are converted into Portfolio Targets by the Portfolio Construction Module. The Portfolio Targets hold a target share quantity we'd like the algorithm to hold. To execute these targets efficiently the Execution module analyzes market conditions and fills the targets efficiently. Finally; the Risk Management Module monitors the market conditions and ensures our targets are still within safe risk parameters.
