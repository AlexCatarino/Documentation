Term Definition
$[Universe Selection Model,T:QuantConnect.Algorithm.Framework.Selection.UniverseSelectionModel] Framework component for selecting assets for your algorithm.
$[Alpha Creation Model,T:QuantConnect.Algorithm.Framework.Alphas.AlphaModel] Framework component for that generates trade signals; on the assets in your universe for your algorithm.
$[Insight,T:QuantConnect.Algorithm.Framework.Alphas.Insight] A trade signal generated by your algorithm Alpha Model. An Insight indicates the Direction, Magnitude, Confidence and Period of your signal.
$[Portfolio Construction Model,T:QuantConnect.Algorithm.Framework.Portfolio.PortfolioConstructionModel] Framework component that constructs a target portfolio from Insight signals created by the Alpha Model.
$[Portfolio Target,T:QuantConnect.Algorithm.Framework.Portfolio.IPortfolioTarget] A target quantity of holdings (e.g. shares, contracts) the algorithm desires to reach. This target is created by the Portfolio Construction Model.
$[Execution Model.T:QuantConnect.Algorithm.Framework.Execution.ExecutionModel] Framework component responsible for fulfilling the Portfolio Targets set by the Portfolio Construction Model. Execute trades to reach the desired portfolio make up.
$[Risk Model,T:QuantConnect.Algorithm.Framework.Risk.RiskManagementModel] Framework component that manages ongoing market and portfolio risk; ensuring the algorithm remains in target parameters.
