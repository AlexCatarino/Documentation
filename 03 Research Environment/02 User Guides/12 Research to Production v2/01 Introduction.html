What is Research to Production:
- A tutorial series where we demonstrate how to research an idea in the Research environment and port the logic to the backtesting environment.

Why use Research to Production techniques:
-It's easy to move logic from research to backtesting because QuantBook and QCAlgorithm are so simliar.
   - often you just need to assign `qb = self` in backtesting and then paste the research code
-Algorithm development should start in the research environment
