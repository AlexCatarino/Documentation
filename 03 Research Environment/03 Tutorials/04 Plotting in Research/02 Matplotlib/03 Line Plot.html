We will visualize the close price of SPY and QQQ using a time-series line plot.

Gathering Data

To begin, we retrieve close price data for SPY and QQQ

  1. Instantiate a QuantBook.
    2. qb = QuantBook()
  2. Call the AddEquity method with SPY and QQQ, and their corresponding resolution.
    3. spy = qb.AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Daily)
qqq = qb.AddEquity("QQQ", Resolution.Daily)

    If you do not pass a resolution argument, Resolution.Minute is used by default.

  3. Call the History method with qb.Securities.Keys, time argument(s), and resolution to request historical data for all of the Securities.
    4. history = qb.History(qb.Securities.Keys, 360, Resolution.Daily)
  4. Select close price data to plot.
    5. close_price = history['close'].unstack(level=0)

Plot the Data

Here, we visualize the close price of SPY and QQQ with a time-series line plot. Note that pandas.DataFrame has built-in support for matplotlib library, making it easier and quicker for us to create plots.

  1. Call the plot method on the pandas object.
    2. close_price.plot(title="Close Price of SPY and QQQ", figsize=(15, 10))
  2. Show the plot.
    3. plt.show()

    Line charts display the value of the property you selected in a time series.
