Get some historical market data to produce the plots. For example, to get data for some banking companies and SPY over the last year, run:

qb = QuantBook()
tickers = ["COF",   # Capital One Financial Corporation
           "GS",    # Goldman Sachs Group, Inc.
           "JPM",   # J P Morgan Chase & Co
           "WFC",   # Wells Fargo & Company
           "SPY"]
symbols = [qb.AddEquity(ticker, Resolution.Daily).Symbol for ticker in tickers]
history = qb.History(symbols, 252, Resolution.Daily)
