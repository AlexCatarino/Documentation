Follow these steps to subscribe to list of Equity securities of Bank companies.

  1. Instantiate a QuantBook.
    2. qb = QuantBook()
  2. Call the AddEquity method with their corresponding resolution while iterrating the ticker list.
    3. symbols = [qb.AddEquity(ticker, Resolution.Daily).Symbol
	for ticker in [
        "COF",   # Capital One Financial Corporation
        "GS",    # Goldman Sachs Group, Inc.
		"JPM",   # J P Morgan Chase & Co
        "WFC" ]] # Wells Fargo & Company

    If you do not pass a resolution argument, Resolution.Minute is used by default.

  3. Call the History method with list of Symbol objects, lookback period, and resolution to request historical data for all of the Securities.
    4. history = qb.History(symbols, 360, Resolution.Daily)
