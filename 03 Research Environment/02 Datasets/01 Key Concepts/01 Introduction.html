You can access most of the data from the Dataset Market in the Research Environment. The data includes Equity, Crypto, Forex, and derivative data going back as far as 1998. Similar to backtesting, to access the data, create a security subscription and then make a history call.

qb = QuantBook()
symbol = qb.AddEquity("SPY").Symbol
history = qb.History(symbol, 10, Resolution.Daily)
