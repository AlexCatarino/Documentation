After you import the libraries, get historical market data to train and test the model. For example, to get data for the SPY ETF during 2020 and 2021, run:

qb = QuantBook()
symbol = qb.AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Daily).Symbol
history = qb.History(symbol, datetime(2020, 1, 1), datetime(2022, 1, 1)).loc[symbol]
