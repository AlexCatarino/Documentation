Paper trading is possible using your brokerage (IB, OANDA, FXCM) or the QuantConnect "Paper Brokerage Model". The QuantConnect Paper Model allows you to simulate immediate brokerage fills without first signing up to a brokerage account. Interactive Brokers supports paper trading by entering paper trading credentials, while other brokerages require selecting different API endpoints on deploying your algorithm.

Whether our paper model or a real brokerage, you can source data for your algorithm from QuantConnect or your brokerage directly. Currently, only Interactive Brokers Brokerage data feed is supported. The other brokerage data sources are practically identical to QuantConnect so we have not built-in ways to use them directly.

QuantConnect Data Feed

Using the QuantConnect data feed you can access the asset classes listed below. Paper trading on Futures or Options accounts is not supported at this time.

  • US Equity Price / Exchange Tick Stream
  • FX / OANDA Brokerage Feed
  • FX / FXCM Brokerage Feed
  • Crypto / GDAX Brokerage Feed
  • Universe Selection

Interactive Brokers Data Feed

Using Interactive Brokers as your data source you can access the asset classes below. You must have the appropriate subscriptions with Interactive Brokers for the assets you need.

  • Equities
  • Futures
  • Options
  • FX / IB

Universe selection is not supported by the Interactive Brokers Brokerage data feed at this time.
