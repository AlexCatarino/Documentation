Your system must meet the following minimum requirements to run the local platform:

  • CPU: Quad core with 3.3 GHz
  • RAM: 4 GB
  • OS: Windows 10+ or macOS 11+

You need internet access to download updates, collaborate with team members, and sync your projects with QuantConnect Cloud. However, organizations on the Trading Firm and Institution tiers can run local backtests and research notebooks without an internet connection for up to 24 hours.
