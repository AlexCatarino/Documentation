Local Platform provides multiple deployment targets to enable you to open notebooks on-premise and in QuantConnect Cloud.

Local

If you use the Local Platform deployment target when you open a notebook, the notebook runs on your local hardware and uses the data in your organization workspace.

Cloud

If you use the Cloud Platform deployment target when you open a notebook, the notebook runs on your local hardware and uses the our cloud datasets. The cloud Research Environment may not be used to export cloud datasets.
