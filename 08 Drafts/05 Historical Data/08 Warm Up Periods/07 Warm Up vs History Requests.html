Warm-up and history requests both provide historical data. The approach you take depends on your specific use case. A benefit to using warm-up is that it only takes one line of code to pump historical data into your algorithm, which processes universe selection and updates all the indicators. A consequence to using warm-up is that it may provide more historica data than you actually need. Furthermore, if you have derivative assets in your algorithm, warm-up can be very slow to process.
