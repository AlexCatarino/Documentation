Backtests have access to data up to \(t-1\) to \(t-4\) days, where \(t\) is the current day. The availablibity depends on the data type.

In live trading, history requests exclude data from the trailing 5-10 seconds because it takes time for LEAN to process the historical data. To access the data in this gap, record the live data stream into a RollingWindow.
