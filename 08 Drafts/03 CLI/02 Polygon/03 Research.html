To access Polygon data from the local Research Environment, open a terminal in your organization workspace and then run lean research <projectName> --data-provider Polygon --polygon-api-key <apiKey>.

$ lean research "My Project" --data-provider Polygon --polygon-api-key apiKey

The lean research command also accepts additional options for Polygon. If you provide any of the preceding options, your Lean configuration file saves them so that you only need to run lean research <projectName> to open the Research Environment with the same options.
