To access Polygon data from the Research Environment, open a terminal in your organization workspace and then run lean research <projectName> --data-provider Polygon --polygon-api-key <apiKey>.

$ lean research "My Project" --data-provider Polygon --polygon-api-key dTB0asdfasdfasdf
Starting JupyterLab, access in your browser at localhost:8888

To get an API key, see the API Keys page on the Polygon website.
