Our TT integration supports trading Futures.

AddFuture(Futures.Indices.SP500EMini, Resolution.Minute, Market.CME);
self.AddFuture(Futures.Indices.SP500EMini, Resolution.Minute, Market.CME)

If you call the SetBrokerageModel method with the correct BrokerageName, then you don't need to pass a Market argument to the AddFuture method because the brokerage model has a default market.
