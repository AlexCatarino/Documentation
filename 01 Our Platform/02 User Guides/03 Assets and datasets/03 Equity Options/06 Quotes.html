QuoteBars
-Definition: built by consolidating the bid and ask ticks from the exchanges into bars
- Add image from Handling Data docs
-Properties:
    - Open, High, Low, Close, Bid, Ask, LastBidSize, and LastAskSize
    - Bid and the Ask properties are Bar objects
    - The QuoteBar Open, High, Low, and Close properties values are the mean of the respective Bid and Ask properties.
-before accessing, should use either:
    - data.QuoteBars.ContainsKey and data.QuoteBars[symbol] is not None
    - slice.ContainsKey and data[symbol] is not None
