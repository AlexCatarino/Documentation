The backtest results page displays a set of built-in charts to help you analyze the performance of your algorithm. The page displays the following charts for all algorithms:

  • Strategy Equity: Time series of equity and daily performance
  • Capacity: Time series of strategy capacity snapshots
  • Drawdown: Time series of equity peak-to-trough value
  • Benchmark: Time series of the benchmark closing price (SPY, by default)
  • Exposure: Time series of long and short exposure ratios
  • Assets Sales Volume: Chart showing the proportion of total volume for each traded security

In addition to the charts listed above, the backtest results page displays the following charts for framework algorithms:

  • Insight Count: Time series of the number of insights emitted
  • Alpha: Time series of the direction and magnitude score of the Alpha model
  • Alpha Assets: Chart showing the number of insights emitted for each security
