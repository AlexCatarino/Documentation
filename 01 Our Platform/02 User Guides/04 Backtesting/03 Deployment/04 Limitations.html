- To run backtests, you need to have an available backtesting node in your organization
- The amount of backtesting nodes you can have in your organization depends on the organization's tier
----- Free => 1
----- Quant Researcher => 2
----- Team => 10
----- Trading Firm & Institution => Unlimited
<h3>Order limits
-The number of orders you can place in a single backtest depends on the org tier
   - Free => 10K
   - Quant Researcher => 10M
   - Other tiers => Unlimited

<h3>Log limits
-The amount of logs you can print per day depends on the org tier
    - Free => 10KB
    - Quant Researcher => 1MB
    - Team => 10MB
    - Trading Firm => 50MB
    - Institution => Unlimited
