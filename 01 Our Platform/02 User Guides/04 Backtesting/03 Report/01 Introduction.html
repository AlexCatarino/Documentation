You can generate a performance report after your backtest finishes executing. The report contains various statistics and charts about the backtest. You can download the report as a PDF and share the results with others. This page describes the content of each section in the report.

Key Statistics

The following key statistics are included at the top of the backtest report:

  • Days Live
  • Turnover
  • CAGR
  • Markets
  • Trades per day
  • Drawdown
  • PSR
  • Sharpe Ratio
  • Information Ratio
  • Strategy Capacity (denominated in USD)
