Your QuantConnect profile page is your place to customize your community appearance. Your profile page is accessible to everyone from the forum. You can customize your profile page to display your personal information, your preferred programming language, your forum activity, and the badges that you have earned.

What is your community profile:
-A profile/account you can tailor to yourself

Why do need a profile:
-To interact with the community under a username / account
-To join QC organizations and use platform resources
-To give members a sense of community / personalization
-To display your background, credentials, achievements, contact information
