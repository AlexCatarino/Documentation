Your profile page displays your latest forum posts and your community engagement statistics. The page displays the last five comments that you've made in the forum so that other members can read your posts and so that you can find discussions on which you recently posted. The page displays your community engagement statistics like the number of experience points that you've earned, the number of comments that you've posted, the number of discussions that you've created, and the number of algorithms that you've shared in the forum. Your community engagement statistics are displayed so that other members can see how active you are in the forum.

In addition to your forum activity, your profile page also platform engagement statistics like the number of backtests that you've run and the proportion of the Bootcamp lessons that you've completed. Your platform engagement statistics are displayed so that other members can see how experienced you are with QuantConnect and Lean.
