What way is the forum organized:
-There are forum pages for
    - Interesting: Marked as interesting discussions by the QC team
    - Newest: Newest discussion threads posted to the forum
    - LEAN: Discussions related directly to LEAN
    - Data Issues: A list of all the open data issues
    - Bookmarked: Discussion threads you've commented on(?)

Why is the forum organized this way:
-To promote easy navigation
-Provides a way to filter discussions to the ones you want to see
