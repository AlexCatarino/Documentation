Discussions are a set of forum posts and comments about a targeted subject. We occasionally post to forum discussions to announce new features, tutorials, and examples. You can contribute to any discussion if you have completed 30% of the Bootcamp lessons. You can create a discussion to connect with other members or to ask for guidance on a specific problem that you are facing. However, always perform your own research and tests before asking other members for assistance.

The forum supports the following functionality:

  • Sharing code snippets, photos, videos, and files
  • Attaching backtest results and research notebooks
  • Publishing text content that includes bold, italics, lists, links, and emojis
  • Tagging other members
  • Rewarding discussion content with QuantConnect Credit and thumbs up
  • Bookmarking discussions
  • Generating links to comments to share
