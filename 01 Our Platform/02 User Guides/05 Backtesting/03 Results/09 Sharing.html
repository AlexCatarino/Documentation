The backtest results page includes a Share tab that enables you to share your backtest results. Under the Share tab, if you click Make backtest public to share, your backtest results become public so you can share them with others. The following methods of sharing backtest results are available. After you've made your backtest results public, they can't be made private again.

Backtest Reports

The Share tab displays a link to your backtest report. Share the link with others to give them access to your backtest report.

Embedded Backtest Results

The Share tab displays a link to an embedded backtest result.

The following table describes the tabs of the embedded backtest result:

Tab Content
Charts Some of the built-in and custom charts that plot the backtest performance
Statistics The overall statistics of the backtest
Code The project files that produced the backtest

To clone the project, click Clone Algorithm.

Share the embedded backtest result with others through the following methods:

Method Instructions
Share the link to the embedded backtest result Get the link from the backtest results page
Attach the embedded backtest result to a forum discussion See Create New Discussions
Display the embedded backtest result on a website Get the script from the backtest results page
