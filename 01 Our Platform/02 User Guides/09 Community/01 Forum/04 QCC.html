What are QCC rewards:
-QuantConnect Credits (QCC) you can give to (or receive from) other community members through the forum
-Rewards are given to insightful answers or answers to community issues

Why would I want to give/receive QCC:
-Give to show your appreciation for others in the community (ex: if someone helped you solve a problem with your algorithm)
-Receiving QCC -- to use in running optimizations, getting a discount on your monthly bill, downloading datasets
