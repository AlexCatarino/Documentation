What activity is shown on your profile account:
-Your profile page shows the latest forum discussions you've participated in
   - This isn't a comprehensive list though. To see everything, you can use the forum search bar
-Experience points earned
-Backtest count
-Number of forum comments
-Number of forum discussions
-Number of public algorithms

Why is activity shown on your account:
-Forum activity
    -So others can follow your activity if they miss something you've posted in the forum
    -So you can use it as history to find threads you've recently commented on or created
-Platform activity
   -To show how active you are on the QC platform and in the forum
   -To show your experience/expertise with using the QC platform (perhaps to show potential employers? tutoring students?)
