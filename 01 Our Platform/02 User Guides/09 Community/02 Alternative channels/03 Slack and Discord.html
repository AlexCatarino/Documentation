What are our channel links:

Why do we have two channels:
-We are migrating our Slack community channel to Discord to support more users and flexibility. Both will be open until October 31st, but please migrate to Discord before November 2021.

What are the pro of using these channels instead of the forum:
-Direct messages
-Push notifications
-Chatroom
    - Quick feedback with other users that are also online
    - Don't have to refresh the page to see new comments and discussions
-You earn a QC badge for joining Slack
-Channels are targeted instead of one open forum

What are the cons of using these channels instead of the forum:
-Chatroom
    - Not indexed by search engines so answers get lost in history 
-No QCC rewards
-Your activity isn't recorded in your QC profile page
-Hard to give support
     - Code snippets aren't as easy
     - Not as easy to clone projects
     - No administrative tools for the QC support team to organize support tickets to answer
