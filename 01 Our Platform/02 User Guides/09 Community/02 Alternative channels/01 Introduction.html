What are alternative channels:
-Communication channels outside of the forum that members can use to connect with other QC members and QC staff

Why are there alternative channels:
-For members to engage with the community if they don't want to use the community forum
-For members to stay up to date on QC offerings when using other platforms they're on
-For QC to engage with the community on social media platforms
    - Each platform has it own unique purpose/content
