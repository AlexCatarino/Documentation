Check the Fees Schedule page on the Bitfinex website to see the live trading order fees. In backtests, the fees are modeled with the BitfinexFeeModel, which charges a 0.1% maker fee and a 0.2% taker fee. If you place a limit order that hits a hidden order or you add liquidity to the order book by placing a limit order that doesn't cross the spread, you pay maker fees. If you place a hidden order or you remove liquidity from the order book by placing an order that crosses the spread, you pay taker fees. The BitfinexFeeModel does not currently model your 30-day trading volume and LEO balance to adjust the fees in backtests. In live trading, Bitfinex adjusts the fees.

To use the Bitfinex brokerage in a live algorithm, the following table shows the fee settings you need on your Account > Fees page on the Bitfinex website:

Fee Setting Value
Default currency for fees USD
