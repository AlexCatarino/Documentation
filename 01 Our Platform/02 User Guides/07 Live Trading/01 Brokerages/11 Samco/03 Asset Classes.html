Our Samco integration supports trading Indian Equities, Futures, and Options.

AddEquity("YESBANK", Resolution.Minute, Market.India);
AddFuture("NIFTY", Resolution.Minute, Market.India);
AddOption("YESBANK", Resolution.Minute, Market.India);
self.AddEquity("YESBANK", Resolution.Minute, Market.India)
self.AddFuture("NIFTY", Resolution.Minute, Market.India)
self.AddOption("YESBANK", Resolution.Minute, Market.India)

If you call the SetBrokerageModel method with the correct BrokerageName, then you don't need to pass a Market argument to the AddEquity, AddFuture and AddOption method because the brokerage model automatically selects the correct market.
