The InteractiveBrokersBrokerageModel models buying power and margin calls to ensure your algorithm stays within the margin requirements.

Buying Power

The InteractiveBrokersBrokerageModel uses the CashBuyingPowerModel for cash accounts. For margin accounts, the buying power model that's used and the amount of leverage that's available depends on the type of security that you're trading. The following table shows the buying power model that is used and the amount of leverage that's available for each security type when you're using a margin account:

Margin Calls

Regulation T margin rules are enforced with the DefaultMarginCallModel. When the amount of margin remaining in your portfolio drops below 5% of the total portfolio value, a warning is sent. When the amount of margin remaining in your portfolio drops to zero or goes negative, the portfolio sorts the generated margin call orders by their unrealized profit and executes each order synchronously until your portfolio is within the margin requirements.
