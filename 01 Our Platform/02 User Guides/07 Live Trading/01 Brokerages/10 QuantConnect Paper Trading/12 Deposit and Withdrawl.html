To simulate deposit, call CashBook.Add to add cash into Portfolio. The below example shows a deposit of 100 in the account currency.

Porfolio.CashBook.Add(AccountCurrency, 100);
self.Porfolio.CashBook.Add(self.AccountCurrency, 100)

To simulate withdrawl, do the same step in deposit, but in negative value.

Porfolio.CashBook.Add(AccountCurrency, -100);
self.Porfolio.CashBook.Add(self.AccountCurrency, -100)

To perform a regular deposit/withdrawl, schedule a Schedule event. The below example shows a monthly deposit of 100 in the account currency at month starts.

Schedule.On(DateRules.MonthStart(), TimeRules.At(0, 0), () =>
{
	Porfolio.CashBook.Add(AccountCurrency, 100);
});
self.Schedule.On(self.DateRules.MonthStart(), \
                 self.TimeRules.At(0, 0), \
                 lambda: self.Porfolio.CashBook.Add(self.AccountCurrency, 100))
