The live performance page has a Holdings tab that displays your positions and cash.

The following table describes the properties that display for each of your positions:

Property Description
Symbol The ticker of the security.
Average Price The average price that you paid for the position.
Quantity The size of your position.
Market Value The value of your position if sold it with market orders.
Unrealized The unrealized profit of your position, including fees and spread costs.

The values in the positions section update as new data points are injected into your algorithm. The cash section displays the quantity of each currency in your algorithm's CashBook. View the Holdings tab to see your holdings, add security subscriptions, and place manual orders. If you enable the Show All Portfolio check box, the Holdings tab displays all of your current holdings and active data subscriptions.
