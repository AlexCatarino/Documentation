What are suspicious ticks
-Ticks are raw/unfilitered but some ticks are flagged as suspicious by QC

What are some ticks suspicious:
-Some ticks occur outside of the bid/ask
-Some ticks are way outside the current price- Some ticks occur on dark pools, which aren't tradable

Why is it important to flag ticks as suspicious:
-Suspicious ticks aren't tradable, so they shouldn't be used in your backtests and they shouldn't be considered informative in live trading
-It's just noise
-You can ignore the suspicious ticks flag. However, we only recommend advanced users incorporate their own tick filtering process
