What is the IB data feed:
-A stream of security prices directly from Interactive Brokers, the data is taken from the trades made on the IB platform

What subscriptions are available:
-To use the IB data feed, you need the appropriate data subscriptions on your IB account:
   https://www.interactivebrokers.ca/en/accounts/fees/marketData.php?ib_entity=ca

Why use the IB data feed:
-It represents the prices/sizes actually available at IB, whereas our QC data feed consolidates the prices / volumes across all the exchanges
