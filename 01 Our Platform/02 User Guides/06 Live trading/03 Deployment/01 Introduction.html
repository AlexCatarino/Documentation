What is deployment:
-Executing your trading strategy on our co-located servers using real market data and placing paper/real orders

Why deploy to live quant trading:
-To see the performance of your trading algorithms that you've developed
-To test your trading strategy with paper trading before deploying to real money
-Live quant trading has less stress, less chance of addiction, less chance of rule breaking (because of algorithmic execution) than live discretionary trading
-It's much cheaper to deploy to live trading with QC than purchasing server space, setting up data feeds, and maintaining the software yourself

What is the Deployment GUI:
-The deployment wizard is a GUI that you can use to deploy live algorithms
    - Select a brokerage
    - Select a live node
    - Setup notifications

Why use the Deployment GUI:
-Very easy -- You can deploy live trading algorithms by providing your brokerage credentials and following the point-and-click menus
