What are live trading notifications:
-Tools to integrate your algorithm into email, sms, webhooks, and telegram so you can stay informed

Why use notifications:
-To notify yourself of orders and insights (through the deployment GUI)
    - The deployment GUI let's you set up multiple notifications of each type (Email, SMS, webhooks)
-To notify yourself whenever something occurs in your live trading algorithm (using the NotificationManager)
-To stay informed in real-time of your algorithms execution/state
