What is Trading Technologies:
-Trading Technologies was founded by Gary Kemp in 1994 with the goal to create professional trading software, infrastructure, and data solutions for a wide variety of users. Trading Technologies provides access to trading Futures, Options, and Cryptocurrencies. Trading Technologies also provides a charting platform, infrastructure services, and risk management tools.
