What is Binance:
-Binance was founded by Changpeng Zhao in 2017 with the goal to "increase the freedom of money globally" [source]. Binance provides access to trading Cryptocurrencies through spot markets and perpetual Futures for clients with no minimum deposit when depositing Cryptocurrencies. Binance also provides an NFT marketplace, a mining pool, and services to deposit Crypto coins in liquidity pools to earn rewards.

What asset classes are supported:
-Crypto

What order types are supported:
-market, limit, stop market, stop limit, market on open, market on close, limit if touched

What account types are supported:
-Cash

What leverage is available:
-Cash account: 1x

What are the fees:
-BinanceFeeModel
    - https://www.binance.com/en/fee/schedule
    - Maker fee: 0.1%
    - Taker fee: 0.1%

What buying power model is used:
-CashBuyingPowerModel

What settlement model is used:
-ImmediateSettlementModel
