What is Tradier:
-

What asset classes are supported:
-Equity, Options

What order types are supported:
-market, limit, stop market, stop limit, limit if touched

What account types are supported:
-Cash, margin

What leverage is available:
-Cash accounts: 1x
-Margin accounts:
    - Equity: 2x
    - Options: 1x

What are the limitations:
-Doesn't support extended market hours. Your order will be processed at market open
-Doesn't allow updating order quantities
-Tradier Brokerage cancels open orders on reverse split symbols

What are the fees:
-$0

What buying power model is used:
-Equity: SecurityMarginModel
-Option: OptionMarginModel

What margin call model is used:
-DefaultMarginCallModel

What settlement model is used:
-Cash accounts: DelayedSettlementModel
-Margin account: ImmediateSettlementModel
