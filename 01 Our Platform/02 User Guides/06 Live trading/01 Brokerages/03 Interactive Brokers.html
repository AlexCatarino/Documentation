What is IB:
-"Interactive Brokers (IB) is a low-cost provider of trade execution and clearing services for individuals, advisors, prop trading groups, brokers, and hedge funds. IB's premier technology provides direct access to stocks, options, futures, forex, bonds, and funds on over 100 markets worldwide from a single IB Universal account. Member NYSE, FINRA, SIPC." // from docs v1

What order types are supported:
-market, limit, stop market, stop limit, market on open, market on close, limit if touched

What asset classes are supported:
-Equity, Options, Futures, Future Options

What are the fees (InteractiveBrokersFeeModel):
-Equity: $0.005/share, $1 minimum fee
-Options:  https://github.com/QuantConnect/Lean/blob/1c3d849ad5b35ca32b0f030b518e8ed8bf86f733/Common/Orders/Fees/InteractiveBrokersFeeModel.cs#L202-L235
   - Option exercise: $0
-Futures: $1.85/contract
-Future Options: $1.85/contract
