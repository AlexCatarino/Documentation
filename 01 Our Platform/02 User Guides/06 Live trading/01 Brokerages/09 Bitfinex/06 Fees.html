What are the fees:
-We don't currently model 30-day volume. We model fixed maker/taker fees
-Maker fees: 0.1%
    - Maker fees are paid when you add liquidity to our order book by placing a limit order under the ticker price for buy and above the ticker price for sell.
-Taker fees: 0.2%
    - Taker fees are paid when you remove liquidity from our order book by placing any order that is executed against an order of the order book.
    - If you place a hidden order, you will always pay the taker fee. If you place a limit order that hits a hidden order, you will always pay the maker fee.Taker fees are paid when you remove liquidity from our order book by placing any order that is executed against an order of the order book.

Members require the following fee settings (on the Account > Fees page) to use Bitfinex live:
 - Default currency for fees: USD
 - Fee type for Exchange orders: Currency Exchange Fee
