What is Bitfinex:
-"As a cryptocurrency exchange founded in 2012, Bitfinex has always been at the forefront of technological innovation in digital asset trading. " // from docs v1
-Bitfinex was founded by Giancarlo Devasini and Raphael Nicolle in 2012 with the goal to "give our users the ultimate cryptocurrency trading experience" [source]. Bitfinex provides access to trading Cryptocurrencies for clients outside prohibited jurisdictions with no minimum deposit to get an account set up. When funding your account with fiat currencies there is a 10,000 minimum for USD, EUR, and GBP [source]. However, when funding your account with Cryptocurrencies, there is no minimum. Bitfinex also provides Cryptocurrency staking, a mobile app, and an unrealized profit leaderboard for traders on the platform.
