What is Atreyu:
-Atreyu was founded by George Kledaras and John Pyrovolakis in 2015 with the goal to connect quantitative managers to US markets. Atreyu provides access to trading Equities, Options, and Futures. Atreyu also provides onboarding services, risk-checks, order routing, and transaction cost analysis.
-Contains a shortable provider

What asset classes are supported:
-Equity

What order types are supported:
-Limit, market, market on close

What time in force is supported:
-DayTimeInForce

What account types are supported:
-Cash, Margin

What leverage is available:
-Cash accounts: 1x
-Margin accounts: 2x

Limitations:
-Fractional shares are not supported

What are the fees:
-$0.0015 per share

What buying power model is used:
-SecurityMarginModel

What margin call model is used:
-DefaultMarginCallModel

What settlement model is used:
-Cash accounts: DelayedSettlementModel
-Margin account: ImmediateSettlementModel
