What is Bloomberg:
-Only supported in live mode. There is no backtesting brokerage model
-You can update orders
-You can cancel orders
-Redirect to : https://www.lean.io/bloomberg/

What order types are supported:
-Market, limit, stop market, stop limit, market on close

What time in force is supported:
-DayTimeInForce
-GoodUntilCancelTimeInForce
-GoodUntilDateTimeInForce

What asset classes are supported:
-Equity, Futures, Options

What are the fees:
-From Bloomberg when the order is filled
-Redirect to their documentation for fees(?)
