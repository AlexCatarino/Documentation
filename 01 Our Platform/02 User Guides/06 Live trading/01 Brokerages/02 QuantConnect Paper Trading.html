What is the QC Paper trading brokerage:
-The QuantConnect Paper Model allows you to simulate immediate brokerage fills like backtesting without first signing up to a brokerage account
-"See how your algorithm would have performed with our paper trading feature. We use real live-data feeds, but a virtual brokerage to execute your trades. Each project is allocated $100,000 virtual currency to track how you've performed. " // from docs v1

What order types are supported:
-market, limit, stop market, stop limit, market on open, market on close, limit if touched

What asset classes are supported:
-Equity, forex, crypto, futures

What are the fees:
-If you don't specify a model for QC paper trading, it will use the default brokerage model. The fees in the default brokerage model are:
   - Equity (InteractiveBrokersFeeModel): $0.005/share, $1 minimum fee
   - Futures (InteractiveBrokersFeeModel): $1.85/contract
   - Forex & crypto (ConstantFeeModel): $0

What are the limitations (apply to all paper trading brokerages):
-We use the backtest modeling to perform live paper trading
- Orders don't have market impact
- Order fills aren't always realistic
- Not as realistic as live trading (your emotions may cause you to intervene with live trading when you wouldn't if it was paper money)
