What are liquidations:
-Selling everything in your portfolio at the current market price using market orders
-There is an option to liquidate your portfolio holdings in the live trading GUI
    -A "kill switch"
-Note: Any holdings in the Equity market will not be liquidated.

Why liquidate holdings:
-If you want to stop trading
-If your algorithm has a bug in it and purchased a lot of securities you didn't want (manually placing trades would be tedious)
-If you want to immediately eliminate all market risk
-If for any reason you no longer want to hold your portfolio of securities
