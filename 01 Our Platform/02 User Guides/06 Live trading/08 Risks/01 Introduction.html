What are live trading risks:
-Risks associated with deploying live trading algorithms

Why are live trading risks a problem:
-They can influence the functioning of your portfolio of strategies
-Potential loss of capital
-Takes longer to intervene on errors with quant trading since you're not always in front of the screen (like with discretionary trading)
-Some risks listed here can be out of your control (for instance, market & counterparty risk) even though there are ways to mitigate/address them
-They can cause great stress & affect your mental health

-QC is not liable for live trading risks
