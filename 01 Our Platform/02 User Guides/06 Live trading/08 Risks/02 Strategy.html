What is strategy risk:
-The risks that result from designing a strategy based on a statistical model.

Why does strategy risk occur:
-over-fitting, survivorship bias, look-ahead bias
-In regards to statistical analysis:
    - Ignoring the model's assumptions (ex: linear regression models assume homoscedasticity)
    - In market regime changes, the assumptions can change the underlying data to violate the assumptions

How to address counterparty risk:
-Use rolling parameters when training your statistical models
-Ensure you have performed the required statistical tests before training such models
