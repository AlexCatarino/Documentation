-The number of live trading algorithms you can deploy depends on your organization's tier
    - Free: 0; Quant Researcher: 2; Team: 10; All other tiers: unlimited
    - You need to have a live trading node for each algorithm you want to deploy
-Each live algorithm requires its own brokerage connection/account
   - When deploying to IB, if you login to your account after you deployed the algorithm, the algorithm will stop running
       - You can only have one access point to your IB account
