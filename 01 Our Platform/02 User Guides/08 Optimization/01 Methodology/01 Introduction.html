What is optimization:
- The process of optimizing algorithm parameters to maximize/minimize a target metric

Why perform optimization:
- To maximize/minimize a target metric
    - For instance, optimizing your indicator parameters or lookback window to maximize the Sharpe ratio over the entire backtest
- Optimization can help you adjust your strategy to achieve your investment objectives

What do I need to watch out for when optimizing:
-Beware of overfitting. Using the optimizer to select optimal parameter values can model the past too closely and may not be robust enough to perform well in the future.
