What are 'all listings':
-Includes a comprehensive list of all the datasets we support

What search functionality is available:
-There are filters for dataset categories
-You can search for only datasets that have "Cloud only" or "cloud and download" delivery options
-You can search for datasets by name/description

What metrics are presented for each dataset:
-Each dataset card includes name, description, coverage, start date, price
-Coverage is the number of assets/securities/contracts/currency pairs/articles the dataset covers
