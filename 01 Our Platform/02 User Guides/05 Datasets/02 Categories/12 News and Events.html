News and events data is data that's collected from news providers regarding current events. This data category includes the Tiingo News Feed, Benzinga News Feed, and Brain Sentiment Indicator datasets. The Tiingo News Feed tracks news releases for over 120 different news providers. The Benzinga News Feed contains structured content that's produced by Benzinga's editorial team for over 15,000 US Equities. The Brain Sentiment Indicator provides a sentiment value to US Equities by performing Natural Language Processing on financial news releases.
