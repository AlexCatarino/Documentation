Financial market data is data that's collected from the trading activity on exchanges. This data category includes security prices, the CBOE Volatility Index, and the US Treasury Yield Curve. Security price data enables you to analyze historical trades in the financial markets. The CBOE Volatility Index data enables you to study the relationship that volatility expectations have on security prices. The US Treasury Yield Curve data lets you monitor the yields of bonds with numerous maturities and how they impact other areas of the financial markets.
