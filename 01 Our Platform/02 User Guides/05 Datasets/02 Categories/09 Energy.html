Energy data is data that's collected on energy production, distribution, and consumption. This data category includes the US Energy Information Administration (EIA) dataset and the US Futures Security Master dataset. The US EIA dataset tracks national and international oil production and consumption. The US Futures Security Master dataset provides mapping reference data for the most liquid contracts of the CME Group exchanges, calculated with the popular rolling techniques. Many of the Futures contracts included in the US Futures Security Master are energy Futures.
