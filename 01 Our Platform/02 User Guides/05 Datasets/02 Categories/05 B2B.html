Business-to-business (B2B) data is data that's collected on businesses that sell goods and services to other businesses. This data category includes firmographics, technographics, and B2B sales. Firmographic data enables you to segment firms into various groups based on their industry, location, size, or other metrics. Technographic data enables you to study the relationship between the technology stack organizations use and their market performance. B2B sales data lets you analyze how businesses in the market interact with each other and how the interaction affects their price in the financial markets.
