Why report new issues:
-When everyone reports the issues they find, we can ensure the datasets are high quality for everyone
-Reported issues are solved by our data team

What makes a good report:
-Reported data issues should include the security, time, resolution, explanation of the issue, and ideally a backtest/notebook demonstrating the issue
-The easier it is for us to detect and reproduce the issue, the faster it'll be resolved
-Please ensure there isn't already a matching data issue reported before posting a new one
-Example of what a good issue report looks like
    - Included backtest/notebook
     - ticker
     - dates,
      - Use the form
