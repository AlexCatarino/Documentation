The Strategies page displays a table that contains all of the Alphas currently listed in the Alpha Streams Market.

The table of Alphas displays the following information for each Alpha:

  • Alpha Name: The name that the Author gave to the Alpha.
  • Report: A PDF report of the Alpha's backtest and live trading performance.
  • Score: The score is calculated as

    • A higher score represents a high-performing, realistic algorithm.

  • PSR: The Probabilistic Sharpe Ratio of the Alpha's backtest and live performance.
  • Sharpe: The Sharpe Ratio of the Alpha's backtest and live performance.
  • Drawdown: The largest peak to trough decline in the Alpha's equity curve during the backtest and live trading periods.
  • Assets: The asset classes that the Alpha trades.
  • Live: The number of days the Alpha has been in the Alpha Streams Market.
  • Bidders: The number of unique members with a bid on the Alpha order book.
  • Capacity Remaining: The amount of capacity that isn't licensed yet.
  • CAGR: The cumulative annual growth rate of the Alpha during the backtest and live trading periods.
  • Turnover: The percentage of the Alpha's portfolio that has been replaced in a given year.
  • Total Capacity: The estimated maximum amount of money that the Alpha can trade before its performance degrades from market impact.
  • Capacity Allocated: The amount of total capacity that is currently licensed.
  • Version: The latest version number of the Alpha.
  • Live Insights: The number of Insights that the Alpha has emitted in live mode.
  • Capital Requirement: The minimum allocation size that you need to license from the Alpha in order to deploy it with real money. If you license less than the capital requirement, you can only deploy the Alpha with a paper trading account.
  • Trades per Day: The total number of trades during the Alpha's live trading period divided by the number of days the algorithm has been live on QuantConnect. Trades per day is an approximation of the Alpha's trading frequency.
  • Reserve Price: The minimum bid price to license the Alpha.
  • Information Ratio: The Information Ratio of the Alpha's backtest and live trading performance.

Search the table on the Strategies page to find Alphas that fit your investment objectives. You can sort the Alphas based on the columns and apply filters to find Alphas that fit specific metrics. Click an Alpha in the table to open up its listing page. Click the PDF icon in the Report column to download the backtesting and live trading report of the Alpha. Click Download Factors at the top of the page to download the equity curves of all of the Alphas in the market for further analysis.
