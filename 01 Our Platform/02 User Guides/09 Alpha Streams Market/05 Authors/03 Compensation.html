If an investor licenses your Alpha, you receive 100% of the price bid by the investor. The investor also covers the 30% market fee and the cost of the datasets that the Alpha uses to make trading decisions. Refer to the Bids documentation to see how total costs are calculated.

The minimum bid price is $3/month by default, but Authors can adjust the monthly reserve price. If the Alpha has not reached capacity, investors must bid at least the reserve price to be granted an allocation. If the Alpha has reached capacity, investors need to place higher bids to be granted an allocation at the monthly auction.

If an investor licenses your Alpha, you can view your earnings in the Algorithm Lab. You can withdraw the dollars in your QuantConnect Wallet or convert them to QuantConnect Credit. The funds are held for at least 2 weeks to allow investors to request refunds.
