What is the compensation
- The price of the alpha is determined either by the minimum price or market force (if capacity is filled)
- 100% of the price goes to the author, an additional 30% on top of your price is paid to QC by the investor.
- Payments are held for 1-month (? -- ask jared)
     - They may be paid at the end of the month (?)
     - If there is a bug in the alpha, we contact the author first. If they don’t address in time, the investor may want to cancel. We use these held funds to process refunds
- Payments are collected into your QC Wallet as Marketplace Earnings. You can convert them to QCC or withdraw.


What’s my alpha worth?
- Maximum earnings at debut = capacity / allocation_lot_size * market_price (if price doesn’t change from the reserve price)
    - Meaning: You can make more if the alpha has a high capacity or the market_price goes up
       - High capacity => You can have a lot of investors
       - Low capacity => If capacity is filled and price moves up, the price may be too high for new investors
