Alphas can be removed from the Alpha Streams Market after their 3 month grace period if any of the following circumstances occur:

  • Dynamic Time Warping distance > 40%
  • Return correlation < .5
  • Sharpe ratio < -3
  • A logic error occurred and the author doesn't fix it

    • In this case, we will temporarily hide the Alpha from the market until the author fixes the error.

  • A runtime error occurred at the author doesn't fix it

    • In this case, we contact the author to help them fix the runtime error so that they can keep the Alpha listed in the market.

If licensees are invested in an Alpha that passes the metrics above to be delisted, we contact the licensees instead of immediately delisting the Alpha.
