The Alpha Streams Market connects trading strategy researchers and developers with investors who are looking to invest in algorithmic trading strategies. If you are an algorithm developer, the market is a place where you can license your algorithms to investment firms or retail investors. If you are an investor, the market is a place where you can license algorithms that are written by experienced quants and deploy the algorithms to our robust colocated servers.

An Alpha is an algorithm written by a QuantConnect member that investors can subscribe to through the Alpha Streams Market. The member who researches and develops the algorithm is known as the Alpha author. The investor who licenses the algorithm is known as the licensee. The Alpha life cycle is the process that an Alpha undertakes to go from discovery to live trading to delisting.
