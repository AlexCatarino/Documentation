Alpha Streams is a global marketplace where Quants and Funds meet. Quants offer their Alpha (algorithms) for licensing and Funds can quickly locate crowd-sourced strategies that fit their target criteria. QuantConnect acts as an independent third-party to verify the strategy performance and run it out-of-sample in our production environment with live market data.

An Alpha is an algorithm written by a QuantConnect member that investors can subscribe to in order to be notified of live changes in the Alpha's holdings. The member who researches and develops the algorithm is the Alpha author and the investor or fund that licenses the algorithm is the licensee. The Alpha life cycle is the process that an Alpha undertakes to go from discovery to live trading to delisting.
