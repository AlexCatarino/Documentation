What is deployment:
-When you own an allocation, you can deploy the alpha to live trading.
-The algorithm is deployed to our co-located servers, using the latest version of LEAN

What are the deployment restrictions:
-If you have an allocation of $50,000 and you deploy to a  brokerage account that has $60,000, the algorithm will not execute.

What is the refund policy:
-If you want a refund for the license you own, contact support@quantconnect.com. Refunds are processed at the discretion of QC.
