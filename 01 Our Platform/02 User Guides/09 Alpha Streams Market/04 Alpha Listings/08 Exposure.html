The Alpha listing page displays time series for the Alpha's overall leverage and the Alpha's long-short exposure by asset class.

Leverage

The Leverage plot displays the Alpha's utilization of leverage over time. The blue bars represent the backtest period and the orange bars represent the live trading period.

Exposure

The Exposure plot displays the Alpha's long-short exposure by asset class over time. Each asset class and trading period is given its own unique color of bars in the plot.
