The Alpha listing page displays time series for the Alpha's rolling beta and Sharpe ratio.

Rolling Portfolio Beta

The Rolling Portfolio Beta plot displays rolling portfolio beta over trailing 6 and 12 month periods. The light blue line represents the 6 month period of during the backtest, the dark blue line represents the 12 month period of during the backtest, the orange line represents the 6 month period during live trading, and the yellow line represents the 12 month period during live trading.

Rolling Sharpe Ratio

The Rolling Sharpe Ratio plot displays the rolling portfolio Sharpe ratio over a trailing 6 month period. The light blue line represents the backtest period and the orange line represents the live trading period.
