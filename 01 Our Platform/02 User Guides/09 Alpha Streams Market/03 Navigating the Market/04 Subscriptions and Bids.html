The Subscriptions & Bids page displays your Alpha subscriptions and active bids.

The My Subscriptions section displays the following information for each of your Alpha subscriptions:

  • Strategy: The name of the Alpha.
  • Expiry Date: The time when your current subscription ends.
  • Allocation: The maximum amount of capital that you can have in your brokerage account when you deploy the Alpha.
  • Fee: The price you paid for the subscription.
  • Actions: The following action buttons:
    • View Details: Open the Alpha listing page.
    • Deploy: Deploy the latest version of the Alpha live.

The My Bids section displays the following information for each of the bids that you currently have in Alpha order books:

  • Strategy: The name of the Alpha.
  • Bid: The price of your bid.
  • Period: The subscription time period of your bid.
  • Allocation: The maximum amount of capital that you can have in your brokerage account when you deploy the Alpha.
  • Actions: The following action buttons:
    • Increase Bid: Adjust the price, allocation size, or subscription period of your bid.
    • Cancel Bid: Cancel your bid for a subscription to the Alpha.
