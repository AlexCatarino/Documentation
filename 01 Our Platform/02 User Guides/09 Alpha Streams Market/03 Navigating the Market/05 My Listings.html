The My Listings page displays a table that contains all of the Alphas that you have submitted to the Alpha Streams Market.

The table of Alphas contains all of the columns in the table on the Strategies page and the following additional columns:

  • Review Status: The review status of your Alpha. The following statuses are supported:
    • :
    • :
    • Rejected
  • Deployment Status: The review status of your Alpha. The following statuses are supported:
    • :
    • :
    • :

View the My Listings page to see how your Alphas are performing in the market. You can sort the table based on the columns and apply filters to find Alphas that fit specific metrics. Click an Alpha in the table to open up its listing page. Click the icon in the Report column to download the backtesting and live trading report of the Alpha.
