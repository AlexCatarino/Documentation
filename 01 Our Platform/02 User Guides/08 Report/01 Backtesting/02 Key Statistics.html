The following key statistics are included at the top of the backtest report to summarize your algorithm's backtest performance:

  • Days Live: The number of days in the backtest.
  • Turnover: The percentage of the algorithm's portfolio that was replaced in a given year.
  • CAGR: The cumulative annual growth rate of the backtest.
  • Markets: The asset classes that the algorithm trades.
  • Trades per day: The total number of trades during the backtest divided by the number of days in the backtest. Trades per day is an approximation of the algorithm's trading frequency.
  • Drawdown: The largest peak to trough decline in the algorithm's equity curve during the backtest.
  • PSR: The Probabilistic Sharpe Ratio of the backtest.
  • Sharpe Ratio: The Sharpe Ratio of the backtest.
  • Information Ratio: The Information Ratio of the backtest.
  • Strategy Capacity: The maximum amount of money that the algorithm can trade (denominated in USD) before its performance degrades from market impact.
