The ObjectStore allows you to freely store 500MB of data. To unlock more storage space, you need to have at least a Team organization. You can subscribe to one of the ObjectStore add-ons on the Pricing page. The cost of ObjectStore storage space is $10/GB. The following storage options are available:

  • 500MB - Free
  • 1GB - $10/month
  • 2GB - $20/month
  • 5GB - $50/month
  • 10GB - $100/month
