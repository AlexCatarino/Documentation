Each member of your organization will have access to the resources available. However, if you are the manager of a Trading Firm or Institution organization, you can also grant various permissions to your members. There are several categories of permissions you can grant to members, including the following:

  • Node permissions - The ability to create, stop, and delete resource nodes.
  • Team permissions - The ability to add members, remove members, and edit member permissions.
  • Storage permissions - The ability to add data to the ObjectStore, remove data from the ObjectStore, and subscribe to more ObjectStore storage space.
  • Alpha token permissions - The ability to create, delete, and read tokens that are used to access the Alpha Streams SDK.
  • Alpha exchange permissions - The ability to read from the Alpha Streams exchange.
