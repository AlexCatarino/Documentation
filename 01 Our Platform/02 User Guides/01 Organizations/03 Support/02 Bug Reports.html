The open-source Lean trading engine is under constant development, so you may occasionally experience bugs when using it. If you think you have found a bug, share a backtest with us that reproduces the issue. You can contact us either through email, Discord, or the forum. We will review your submission and if we confirm that it is a bug, we will create a GitHub Issue to have it resolved. Subscribe to the GitHub Issue to track our progress in fixing the bug.
