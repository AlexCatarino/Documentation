Team organizations have all of the features included in Quant Researcher organizations and more features are included. With the Team organization, you can have a team of up to 10 members and all of the members can collaborate on a single project. These organizations can have up to 10 backtesting nodes, 10 research nodes, and 10 live trading nodes. The organization can produce 10 times the number of logs per day and per backtest as Quant Researcher organizations.

With a Team organization, you can expand the capacity of your ObjectStore from 500MB up to 5GB. To accelerate your organization's onboarding to the QuantConnect platform, annual contracts for onboarding services are provided to Team organizations.
