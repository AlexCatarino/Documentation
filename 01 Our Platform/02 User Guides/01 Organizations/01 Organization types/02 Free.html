Free organizations get access to data for all the asset classes we support. This organization type has access to daily, hour, and minute resolutions for backtesting and researching trading strategy. Free organizations get one free backtest node and one free research node. Backtesting is free and you can use the built-in auto-complete and debugging features to assist with writing algorithms. When your backtests are done running, you can create a professional grade report to summarize your algorithm’s performance.

Free organizations get access to the educational content listed in our documentation, community forum, and YouTube channel. For further assistance writing algorithms, Free organizations also have access to the Learning Center. When you’re comfortable developing algorithms and running backtests, you can upgrade your organization to unlock live trading.
