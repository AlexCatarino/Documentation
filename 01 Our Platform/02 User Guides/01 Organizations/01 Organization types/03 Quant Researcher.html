Quant Researcher organizations have all of the features included in Free organizations and more features are included. With the Quant Research organization, you gain access to the LEAN CLI and QuantConnect API. Second and tick resolution datasets are available to run high-frequency strategies. The Quant Researcher organization has access to live trading through real money and paper trading brokerages.

Quant Researcher organizations can have an unlimited number of projects, produce more backtest logs, and place 10 million orders per backtest. Quant Researcher organizations can have up to two backtesting and two live trading nodes for concurrent development and trading. This organization type has access to the Alpha Streams market and the Alpha Analytics API. To assist with developing algorithms, Quant Researcher organizations can request support from a QuantConnect engineer and can use the parameter optimization tools to boost strategy performance.
