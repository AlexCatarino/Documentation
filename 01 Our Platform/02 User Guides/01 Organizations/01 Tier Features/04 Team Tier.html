The Team tier builds on the features included in the Quant Researcher tier.

What is included in the Team tier:
-Contains all of the features included in Quant Researcher organizations and more features are included
-can have a team of up to 10 members
-Project collaboration
-can have up to 10 backtesting nodes, 10 research nodes, and 10 live trading nodes
-can produce 10 times the number of logs per day and per backtest as Quant Researcher organizations
-can expand the capacity of your ObjectStore
-annual contracts for onboarding services are provided
