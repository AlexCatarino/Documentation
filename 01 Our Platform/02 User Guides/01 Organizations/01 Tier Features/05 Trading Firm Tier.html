The Trading Firm tier builds on the features included in the Team tier. Organizations on the Trading Firm tier can have an unlimited number of members and an unlimited number of collaborators simultaneously working on individual projects while maintaining IP ownership of all the projects. There is no limit on the number of backtesting, research, and live trading nodes these organizations can rent. Trading Firm organizations can produce 5MB of logs/backtest and 50MB of logs/day.

The owner of a Trading Firm organization can grant various permissions to the organizationâ€™s members, including designating a member to manage billing. Trading Firm organizations have access to custom lean builds, so they can use feature branches or historical master branches to run their strategies. In addition to the brokerages and data feeds available to Team organizations, Trading Firm organizations have access to the Atreyu brokerage and can use Interactive Brokers Financial Advisor accounts to manage clients sub accounts.
