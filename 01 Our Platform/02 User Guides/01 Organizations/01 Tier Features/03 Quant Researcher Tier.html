The Quant Researcher tier builds on the features included in the Free tier. Organizations on the Quant Researcher tier have access to the CLI, QuantConnect API, Alpha Streams Market. Quant Researchers can add on the Alpha Analytics API to perform bulk research, licensing, and correlation analysis on the Alphas in the Alpha Market. In addition, if you need assistance from a QuantConnect engineer, support seats are available for Quant Researchers to request private support.

In Quant Researcher organizations, members can use second and tick resolution data from the Datasets Market. There is no limit on the number of projects these organizations can hold. They can produce up to 100KB of logs/backtest, 1MB of logs/day. and 10 million orders/backtest, and run up to two concurrent backtests by using two backtesting nodes. After a successful backtest, members in Quant Researcher organizations can use parameter optimization tools to improve the performance of their backtest. When you're ready to deploy your strategies to live, Quant Researcher organizations can add up to 2 live trading nodes to unlock live trading with real or fictional money.
