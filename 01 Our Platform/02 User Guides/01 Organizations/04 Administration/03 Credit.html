QuantConnect Credit (QCC) is credit within your organization that you can use on the platform. QCC enables members to make micropayments on the website without using a credit card for each transaction. You can use QCC to perform the following actions:

  • Run parameter optimizations
  • Download datasets
  • Gift to members in the forum
  • Apply to your organization's monthly invoice

You can purchase QCC in the Algorithm Lab. The conversion rate is 1 QCC = $0.01 USD. Since QCC is owned by organizations instead of members, all of the members within your organization have the ability to spend the QCC balance.
