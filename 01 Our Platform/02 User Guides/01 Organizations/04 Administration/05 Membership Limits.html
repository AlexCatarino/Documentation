The number of members an organization can have depends on the organization's tier. In general, higher organization tiers can have more members within the organization to share resources. This design enables you to upgrade your organization as your trading business grows over time. The following list shows how many members each organization tier can have:

  • Free: 1
  • Quant Researcher: 1
  • Team: 10
  • Trading Firm: Unlimited
  • Institution: Unlimited
