What are resources:
-Hardware resources are for running backtests, launching research notebooks, and deploying live trading algorithms to co-located servers
-Storage resources for storing data between backtests and during live trading via the ObjectStore
-QuantConnect Credits (QCC) resources are for:
    - running parameter optimizations
    - downloading datasets
    - giving as tips in forum
    - applying to your invoice

Why are resources shared:
-To promote efficiency.
    - A team of several quants can all share 1 backtest/reserach/live trading node
