What is storage:
    - The ObjectStore is a project-specific key-value storage location to save and retrieve strings and bytes
    - You can save projects and files on our cloud servers under your QC account
    - organizations can save data in the ObjectStore

Why use storage:
    -Common OS use cases:
        - Saving algorithm variable values between deployments
        - Transporting data between the backtesting environment and the research environment
        - Training machine learning models in the research environment before deploying them to live trading
