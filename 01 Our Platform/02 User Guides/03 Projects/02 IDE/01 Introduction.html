What is the IDE:
    - Integrated development environment to develop algorithms and launch research notebooks
    - We have an IDE for web or local to create your projects
          - When using a local IDE, you can either execute locally or push to our servers for execution
Why use it:
    -When a project is created/opened, the IDE appears.
    -Allows you to access your trading algorithms from anywhere in the world with just an internet connection and a browser
    -If you'd prefer to use a different IDE, checkout the CLI (link to docs) and develop locally in your preferred editor/IDE
