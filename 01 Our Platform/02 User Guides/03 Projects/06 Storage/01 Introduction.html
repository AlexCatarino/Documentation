The projects of your organizations are stored on our cloud servers and are accessible with your account. The ObjectStore is a project-specific key-value storage location to save and retrieve strings and bytes. Some common use cases of the ObjectStore include the following:

  • Saving algorithm variable values between deployments
  • Transporting data between the backtesting environment and the research environment
  • Training machine learning models in the research environment before deploying them to live trading

This page explains the limitations imposed on storage for the ObjectStore, files, and projects.
