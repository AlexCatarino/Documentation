-What is it:
    - Inviting a team member to collaborate
    - You add them by their name in the organization or their QC email address
    - They will receive an email with a link to the project, you can also just send them the project link
    - They can then access the project from their list of QC projects
    - Team members can't be removed
Why add members:
    - Unlocks project collaboration
    - Multiple team members working on the same project simultaneously (you'll see their cursors)
