There are several ways you can share a research notebook with others. First, you can share the project that contains your research notebook with a member of your organization. When you share the project with other members, they can view the notebook but collaboration on notebooks is not currently supported. Second, you can run a backtest, generate a link to share the backtest, and give the backtest link to others to give them a copy of the notebook. Third, you can download the notebook in various file formats (.html, .pdf, .ipynb, etc) and share the downloaded file. Although, uploading .ipynb files is not currently supported.

Sharing your notebooks is beneficial when you want to accomplish any of the following:

  • Publishing your findings online or in the community forum
  • Get feedback on your research process
  • Explain your methodology to others
  • Report data issues
