Sharing your notebooks is helpful when you want to accomplish any of the following:

  • Publish your findings online or in the community forum
  • Get feedback on your research process
  • Explain your methodology to others
  • Report data issues

There are several ways that you can share a research notebook with others:

  • Add a team member to the project. They will be able to view the notebook but the Research Environment does not currently support collaboration.
  • Run a backtest, generate a link to share the backtest, and then give the backtest link to the other person. They will be able to clone the project and launch the Research Environment using the notebook files.
  • Download the notebook and then share the downloaded file with the other person. You can download notebooks in various file formats (.html, .pdf, .ipynb, etc) but the Research Environment does not support uploading .ipynb files.
