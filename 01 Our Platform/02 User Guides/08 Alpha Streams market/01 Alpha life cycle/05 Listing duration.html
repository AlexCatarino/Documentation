What is the listing duration:
-Alphas are listed until they have failed the live trading performance metrics (after the grace period)
-Authors can request to have their alpha removed from the marketplace at anytime but QuantConnect reserves the right to list an alpha for 1 year (refer to contract that’s in Author section)

Why is there a listing duration:
-To ensure the market is filled with high-quality alphas
-To churn out bad-performing (or overfit) alphas
-So if you’re invested in an alpha, if you want to keep invested in the alpha, we will keep the alpha for 1 year after it was published, even if the author wants to remove it.
