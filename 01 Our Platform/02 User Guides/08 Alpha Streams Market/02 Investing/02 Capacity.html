What is capacity:
- The maximum amount of money an alpha can trade before its performance degrades from market impact
- We have a model to estimate the capacity of each alpha streams algorithm

Why is there capacity:
-To ensure the alphas listed in the marketplace aren't placing over-crowded trades (which would harm the performance of the alpha)
-To limit the number of investors that can subscribe to an alpha's signals to a reasonable amount
