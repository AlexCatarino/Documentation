What is sharing:
- Definition: giving someone else access to your research notebook
- Collaboration (working on the same notebook) is not supported
- Notebooks can be downloaded in various file formats (html, pdf, ipynb, etc) [ipynb is not uploadable]
- Running a backtest and then giving the link to another person

Why share:
- To publish your findings online or in the forum
- To get a second opinion on your research process
- To document how to do something (you can add markdown that explains your methodology)
- To report a data issue
