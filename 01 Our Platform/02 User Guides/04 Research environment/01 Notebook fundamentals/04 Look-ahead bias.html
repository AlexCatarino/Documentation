^ Rename to Lookahead bias?

What are the risks:
- Lookahead bias: Using information in the future to make decisions in the present

Why is lookahead bias a risk in Research:
-Research is batch processing instead of event-driven (one-by-one, backtesting)
-You aren't bounded by the Time frontier like you are in backtesting
-Accidentally training and testing on the same dataset (in-sample)
-Researching using a universe of securities that you know have already performed well (aka survivorship bias)
