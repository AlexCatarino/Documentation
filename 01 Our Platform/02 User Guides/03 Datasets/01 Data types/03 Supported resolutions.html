Resolution is the duration of time that was used to sample a data source. We support the following data resolutions:

  • Tick
  • Second
  • Minute
  • Hour
  • Daily

Data density describes the frequency of entries in a dataset. Datasets at the tick resolution have dense data density. All other resolutions have regular data density. If a non-tick resolution dataset doesnâ€™t have an entry at each sampling, it has sparse density.
