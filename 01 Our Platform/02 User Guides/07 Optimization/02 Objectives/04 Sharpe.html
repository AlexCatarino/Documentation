What is Sharpe ratio:
-A measure of the excess returns relative to a benchmark, divided by the standard deviation of those returns
   - This definition will be in the glossary, with the formula

Why optimize for sharpe (pros):
-It maximizes returns while minimizing large swings in the equity curve
-A strategy with a smooth equity curve is easy to keep running live

Why not to optimize for sharpe (cons):
-Your strategy's returns may be limited in favor of a smoother equity curve
