What is CAGR:
-Compounding annual growth rate (CAGR)
-The yearly growth rate that would be required to generate the backtest return
     - this definition will be in the glossary

Why use CAGR (Pros):
-Maximizes the return of the strategy over the long term

Why not to use CAGR (Cons):
-May experience large swings in the equity (lost of risk)
-It may be hard to keep an algorithm running in live mode that has large swings in equity value
    -Stress and human emotions
-If a lot of traders are optimizing for CAGR, perhaps your strategy will be correlated with other traders running/optimizing a similar strategy
