What is PSR:
-Probabilistic Sharpe Ratio (PSR)
-the probability that the estimated Sharpe ratio SR is above a given benchmark
-Redirect to forum thread (https://www.quantconnect.com/forum/discussion/6483/probabilistic-sharpe-ratio) or move to glossary

Why optimize for PSR (Pros):
-It's used as a requirement for Alpha Streams algorithms
-Ensures a smooth equity curve throughout the backtest

Why not optimize for PSR (cons):
-Like optimizing for the Sharpe ratio, optimizing for PSR can limit your returns in favor of a smoother equity curve
