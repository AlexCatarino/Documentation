What are the resources:
- Running an optimization requires the use of optimization nodes
- <table of optimizatio node specs>
     - 02-8, 2 cores, 8GB RAM
     - 04-12, 4 cores, 12 GB RAM
     - O8-16, 8 cores, 16GB RAM
-Optimization nodes are not the nodes subscribed to in your organization
-You can use 1-24 nodes when optimizing. When using multiple nodes, the backtests run concurrently. When selecting multiple nodes, they all have the same specs (ex: all are 02-8 nodes)

Why are there different optimization nodes available:
- Different strategies require different node specs.
    - 02-8 is best for relatively simple strategies with less than 100 assets.
    - 04-12 is best for strategies with less than 500 assets and simple universe selections.
    - 08-16 is best for complex strategies and machine learning.
