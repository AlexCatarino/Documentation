What is the optimization wizard:
-A GUI in the IDE where you can select the optimization parameters, objectives, strategy, constraints, and which hardware resources to use, and how many nodes to run with
-The wizard automatically calculates the cost of running the optimizer for you

Why is there a wizard:
-To launch the optimization process for you through a point and click process
-To make performing optimizations easy and in the same location as where you write the algorithms & perform research
-To estimate how long the optimization process will take
