What:
-Optimization results list all of the individual backtests that were run during the optimization, along with their values from all the 'objectives'
-These results can be exported to csv format for further analysis, or you can open them to view the individual backtest result page

Why would I use these:
-To perform deeper analysis into individual backtests that were performed by the optimizer
    - You can use these individual backtests to review their performance in the GUI or use the api `ReadBacktest` method
