There are limitations of the optimizer when your backtests throw a runtime error or produce too much data.

Runtime Errors

If a backtest in your optimization job throws a runtime error, the backtest will not complete but your organization's QuantConnect Credit will still be consumed.

Data Overload

If a backtest in your optimization job produces more than 700MB of data, then Lean can't upload the results and the optimization job appears to never be complete.
