There are limitations of the optimizer when your backtests throw a run-time error or produce too much data.

Run-time Errors

If a backtest in your optimization job throws a run-time error, the backtest will not complete but you will still be charged.

Data Overload

If a backtest in your optimization job produces too much data (> 700MB), then Lean is not capable of uploading the results and the optimization job appears to never complete.
