What are the equity curves:
- The equity curves of each backtest that was run
- These are displayed in real-time as the optimizer progresses

Why are equity curves displayed:
-To show how the backtests have performed in terms of equity as the parameters are adjusted
-To show how sensitive the returns are to the range of parameters that the optimizer selects
-So you can get a closer look at specific times in history that the backtests were run over
