We encourage you to contribute optimization strategies to the Lean open-source project. You can contribute any strategy that is popular in the literature and is not already implemented. Refer to our GitHub repository to view which optimization strategies are already implemented. If you contribute a strategy, you'll receive some QuantConnect Credit, you'll be shown as a contributor to Lean on your GitHub profile, and your work will be used in the Algorithm Lab by our community of over 160,000 quants.
