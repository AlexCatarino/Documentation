What are QC educators:
-QC experts who create courses in the Learning Center

Why are there QC educators:
-We are looking for experts to share their insight

What do QC educators need to provide:
-You create the description, requirements, and lessons of each course
-You provide the images for the course listing

What do QC educators receive:
-You'll get feedback from your students
-Knowledge -- the best way to learn something is to teach it to others
