What is the Tradier data feed:
-A stream of security prices directly from Tradier, the data is taken from the trades made on the Tradier platform

What subscriptions are available:
-With a Tradier Brokerage account, you can access real-time data for US Equities and US Options
   - Refer to the Tradier docs: https://documentation.tradier.com/brokerage-api

Why use the Tradier data feed:
-It represents the prices/sizes actually available at Tradier, whereas our QC data feed consolidates the prices / volumes across all the exchanges
