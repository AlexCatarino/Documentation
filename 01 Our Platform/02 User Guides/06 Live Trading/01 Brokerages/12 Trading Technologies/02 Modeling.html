The TradingTechnologiesBrokerageModel does not have specific modeling since it's not a real brokerage. Instead, the TradingTechnologiesBrokerageModel applies the modeling of another brokerage that we specify. For the TT limitations, we recommend additional tests with setting the brokerage model to TradingTechnologiesBrokerageModel.

SetBrokerageModel(BrokerageName.TradingTechnologies, AccountType.Cash);
SetBrokerageModel(BrokerageName.TradingTechnologies, AccountType.Margin);
self.SetBrokerageModel(BrokerageName.TradingTechnologies, AccountType.Cash)
self.SetBrokerageModel(BrokerageName.TradingTechnologies, AccountType.Margin)
