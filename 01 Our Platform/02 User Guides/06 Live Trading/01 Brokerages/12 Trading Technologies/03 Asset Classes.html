The TradingTechnologiesBrokerageModel supports trading Futures.

AddFuture(Futures.Indices.SP500EMini, Resolution.Minute, Market.CME);
self.AddFuture(Futures.Indices.SP500EMini, Resolution.Minute, Market.CME)

If you call SetBrokerageModel with the correct BrokerageName, then you don't need to pass a Market argument to AddFuture because the market will be automatically mapped.
