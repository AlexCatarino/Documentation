The OandaBrokerageModel supports trading Forex and CFDs.

AddForex("EURUSD", Resolution.Minute, Market.Oanda);

AddCfd("AU200AUD", Resolution.Minute, Market.Oanda);
self.AddForex("EURUSD", Resolution.Minute, Market.Oanda)

self.AddCfd("AU200AUD", Resolution.Minute, Market.Oanda)

If you call SetBrokerageModel with the correct BrokerageName, then you don't need to pass a Market argument in the method calls above because the market will be automatically mapped.
