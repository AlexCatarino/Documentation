Orders filled with the Coinbase brokerage are subject to maker and taker fees. Before 2019/3/23 1:30am UTC, orders are subject to a 0% maker fee and a 0.3% taker fee. After 2019/3/23 1:30am UTC, orders are subject to a 0.15% maker fee and a 0.25% taker fee. Maker fees are paid when you add liquidity to the order book by placing a limit order that doesn't cross the spread. Taker fees are paid when you remove liquidity from the order book by placing an order that crosses the spread. The GDAXFeeModel does not currently model your 30-day trading volume to adjust the maker and taker fees.
