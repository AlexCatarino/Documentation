Coinbase was founded by Brian Armstrong and Fred Ehrsam in 2012 with the goal to "increase economic freedom in the world"1. Coinbase provides access to trading Cryptocurrencies for clients in over 100 countries2 with no minimum deposit. Coinbase also provides a self-hosted Crypto wallet, a Visa debit rewards card, and Bitcoin collateral-backed lines of credit. The former name of Coinbase and Coinbase Pro was GDAX, so the GDAXBrokerageModel connects to the brokerage connection in live trading and simulates order fills in backtesting.
