The QuantConnect Paper Trading brokerage supports trading Equities, Cryptocurrencies, Forex, and Futures.

// If SetBrokerageModel is correctly called, the market will be mapped automatically without explicitly stating it
AddCrypto("BTCUSDT", Resolution.Minute);
AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Minute);
AddForex("EURUSD", Resolution.Minute);
AddFuture(Futures.Indices.SP500EMini, Resolution.Minute);
# If SetBrokerageModel is correctly called, the market will be mapped automatically without explicitly stating it
self.AddCrypto("BTCUSDT", Resolution.Minute)
self.AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Minute)
self.AddForex("EURUSD", Resolution.Minute)
self.AddFuture(Futures.Indices.SP500EMini, Resolution.Minute)

If you set the brokerage model to a model other than the DefaultBrokerageModel, the new brokerage model defines the subset of asset classes that are available to trade.
