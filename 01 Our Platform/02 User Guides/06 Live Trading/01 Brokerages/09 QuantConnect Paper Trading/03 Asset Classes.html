The QuantConnect Paper Trading brokerage supports trading Equities, Cryptocurrencies, Forex, and Futures.

AddCrypto("BTCUSDT", Resolution.Minute, Market.GDAX);

AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Minute, Market.USA);

AddForex("EURUSD", Resolution.Minute, Market.Oanda);

AddFuture(Futures.Indices.SP500EMini, Resolution.Minute, Market.CME);
self.AddCrypto("BTCUSDT", Resolution.Minute, Market.GDAX)

self.AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Minute, Market.USA)

self.AddForex("EURUSD", Resolution.Minute, Market.Oanda)

self.AddFuture(Futures.Indices.SP500EMini, Resolution.Minute, Market.CME)

If you set the brokerage model to a model other than the DefaultBrokerageModel, the new brokerage model defines the subset of asset classes that are available to trade. If you call SetBrokerageModel with the correct BrokerageName, then you don't need to pass a Market argument in the method calls above because the market will be automatically mapped.
