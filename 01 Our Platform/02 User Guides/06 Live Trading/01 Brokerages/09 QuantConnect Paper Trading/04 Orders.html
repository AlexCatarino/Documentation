Order Types

The QuantConnect Paper Trading brokerage model supports the following order types:

  • MarketOrder
  • LimitOrder
  • StopMarketOrder
  • StopLimitOrder
  • MarketOnOpenOrder
  • MarketOnCloseOrder
  • LimitIfTouchedOrder

Handling Splits


If you have limit orders in an order book and a stock split occurs, the QuantConnect Paper Trading brokerage automatically adjusts the quantity, limit price, and stop price of your orders.
