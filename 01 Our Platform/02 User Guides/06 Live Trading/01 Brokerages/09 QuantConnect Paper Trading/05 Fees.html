If you don't set a brokerage model with the SetBrokerageModel method, the QuantConnect Paper Trading brokerage model uses the DefaultBrokerageModel. The DefaultBrokerageModel charges the following fees for each of the supported asset classes:

  • Equity: $0.005/share with a $1 minimum fee
  • Crypto: $1.85/contract
  • Forex: $0
  • Futures: $0
