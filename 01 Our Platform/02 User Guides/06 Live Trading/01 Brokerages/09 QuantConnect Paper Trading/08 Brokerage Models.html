If you don't want to use the DefaultBrokerageModel in your paper trading algorithm, select a different brokerage model by passing any of the following BrokerageNames to the SetBrokerageModel method:

  • AlphaStreams
  • Atreyu
  • Binance
  • Bitfinex
  • FTX
  • GDAX
  • InteractiveBrokersBrokerage
  • Kraken
  • OandaBrokerage
  • TradierBrokerage
  • TradingTechnologies
