The AtreyuBrokerageModel provides short availability information by the AtreyuShortableProvider. If you place a short order for a security when there are no shares available to short for the security, the order is invalidated.

var quantity = CalculateOrderQuantity(symbol, -0.1m);
var canShort = Shortable(symbol, quantity);
quantity = self.CalculateOrderQuantity(self.symbol, -0.1)
can_short = self.Shortable(self.symbol, quantity)
