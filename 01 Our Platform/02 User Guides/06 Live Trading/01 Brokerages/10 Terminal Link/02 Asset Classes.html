Terminal Link supports trading Cryptocurrencies, Equities, Equity Options, Futures, and Future Options.

AddCrypto("BTCUSDT", Resolution.Minute);

AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Minute);

AddOption("SPY", Resolution.Minute);

var goldFutures = AddFuture(Futures.Metals.Gold, Resolution.Minute);

AddFutureOption(goldFutures.Symbol, universe => universe.Strikes(-5, +5));
self.AddCrypto("BTCUSDT", Resolution.Minute)

self.AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Minute)

self.AddOption("SPY", Resolution.Minute)

gold_futures = self.AddFuture(Futures.Metals.Gold, Resolution.Minute)

self.AddFutureOption(gold_futures.Symbol, lambda universe: universe.Strikes(-5, +5))

If SetBrokerageModel method is correctly called, the market will be mapped automatically without explicitly stating the market attribute.
