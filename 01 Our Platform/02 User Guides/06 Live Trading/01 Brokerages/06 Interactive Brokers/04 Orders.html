Order Types

The IB brokerage model supports the following order types:

  • MarketOrder
  • LimitOrder
  • StopMarketOrder
  • StopLimitOrder
  • MarketOnOpenOrder
  • MarketOnCloseOrder
  • LimitIfTouchedOrder
