The InteractiveBrokersBrokerageModel supports trading Equities, Options, Futures, and Future Options.

AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Minute, Market.USA);

AddOption("SPY", Resolution.Minute, Market.USA);

var goldFutures = AddFuture(Futures.Metals.Gold, Resolution.Minute, Market.USA);

AddFutureOption(goldFutures.Symbol, universe => universe.Strikes(-5, +5));
self.AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Minute, Market.USA)

self.AddOption("SPY", Resolution.Minute, Market.USA)

gold_futures = self.AddFuture(Futures.Metals.Gold, Resolution.Minute, Market.USA)

self.AddFutureOption(gold_futures.Symbol, lambda universe: universe.Strikes(-5, +5))

If you call SetBrokerageModel with the correct BrokerageName, then you don't need to pass a Market argument in the method calls above because the market will be automatically mapped.
