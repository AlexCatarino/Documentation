The price of filling orders with the IB brokerage model depends on the asset class that you're trading. The fees for trading Equities, Futures, and Futures Options are the following:

  • Equities: $0.005/share with a $1 minimum fee
  • Futures: $1.85/contract
  • Future Options: $1.85/contract

The fees for trading Options depend on your monthly volume and the premium of the contract you're trading. The following table summarizes the fees for trading Options:
