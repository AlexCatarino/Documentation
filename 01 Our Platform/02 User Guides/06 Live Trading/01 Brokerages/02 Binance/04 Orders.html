The Binance brokerage supports the following order types:

  • MarketOrder
  • LimitOrder
  • LimitIfTouchedOrder
  • StopMarketOrder
  • StopLimitOrder
  • MarketOnOpenOrder
  • MarketOnCloseOrder

When creating orders, the quantity must be rounded to the nearest integer. After orders are created, they can be updated and canceled as desired. When applying time in force instructions to orders, the DayTimeInForce is supported.
