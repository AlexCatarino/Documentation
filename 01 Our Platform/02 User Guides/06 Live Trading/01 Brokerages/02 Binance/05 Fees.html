Orders filled with the Binance brokerage are subject to a 0.1% maker and taker fee. Maker fees are paid when you add liquidity to the order book by placing a limit order that doesn't cross the spread. Taker fees are paid when you remove liquidity from the order book by placing an order that crosses the spread. The BinanceFeeModel does not currently model the VIP trading levels to adjust the maker and taker fees.
