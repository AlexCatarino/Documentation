Check the Fees Schedule page on the Bitfinex website to see the live trading order fees. In backtests, the fees are modeled with the BitfinexFeeModel, which charges a 0.1% maker fee and a 0.2% taker fee. If you add liquidity to the order book by placing a limit order that doesn't cross the spread, you pay maker fees. If you remove liquidity from the order book by placing an order that crosses the spread, you pay taker fees. The BitfinexFeeModel does not currently model your 30-day trading volume and LEO balance to adjust the fees.

You must have the following fee settings on your Account > Fees page on the Bitfinex website to use the Bitfinex brokerage in a live algorithm:

  • Default currency for fees: USD
  • Fee type for Exchange orders: Currency Exchange Fee
