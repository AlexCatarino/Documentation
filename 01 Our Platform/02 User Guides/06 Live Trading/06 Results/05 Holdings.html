The live performance page has a Holdings tab that displays your positions and cash.

The positions section displays the following information for each of your positions:

  • Symbol: The ticker of the security.
  • Average Price: The average price you paid for your position.
  • Quantity: The size of your position.
  • Market Value: The value of your position if you were to sell it with market orders.
  • Unrealized: The unrealized profit of your position, including fees and spread costs.

The Cash section displays the quantity of each currency in your CashBook.

Use the Holdings tab to view your holdings, add security subscriptions, and place manual orders.
