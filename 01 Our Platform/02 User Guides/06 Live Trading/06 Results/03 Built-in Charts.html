The live performance page displays a built-in Strategy Equity chart so that you can analyze the performance of your algorithm in real-time. The Strategy Equity chart displays the following series:

  • Equity: The live equity curve of your algorithm.
  • Out of Sample Backtest: The backtest equity curve of your algorithm during the live trading period.
  • Meta: Points in time when you stopped your algorithm, redeployed your algorithm, or your algorithm encountered a runtime error.
