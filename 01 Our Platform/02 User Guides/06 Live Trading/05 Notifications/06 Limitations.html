Note the following limitations of the notification system.

Backtests

You can only send notifications in live algorithms. All notifications are ignored in backtesting so that you don't send thousands of notifications when you run a backtest.

Data Distribution

You may not distribute data through notifications. We source datasets from external vendors and we must uphold their license terms.

Brokerage Replacement

You may not use the notification system to place trades through unsupported brokerages. If your brokerage is not supported by LEAN, contact us and weâ€™ll consider integrating your brokerage.
