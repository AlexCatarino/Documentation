What is reconciliation:
-When a live trading a backtesting algorithm have different results when trading the same strategy over the same time period.

Why does reconciliation occur:
-Backtesting is a simulation where we try to model reality as close a possible.
-There is always a slight difference in how orders are processed between backtests and live trading.
-Discrepancy in data and modeling vs reality
    - Trade slippage, fill prices, partial fills, market impact, capacity
