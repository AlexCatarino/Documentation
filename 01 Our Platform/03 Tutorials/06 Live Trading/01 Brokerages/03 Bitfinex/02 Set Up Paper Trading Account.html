Create a paper trading sub-account, create API credentials, and then add paper capital to your account to set up your account for paper trading.

Create Sub-Account

Follow these steps to set up a sub-account for paper trading:

  1. Log in to your Bitfinex account.
  2. In the top navigation bar, hover over the person icon and then click Sub-Accounts.
  3. On the Sub-Accounts page, enter your email, username, and password, check the Paper Trading account check box, and then click Create.
  4. If you have two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled, in the Two-Factor Authentication window, enter your 2FA token and then click Authenticate.
  5. Open your email and then click the confirmation link from Bitfinex.
  6. In the top navigation bar, click Return to Platform.
  7. In the top navigation bar, hover over the Sub-Accounts menu and then click the username of your paper trading account.
  8. In the Two-Factor Authentication section, click Setup and then follow the instructions to set up 2FA for your sub-account.

Create API Credentials

After you create your paper trading sub-account, follow these steps to create API credentials for your sub-account:

  1. In the left navigation bar, click API.
  2. On the API page, click Create New Key.
  3. Turn on the Create and cancel orders toggle.
  4. In the Label Your API Key field, enter a label and then click Generate API key.
  5. In the Two-Factor Authentication window, enter your 2FA token and then click Authenticate.
  6. Click Okay.
  7. Open your email and then click the confirmation link from Bitfinex.
  8. Store your API key and API key secret somewhere safe.

Add Paper Capital

After you create API credentials for your paper trading sub-account, follow these steps to add capital to your account:

  1. In the top navigation bar, click Trading.
  2. In the Balances section, click Refill Paper Balances.
  3. In the TESTUSD and TESTUSDT fields, enter the amount of paper capital that you want.
  4. Click Submit.

    5. "Paper trading balances updated" displays.
