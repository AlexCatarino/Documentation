Follow these steps to set up SMS notifications:

  1. In the Deploy Live page, enable at least one of the following:
    • Order Events: Be notified when order events occur.
    • Insights: Be notified when insights are emitted.
  2. Click SMS.
  3. Enter a phone number.
  4. Click Add.

    5. If you want to add more SMS notifications, click Add Notification and then continue from step 2.
