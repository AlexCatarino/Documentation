Follow these steps to set up email notifications:

  1. On the Deploy Live page, enable at least one of the following:
    • Order Events: Be notified when order events occur.
    • Insights: Be notified when insights are emitted.
  2. Click Email.
  3. Enter an email address.
  4. Enter a subject.
  5. Click Add.

    6. If you want to add more email notifications, click Add Notification and then continue from step 2.
