Follow these steps after selecting Tradier as your brokerage in the tutorial in the introduction:

  1. On the algorithm deployment page, enter your Tradier account ID and access token.

    2. Your Tradier account ID and access token are on your Settings/API Access page.

  2. Click the Environment dropdown and then click one of the following environments:
    • Real: Trade with real money.
    • Demo: Trade with fictional money.
  3. If you have subscriptions to Tradier data feeds and want to use them, click the Data Provider dropdown and then click Tradier.
  4. Continue from step 5 in the tutorial in the introduction.
