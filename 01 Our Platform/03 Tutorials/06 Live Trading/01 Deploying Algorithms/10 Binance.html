Follow these steps after selecting Binance as your brokerage in the tutorial in the introduction:

  1. Enter your Binance API key ID and secret.

    2. You can generate new API credentials on the API Management page.

  2. Click the Environment dropdown and then click one of the following environments:
    • Real: Trade with real money.
    • Demo: Trade with fictional money.
  3. Continue from step 5 in the tutorial in the introduction.
