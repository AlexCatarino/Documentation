Follow these steps after selecting Oanda as your brokerage in the tutorial in the introduction:

  1. Enter your Oanda account ID.

    2. You can find this number on your Account Statement page.

  2. Enter your Oanda API token.

    3. You can generate an API token on the Manage API Access page.

  3. Click the Environment dropdown and then click one of the following environments:
    • Real: Trade real money with fxTrade.
    • Demo: Trade fictional money with fxTrade Practice.
  4. Continue from step 5 in the tutorial in the introduction.
