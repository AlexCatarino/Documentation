Follow these steps to update or cancel a bid that you have on an Alpha allocation order book:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Strategies > Subscriptions & Bids.
  3. If you want to update a bid, follow these steps:
    1. On the Subscriptions & Bids page, click Increase Bid next to the alpha for which you want to update your bid.
    2. Place a new bid with the updated allocation and price.
  4. If you want to cancel a bid, follow these steps:
    1. On the Subscriptions & Bids page, click Cancel Bid next to the alpha for which you want to cancel your bid.
    2. Click OK.
