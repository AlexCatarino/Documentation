Follow these steps to check the viability of licensing an Alpha:

  1. Open the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Strategies > Strategies.
  3. On the All Strategies page, click an Alpha in the table.
  4. On the Alpha page, read the About section.

    5. If you reside in the European Union, you can not trade ETFs. If you reside in other countries, including the US, you may need to contact your broker to gain permission to trade ETFs.
