Follow these steps to check the viability of licensing an Alpha:

  1. Open the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Strategies > Strategies.
  3. On the All Strategies page, click an Alpha in the table.
  4. On the Alpha page, in the Key Statistics section, check the capital requirement.

    5. An Alpha's capital requirement is the minimum amount of capital that you need to allocate to the Alpha to deploy it live. If you license the Alpha less than its capital requirement, you can only deploy with a paper trading account.

  5. Check the About and Asset Allocation sections.

    6. If you reside in the European Union, you can not trade US ETFs. If you reside in other areas, including the US, you may need to contact your broker to gain permission to trade ETFs.
