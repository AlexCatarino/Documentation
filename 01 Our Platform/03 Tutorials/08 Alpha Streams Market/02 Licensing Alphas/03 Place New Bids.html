Follow these steps to place a new bid on an alpha:

  1. On the Alpha page, click License Alpha.
  2. Enter an allocation and maximum bid.

    3. Click Show Detailed View to see other bids on the order book.

  3. If you want to license the alpha for more than one month, click Show Detailed View and then select a longer period.
  4. Click License Now.

Your bid will be automatically accepted if the alpha has capacity available. Otherwise, your bid will stay on the order book until the next auction.
