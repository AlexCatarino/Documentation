Follow these steps to calculate the cost of licensing an Alpha:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Strategies > Strategies.
  3. On the Strategies page, click an Alpha in the Alphas table.
  4. On the Alpha page, click License Alpha.
  5. Enter an allocation and maximum bid price.

    6. The Summary section displays the cost of licensing the Alpha.
