Follow these steps to place a new bid on an Alpha:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Strategies > Strategies.
  3. On the All Strategies page, click the Alpha on which you want to place your bid.
  4. On the Alpha page, click License Alpha.
  5. Enter an allocation and maximum bid.

    6. You can click Show Detailed View to see other bids on the order book.

  6. If you want to license the Alpha for more than one month, click Show Detailed View and then select a longer period.
  7. Click License Now or Place Bid.

Your bid is automatically accepted if the Alpha has capacity available. Otherwise, your bid stays on the order book until the next auction.
