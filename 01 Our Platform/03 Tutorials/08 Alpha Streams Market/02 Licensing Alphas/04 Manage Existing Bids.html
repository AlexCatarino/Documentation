Update

Follow these steps to update a bid:

  1. On the Strategies > Subscriptions & Bids page, click Increase Bid next to the alpha you want to update your bid for.
  2. Place a new bid with the updated allocation and price.

Cancel

Follow these steps to cancel a bid:

  1. On the Strategies > Subscriptions & Bids page, click Cancel Bid next to the alpha you want to cancel your bid for.
  2. Click OK.
