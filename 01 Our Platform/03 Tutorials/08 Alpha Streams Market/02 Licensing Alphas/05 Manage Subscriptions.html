You can deploy Alphas to live trading, update the version of Alphas that you have deployed, and adjust your allocation to an Alpha.

Deploy Alpha

Follow these steps to deploy an Alpha to live trading:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Strategies > Subscriptions & Bids.
  3. On the Subscriptions & Bids page, click Deploy next to the Alpha that you want to deploy.
  4. Follow the live trading tutorial of the brokerage that you want to use when deploying the Alpha.

    5. If the capital in your brokerage account that you use to deploy the Alpha exceeds the allocation size granted by your Alpha subscription, you will not be able to deploy the Alpha.

Update Alpha

If the author of an Alpha you're running live updates the Alpha, stop the algorithm and then redeploy the Alpha to update the version you're running live.

Adjust Alpha Allocation

Follow these steps to adjust the amount of capital that you allocate to an Alpha:

  1. Stop the algorithm.
  2. If you want to increase your allocation to the Alpha passed the allocation size that your subscription allows, place a new bid for the Alpha with the updated allocation size.
  3. Adjust the amount of capital in your brokerage account to the amount of capital that you want to allocate to the Alpha.
  4. Redeploy the Alpha.

Cancel Subscription

You can not cancel an Alpha subscription. However, you may cancel the auto-renewing bid that you have for the Alpha.
