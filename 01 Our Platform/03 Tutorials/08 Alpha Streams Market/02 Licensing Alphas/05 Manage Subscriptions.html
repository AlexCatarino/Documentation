Deploy

Follow these steps to deploy an alpha to live trading:

  1. On the Strategies > Subscriptions & Bids page, click Deploy next to the alpha you want to deploy.
  2. Follow the Deploying Algorithms documentation.

Update

If the author of an alpha you're running live updates the alpha, follow these steps to update your deployed version:

  1. Stop the algorithm.
  2. Follow the steps in the Deploy Alpha section above.
