Follow these steps to withdraw your Alpha Streams earnings:

  1. Open your Alpha Streams transaction history.
  2. If you want to withdraw to your bank account, click Withdraw Balance.

    3. You need at least a $100 balance to perform a withdrawal. Withdraws are processed every 2 weeks and can only be processed 2 weeks after the initial deposit of your funds. Your funds are retained for at least 2 weeks to give the investors that licensed your Alpha the opportunity to request a refund.

  3. If you want to transfer your balance to QuantConnect Credit (QCC), click Transfer to QCC (+10%) twice.

    4. If you convert your balance to QCC, you will receive 110% of the value of your balance in QCC and it will expire in one year.
