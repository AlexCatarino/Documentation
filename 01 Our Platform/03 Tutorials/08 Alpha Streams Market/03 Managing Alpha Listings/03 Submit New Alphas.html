You need to register as an Alpha Streams Author to submit Alphas to the Alpha Streams Market.

Follow these steps to submit Alphas to the Alpha Streams Market:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Strategies > List to Marketplace.
  3. On the List to Marketplace page, click Submit New Alpha.
  4. Click the Select a Project field and then click the project that you want to submit to the marketplace from the drop-down menu.
  5. Click the Select a Backtest field and then click the name of the backtest that you want to submit to the marketplace from the drop-down menu.

    6. The screening tool analyzes your backtest results to determine if your algorithm is suitable for the Alpha Streams Market.

  6. If the Alpha Streams analysis tool determines your algorithm is not suitable for the Alpha Streams Market, improve your algorithm and then continue from step 1.
  7. Enter a name for the Alpha.

    8. The name must be at least 10 characters long.

  8. Enter a description for the Alpha.

    9. The description must be at least 300 characters long.

  9. If you would like to set a custom reserve price, deselect the Accept default pricing check box and then enter a reserve price.
  10. Select an image for the Alpha.
  11. Select the Yes, I'm live trading this strategy or the No, I'm not live trading this strategy check box.
  12. Select the type(s) of universe selection the algorithm uses and then click Next.
  13. Select the type(s) that best describe the alpha generation model your algorithm uses and then click Next.
  14. Select the type(s) of portfolio construction that your algorithm uses and then click Next.
  15. Select the type(s) of risk control that your algorithm uses and then click Next.
  16. Select the type(s) of execution model(s) that your algorithm uses and then click Next.
  17. Click Submit Alpha.

The Alpha Streams Team will review your submission. In the left navigation bar, click Strategies > My Listings to see the review status of your submission.
