You can edit the family settings of Alphas and submit new versions of Alphas.

Update Family Settings

Follow these steps to update the family settings of an Alpha:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Strategies > My Listings.
  3. On the My Listings page, click the Alpha that you want to update.
  4. Click Edit Alpha.
  5. Update any of the following fields:
    • Set suggested live server
    • Set family reserve price
    • Are you trading your own money?
  6. Click Save Changes.

    7. "Alpha updated successfully" displays.

Submit New Versions

Follow the steps in the Submit New Alphas section to submit a new version of an Alpha.
