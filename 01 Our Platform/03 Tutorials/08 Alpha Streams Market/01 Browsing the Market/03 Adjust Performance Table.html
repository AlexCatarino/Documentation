On the All Strategies page, you can apply filters to the Alphas table, sort the table by a column, and toggle columns in the table.

Apply Filters

Follow these steps to apply filters to the table:

  1. On the right edge of the table, click Filters.
  2. Click the column name that you want to filter.
  3. If the column you selected is alphabetical, enter the characters that must be included in the column for the Alpha to be presented in the table.
  4. If the column you selected is numerical, follow these steps:
    1. Click the operation field and then select one of the operations from the drop-down menu.
    2. Enter filter value(s) in the field(s) below the operation drop-down.

Sort Columns

Click one of the column names in the table header to sort the table by that column.

Toggle Columns

On the right edge of the table, click Columns and then select the columns that you want to include in the table and deselect the columns that you want to exclude from the table.
