On the Strategies page, you can apply filters to the Alphas table, sort the table by a column, and toggle columns in the table.

Apply Filters

Follow these steps to apply filters to the table:

  1. On the right edge of the table, click Filters.
  2. Click the column name you want the filter to be applied to.
  3. If the column you selected in step 2 is alphabetical, enter the characters that must be included in the column for the Alpha to be presented in the table.
  4. If the column you selected in step 2 is numerical, follow these steps:
    1. Click the operation field and then select one of the operations from the drop-down menu.
    2. Fill the field(s) below the operation drop-down.

Sort Columns

Click one of the column names in the table to sort the table by that column.

Toggle Columns

On the right edge of the table, click Columns and then select (deselect) the columns you want to include (exclude) in the table.
