Follow these steps to configure the optimization nodes:

  1. Under the Parameters & Constraints tab of the optimization wizard, select one of the follow node types:
    • O2-8: For relatively simple strategies with less than 100 assets.
    • O4-12: For strategies with less than 500 assets and simple universe selections.
    • O8-16: For complex strategies and machine learning.
  2. Select a maximum number of nodes to use.

After configuring the nodes, click Launch Optimization to start the optimizer.
