(Optional) Follow these steps to add optimization constraints:

  1. Under the Parameters & Constraints tab of the optimization wizard, click Add Constraint.
  2. Click the target dropdown and then select a target for the constraint.
  3. Click the operation drawdown and then an operation.
  4. Enter a constraint value.
  5. If you want to add additional constraints, continue from step 1.

After defining the parameter values and adding constraints, click Next to continue the optimization wizard.
