To remove a member from your organization, you need team removal permission. Before removing a member, ensure they are not using any compute nodes. Removing a member is not reversible and the member will lose access to the organization's projects. On the Team tier, projects that the member created in the organization will remain with the member. On the Trading Firm tier, projects that the member created in the organization will be transferred to the organization owner.

The steps to remove a member from the organization depend on the format of the Organization > Members page.

Tile View

If the Organization > Members page is in tile view, follow these steps:

  1. Click the three dots in the top-right corner of the member you want to remove and then click Remove.
  2. Click Remove.

List View

If the Organization > Members page is in list view, follow these steps:

  1. Click Remove next to the member you want to remove from the organization.
  2. Click Remove.
