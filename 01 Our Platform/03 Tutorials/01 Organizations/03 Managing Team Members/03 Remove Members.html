You need team removal permission to remove members from your organization. Before removing members, stop all nodes they are using. Removing a member is not reversible and the member will lose access to the organization's projects. On the Team tier, projects that the member created in the organization will remain with the member. On the Trading Firm and Institution tiers, projects that the member created in the organization will be transferred to the organization manager.

The steps to remove a member from the organization depend on the layout of the Organization > Members page.

Tile Layout

Follow these steps to remove members from your organization:

  1. Click the three dots in the top-right corner of the member you want to remove and then click Remove.
  2. Click Remove.

List Layout

Follow these steps to remove members from your organization:

  1. Click Remove next to the member you want to remove from the organization.
  2. Click Remove.
