You need vacant team seats and permission to add team members.

Follow these steps to add team members to your organization:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Members.
  3. On the Team Management page, click Add Member.
  4. Enter the email address of the person you'd like to add to the organization and then click Add Member.

    5. The email you enter should be the email the new member uses to log in to their QuantConnect account.

  5. If you have team edit permissions, select the permissions for the new member and then click Save Changes. Otherwise, click Cancel.

    6. The Team Management page displays and the new member included.
