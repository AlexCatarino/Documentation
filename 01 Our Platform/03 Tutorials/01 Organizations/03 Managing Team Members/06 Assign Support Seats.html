Follow these steps to assign support seats to members in your organization:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Members.
  3. On the Team Management page, scroll down to the Organization Support section and then click Add Seat.
  4. On the Support page, click Select under the tier of seat you would like to assign.
  5. In the Assign seatTier Seat section, click the team member dropdown and then select the team member to assign to the seat.
  6. Click Add Seat +$price.

If you successfully assign support seats, the Organization Support section of the Team Management page includes the new assignment.
