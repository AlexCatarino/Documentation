You need team edit permission to edit the permissions of team members. The steps to edit team permissions depend on the layout of the Organization > Members page.

Tile Layout

Follow these steps to edit team member positions:

  1. On the Organization > Members page, click the three dots in the top-right corner of the member you want to edit permissions for and then click Edit Permissions.
  2. Select and deselect permissions as desired and then click Save Changes.

List Layout

Follow these steps to edit team member positions:

  1. On the Organization > Members page, click Edit Permissions next to the member you want to edit permissions for.
  2. Select and deselect permissions as desired and then click Save Changes.
