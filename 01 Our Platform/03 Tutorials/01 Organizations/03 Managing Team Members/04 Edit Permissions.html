To edit the permissions of team members, you need team edit permission. The steps to edit team permissions depend on the format of the Organization > Members page.

Tile View

If the Organization > Members page is in tile view, follow these steps:

  1. Click the three dots in the top-right corner of the member you want to edit permissions for and then click Edit Permissions.
  2. Select and deselect permissions as desired and then click Save Changes.

List View

If the Organization > Members page is in list view, follow these steps:

  1. Click Edit Permissions next to the member you want to edit permissions for.
  2. Select and deselect permissions as desired and then click Save Changes.
