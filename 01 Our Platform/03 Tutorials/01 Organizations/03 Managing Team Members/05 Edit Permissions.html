You need team edit permission to edit the permissions of team members.

Follow these steps to edit team permissions:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Members.

    3. The Team Management page displays.

  3. If the Team Management page is in tile view, follow these steps:
    1. On the Team Management page, click the three dots in the top-right corner of the member you want to edit permissions for and then click Edit Permissions.
    2. Select and deselect permissions as desired and then click Save Changes.
  4. If the Team Management page is in list view, follow these steps:
    1. On the Team Management page, click Edit Permissions next to the member you want to edit permissions for.
    2. Select and deselect permissions as desired and then click Save Changes.

If you successfully edit team permissions, the new permissions display after clicking Edit Permissions again.
