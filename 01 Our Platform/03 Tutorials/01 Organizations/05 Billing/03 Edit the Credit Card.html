You can add, replace, and remove the organization's credit card.

Add

Follow these steps to add a credit card:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. On the Organization > Billing page, click Add Card.
  3. Enter the credit card details and then click Save.

If you successfully add a credit card, the Credit Card section displays your credit card.

Replace

Follow these steps to change the credit card:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. On the Organization > Billing page, under the Credit Card section, click Edit.
  3. Enter the new credit card details and then click Save.

If you successfully replace the credit card, the Credit Card section displays your new credit card.

Remove

Follow these steps to remove the credit card:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. On the Organization > Billing page, click Remove.
  3. Click OK.

If you successfully remove the credit card, the Credit Card section displays "No entries found".
