Follow these steps to purchase QuantConnect Credit (QCC):

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Billing.
  3. On the Billing page, scroll down to the Organization Credit (QCC) section and then click Purchase Credit.
  4. Select a credit pack and then click Continue.
  5. If your preferred organization has a credit card and you would like to charge that card, click Purchase. If your preferred organization doesn't have a credit card, enter the credit card details and then click Purchase.

    6. Only the preferred organization can be charged when purchasing QCC. If you would like to charge a different organization that you are a member of, set the organization you would like to charge as your preferred organization.

If you successfully purchase QCC, the Credit Balance section on the organization homepage displays the new balance.
