Follow these steps to download invoices and receipts:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Billing.
  3. On the Billing page, scroll down to the Invoices section and then click Download as PDF next to the invoice or receipt you want to download.
  4. Click Download invoice or Download receipt.

    5. A prompt displays to download the file to your local machine.
