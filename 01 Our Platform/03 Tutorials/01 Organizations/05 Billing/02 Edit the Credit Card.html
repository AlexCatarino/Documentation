You can add, replace, and remove the organization's credit card.

Add

Follow these steps to add a credit card:

  1. On the Organization > Billing page, click Add Card.
  2. Enter the credit card details and then click Save.

Replace

Follow these steps to change the credit card:

  1. On the Organization > Billing page, under the Credit Card section, click Edit.
  2. Enter the new credit card details and then click Save.

Remove

Follow these steps to remove the credit card:

  1. On the Organization > Billing page, click Remove.
  2. Click OK.
