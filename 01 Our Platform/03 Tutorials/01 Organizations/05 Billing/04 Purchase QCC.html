Follow these steps to purchase QuantConnect Credit (QCC):

  1. On the Organization > Billing page, scroll down to the bottom and then click Purchase Credit.
  2. Select a credit pack and then click Continue.
  3. If your preferred organization has a credit card and you would like to charge that card, click Purchase. If your preferred organization doesn't have a credit card, enter the credit card details and then click Purchase.

    4. Only the preferred organization can be charged when purchasing QCC. If you would like to charge a different organization that you are a member of, set the organization you would like to charge as your preferred organization.
