Follow these steps to view your organization's billing information:

  1. Log into the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Billing.

    3. The Billing page displays.

The Billing page includes the following sections:

  • The billing cost, date, and frequency are at the top.
  • Details: The billing name and address associated with the credit card.
  • Credit Card: The credit card used to pay the bill.
  • Products: A breakdown of the organization's subscriptions.
  • Invoices: All the organizations invoices.
  • Organization Credit (QCC): All QCC purchases and expenses.
