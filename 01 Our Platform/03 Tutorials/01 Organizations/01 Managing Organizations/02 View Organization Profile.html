Follow these steps to view your organization profile:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation menu, click Organization.

    3. The organization profile displays.

Your organization profile displays the following information:

  • View your organization's brand and stats at the top.
  • Actions: Add nodes.
  • Resources: View and manage nodes.
  • Billing: View and manage bills.
  • Team: View and manage team members.
  • Support History: View support tickets.
  • Plan: View and edit tier.
  • Credit Balance: View and purchase QuantConnect Credit (QCC).
