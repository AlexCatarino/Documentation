Log in to the Algorithm Lab and then, in the left navigation bar, click Organization > Home to view your organization profile.

Your organization profile displays the following information:

  • View your organization's brand and stats in the top banner.
  • Actions: Add nodes.
  • Resources: View and manage nodes.
  • Billing: View and manage bills.
  • Team: View and manage team members.
  • Support History: View support tickets.
  • Plan: View and edit tier.
  • Credit Balance: View and purchase QuantConnect Credit.
