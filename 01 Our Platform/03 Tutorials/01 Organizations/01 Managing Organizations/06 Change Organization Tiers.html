To change organization tiers, follow these steps:
  1. On the Pricing page, click Change Organization.
  2. Click the organization you'd like to change the tier of.
  3. Click CHOOSE TIER under the appropriate tier.
  4. Select the tier pack:
    • Select the number of tier packs you'd like in the organization
    • Click Build Your Own Pack to create a custom pack
  5. Click Proceed to Checkout
  6. Enter your credit card details and click Subscribe Now.
