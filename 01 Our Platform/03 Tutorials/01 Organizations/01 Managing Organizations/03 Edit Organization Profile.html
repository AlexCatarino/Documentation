You can edit your organization image, name, and description.

Image

Follow these steps to change your organization image:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Home.
  3. On the organization homepage, click the organization image.
  4. Click Choose file, select a file from your device, and then click Open.

    5. Your organization image must be in GIF, JPG or PNG format and less than 1MB in size.

  5. Click Save.

    6. "Photo Uploaded" displays.

Name

Follow these steps to change your organization name:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Home.
  3. On the organization homepage, hover over the organization name and then click the pencil icon that appears.
  4. Enter the new organization name and then click Save Changes.

    5. "Organization Name Updated Successfully" displays.

Description

Follow these steps to change your organization description:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Home.
  3. On the organization homepage, hover over the organization description and then click the pencil icon that appears.
  4. Enter the new organization description and then click Save Changes.

    5. "Organization Short Description Updated Successfully" displays.
