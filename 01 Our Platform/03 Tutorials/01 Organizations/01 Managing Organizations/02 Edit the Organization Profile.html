Follow these steps to change your organization image:

  1. On the Organization > Home page, click the organization image.
  2. Click Choose file, select a file from your device, and then click Open.

    3. Your organization image must be in GIF, JPG or PNG format and less than 1MB in size.

  3. Click Save.

Follow these steps to change your organization name:

  1. On the Organization > Home page, hover over the organization name and then click the pencil icon that appears.
  2. Enter the new organization name and then click Save Changes.

Follow these steps to change your organization description:

  1. On the Organization > Home page, hover over the organization description and then click the pencil icon that appears.
  2. Enter the new organization description and then click Save Changes.
