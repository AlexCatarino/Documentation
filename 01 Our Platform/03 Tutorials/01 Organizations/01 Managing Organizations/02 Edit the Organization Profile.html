Image

To change the organization image, follow these steps:
  1. On the Organization page, select the organization image.
  2. Click Choose file, select a file from your device, and then click Open.

    3. Your organization image must be in GIF, JPG or PNG format and less than 1MB in size.

  3. Click Save.

Name

To change the organization name, follow these steps:
  1. On the Organization page, hover over the organization name and then click the pencil icon that appears.
  2. Enter the new organization name and then click Save Changes.

Description

To change the organization description, follow these steps:
  1. On the Organization page, hover over the organization description and then click the pencil icon that appears.
  2. Enter the new organization description and then click Save Changes.
