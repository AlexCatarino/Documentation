Follow these steps to comment on support tickets:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. On the Organization > Support History page, click the ticket you would like to comment on.

    3. If you don't see the ticket you would like to comment on, the ticket may be closed. Click Closed to view closed tickets.

  3. Enter your comment and then click Send.

If you successfully comment on tickets, the comment displays in the chat window.
