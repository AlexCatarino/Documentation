Follow these steps to open closed support tickets:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Support History.
  3. On the Support History page, click Closed.
  4. Click the ticket you want to open.
  5. (Optional) Enter a reason for opening the ticket.
  6. Click Open Ticket and Send.

If you successfully open closed support tickets, the tickets are labeled as Open.
