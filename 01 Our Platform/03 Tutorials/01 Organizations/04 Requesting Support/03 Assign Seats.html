To assign support seats to members in your organization, follow these steps:

  1. On the Organization > Members page, scroll to the bottom of the page and then click Add Seat.
  2. Click Select under the tier of seat you would like to assign.
  3. Click the team member dropdown and then select the team member to assign to the seat.
  4. If there is a vacant support seat in your organization with the same tier you selected in step 2, click Assign Seat. Otherwise, click Add Seat +$price.
