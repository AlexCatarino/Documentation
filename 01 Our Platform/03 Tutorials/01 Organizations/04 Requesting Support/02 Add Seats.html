The number of support seats your organization can have is capped by the number of team seats the organization has. To add support seats to your organization, follow these steps:

  1. On the Organization > Home page, click Edit Plan.
  2. If the page is titled "Suggested Pack for organizationName", click Build Your Own Pack.
  3. In the "Support Seats" section, click the plus sign next to the desired seat tier.
  4. If your organization doesn't have a credit card added, click Proceed to Checkout and then enter your credit card details.
  5. Click Update Subscriptions or Subscribe Now.
