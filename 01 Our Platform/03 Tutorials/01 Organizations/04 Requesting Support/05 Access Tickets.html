View Open Tickets

To view open tickets, follow these steps:

  1. On the Organization page, click Organization > Support History.
  2. Click the ticket you would like to view.

View Closed Tickets

To closed tickets, follow these steps:

  1. On the Organization page, click Organization > Support History.
  2. Click Closed.
  3. Click the ticket you would like to view.

Close Tickets

To close tickets, follow these steps:

  1. On the Organization page, click Organization > Support History.
  2. Click the ticket you would like to close.
  3. (Optional) Enter a reason for closing the ticket.
  4. Click Send and Close Ticket.

Open Closed Tickets

To open closed tickets, follow these steps:

  1. On the Organization page, click Organization > Support History.
  2. Click Closed.
  3. Click the ticket you would like to open.
  4. (Optional) Enter a reason for opening the ticket.
  5. Click Open Ticket and Send.
