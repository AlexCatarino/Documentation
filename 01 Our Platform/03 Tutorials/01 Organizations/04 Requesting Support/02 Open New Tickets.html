Follow these steps to open a support ticket:

  1. On the Support page, enter a subject and message.
  2. If you would like to attach a live deployment or backtest to the support ticket, follow these steps:
    1. Click Add Project, select a project, and then click OK.
    2. (Optional) Click Add Live Deployment, select a live deployment, and then click OK.
    3. (Optional) Click Add Backtest, select a backtest, and then click OK.
  3. Click the By submitting this request, I give QuantConnect Support Staff permission to view my project check box.
  4. Click Send.
