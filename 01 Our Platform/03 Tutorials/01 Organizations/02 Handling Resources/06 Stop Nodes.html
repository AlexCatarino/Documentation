You need stop node permissions in the organization to stop nodes.

Follow these steps to stop nodes that are running in your organization:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Organization > Resources.
  3. On the Organization > Resources page, click the icon with the three horizontal lines in the top-right corner to format the page into list view.
  4. Click Stop in the row with the node you want to stop.

If you successfully stop nodes, the Organization > Resources page will not display Stop next to the nodes.
