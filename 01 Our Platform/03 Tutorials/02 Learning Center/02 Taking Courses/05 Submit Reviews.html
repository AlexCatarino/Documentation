You need to complete a course before you can submit a review on it.

Follow these steps to review courses:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the left navigation bar, click Learning Center > Completed.
  3. On the Completed Courses page, click the course you want to review.
  4. Scroll down to the User Reviews section and then click the number of stars you want to give the course in your review.
  5. Write your review.
  6. Click Submit Review.

    7. The User Reviews section displays your review.
