Sort Reviews

To sort reviews, at the bottom of the dataset's listing page, click Most Recent and then select a metric to sort the reviews by.

Filter Reviews

To filter reviews, at the bottom of the dataset's listing page, click All and then select the number of stars to filter the reviews by.

Write Reviews

To write a review, follow these steps:

  1. At the bottom of the dataset's listing page, select a number of stars to give your review.
  2. Write your review.
  3. Click Submit Review.
