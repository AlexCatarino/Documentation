You can view the orders of your backtest in the GUI or download them to your local machine.

View in the GUI

Open the backtest results page and then click the Orders tab to see the orders that the backtest created. If there are more than 10 orders, use the pagination tools at the bottom of the Orders Summary table to see all of the orders. Click on an individual order in the Orders Summary table to reveal additional information regarding the following:

  • Submissions
  • Fills
  • Partial fills
  • Updates
  • Cancellations
  • Option contract exercising

Download CSV

Open the backtest results page, click the Orders tab, and then click Download Orders to download the orders in CSV format. The content of the CSV file is the content displayed in the Orders Summary table when the table rows are collapsed. Download the backtest results JSON file to retrieve all of the content in the Orders Summary table.
