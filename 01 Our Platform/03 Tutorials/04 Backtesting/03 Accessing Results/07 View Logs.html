You can view the logs of your backtest in the GUI or download them to your local machine.

View in the GUI

Open the backtest result page and then click the Logs tab to see the log file that was created throughout a backtest.

Download Text File

Open the backtest result page, click the Logs tab, and then click Download Logs to download the log file that was created throughout a backtest. If you just ran the backtest, you can also download the log file by clicking the link in the Console. Don't copy the link in the Console and paste it into your browser URL bar.
