To see the log file that was created throughout a backtest, on the backtest result page, click the Logs tab.

Download

To download the log file that was created throughout a backtest, follow these steps:

  1. On the backtest result page, click the Logs tab.
  2. Click Download Logs.

If you just ran the backtest, you can also download the log file by clicking the link in the Console. Copying the link in the Console and pasting it into your broswer URL bar will not work.
