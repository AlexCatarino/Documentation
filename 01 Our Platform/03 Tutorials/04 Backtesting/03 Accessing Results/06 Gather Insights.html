You can view the insights of your backtest in the GUI or download them to your local machine.

GUI

Open the backtest result page and then click the Insights tab to see the insights emitted throughout a backtest. If there are more than 10 insights, use the pagination tools at the bottom of the Insights Summary table to see all of the insights.

Download

Open the backtest result page, click the Insights tab, and then click Download Insights to download the insights in JSON format.
