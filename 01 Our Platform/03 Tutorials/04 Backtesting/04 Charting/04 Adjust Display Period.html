On the backtest results page, you can apply zoom to charts and scroll through their display period.

Zoom

Zoom in and out of a time series chart by either:

  • Clicking the 1m, 3m, 1y, or All period in the top-right corner of the chart.
  • Clicking a point on the chart and drag your mouse horizontally to highlight a specific period of time in the chart.

If you adjust the zoom on a chart, it affects all of the charts.

Scroll

After zooming in on a chart, slide the horizontal bar at the bottom of the chart to adjust the time frame that's displayed.
