GUI

To see the orders that the backtest created, on the backtest result page, click the Orders tab. Use the pagination tools at the bottom of the Orders Summary table to see all of the orders if there are more than 10. Click on an individual order in the Orders Summary table to reveal additional information regarding order fills, updates, and cancellations.

Download

To download the orders in CSV format, follow these steps:

  1. On the backtest result page, click the Orders tab.
  2. Click Download Orders.

The content of the CSV file is the content displayed in the Orders Summary table when the table rows are collapsed. Download the backtest results JSON file to retrieve all of the content in the Orders Summary table.
