You can add, update, and remove watch variables in the Debug panel.

Add

Follow these steps to add watch variables:

  1. In the Debug panel, click the plus icon.
  2. Enter the name of the variable to watch and then click OK.

Update

Follow these steps to update the name of a watch variable:

  1. In the Debug panel, click the variable name you want to update.
  2. Enter the new variable name and then click OK.

Remove

In the Debug panel, click X next to the watch variable you want to remove.
