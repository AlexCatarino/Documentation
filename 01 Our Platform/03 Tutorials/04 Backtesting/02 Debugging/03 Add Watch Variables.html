Add

To add watch variables, follow these steps:

  1. On the right edge of the IDE, click Debug.
  2. Click the plus icon.
  3. Enter the name of the variable to watch and then click OK.

Update

To update the name of a watch variable, follow these steps:

  1. On the debug panel, click the variable name you want to update.
  2. Enter the new variable name and then click OK.

Remove

To remove a watch variable, on the debug panel, click the X icon next to the watch variable you want to remove.
