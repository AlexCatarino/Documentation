To share a backtest, you must first make the backtest public. To make a backtest public, follow these steps:

  1. Open the backtest you want to share.
  2. Under the performance charts, click the Share tab.
  3. Click Make backtest public to share.

After making the backtest public, you can share the backtest by:

  • Attaching the backtest to a forum post.
  • Giving the backtest link to another person.
  • Embedding the backtest result widget into your website.
