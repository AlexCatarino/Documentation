Follow these steps to stop a backtest during execution:

  1. Open the project that you are backtesting.
  2. In the project panel, click one of the project files.
  3. On the right edge of the IDE, click Resources.
  4. Click the stop button next to the backtest node you want to stop.

    5. If you have multiple backtests executing, hover over the member name in the In Use By column to see which project the node is backtesting.
