Follow these steps to select a programming language:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. In the top navigation bar, click username > My Account.
  3. On the Your Account page, in the Account Settings section, click C# or Python.

By changing the programming language, new projects are populated with the appropriate file types (.cs or .py). In addition, autocomplete and IntelliSense are verified according to your selected programming language.
