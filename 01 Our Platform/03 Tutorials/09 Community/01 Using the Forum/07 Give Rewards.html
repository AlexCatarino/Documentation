You can reward discussions and comments with QuantConnect Credit (QCC) or a thumbs up.

QCC

You need sufficient QCC in your organization to give QCC rewards.

Follow these steps to reward a discussion or comment with QCC:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. On the discussion page, click Reward Discussion.
  3. Click a QCC reward.
  4. Click Send Reward.

Follow these steps to reward a discussion comment with QCC:

  1. At the top of the comment, click Reward Answer.
  2. Click a QCC reward.
  3. Click Send Reward.

When you give a QCC reward, the QCC is deducted from your organization's balance.

Thumbs Up

In the top-left corning of the discussion comment, click the thumb icon to give a thumbs up.
