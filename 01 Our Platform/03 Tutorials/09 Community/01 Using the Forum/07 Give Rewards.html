You need sufficient QCC in your organization to give QCC rewards.

Follow these steps to reward a discussion or comment:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. On the forum page, open the discussion for which you want to give your reward.
  3. If you want to give a QCC reward, follow these steps:
    1. On the discussion page, if you want to reward the discussion, click Reward Discussion. If you want to reward a comment in the discussion, on the comment you want to reward, click Reward Answer.
    2. Click a QCC reward.
    3. Click Send Reward.

      4. The reward displays and the QCC value of the reward is deducted from your organization's balance.

  4. If you want to give a thumbs-up, click the thumbs-up icon on the comment that you want to reward.
