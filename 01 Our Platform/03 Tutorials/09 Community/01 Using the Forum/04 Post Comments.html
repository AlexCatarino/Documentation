You can only post comments on open forum discussions.

Follow these steps to post a comment on a forum discussion:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. On the forum page, click the discussion that you want to add your comment.
  3. At the bottom of the discussion page, enter your comment.
  4. (Optional) Follow these steps to attach a backtest:
    1. Click Attach Backtest.
    2. Click the Project field and then select the project that contains the backtest you want to attach from the drop-down menu.

      3. You can only attach projects that you own. If you don't own a project that you want to attach, clone the project and then attach the cloned version of the project.

    3. Click the Backtest field and then select a backtest from the drop-down menu.

    When you attach a backtest, you're sharing all of the project files.

  5. Click Comment.

    6. Your comment is appended to the discussion.
