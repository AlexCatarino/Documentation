You can follow all forum discussions or individual discussions.

All Discussions

Log in to your account and then, on the forum homepage, click Subscribe to subscribe or unsubscribe from all forum discussions.

Individual Discussions

Follow these steps to toggle your subscription to individual discussions:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. On the forum page, open the discussion for which you want to toggle your subscription.
  3. On the discussion page, click Follow Discussion.
