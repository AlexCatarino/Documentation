You can mark individual discussions or all discussions as read.

Individual Discussions

Log in to your account and then, on the forum page, open a discussion to mark it as read.

All Discussions

Follow these steps to mark all discussions as read:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. At the top of the forum page, click the three dots icon and then click Mark all as read

    3. The notification bubbles at the top of the page disappear.
