Profile Image

Follow these steps to update your profile image:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. In the top navigation, click username > My Profile.
  3. On your profile page, click your profile image.
  4. Click Browse.
  5. Select a file from your local machine and then click Open.

    6. Your profile image must be in GIF, JPG or PNG format and less than 1MB in size.

  6. Click Save.

Username

Follow these steps to update your username:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. In the top navigation, click username > My Profile.
  3. On your profile page, hover over your username and then click the Edit box that appears.
  4. Enter your new username and then click OK.

Title

Follow these steps to update your title:

  3. On your profile page, hover over your title and then click the Edit box that appears.
  4. Enter your new title and then click OK.

Biography

Follow these steps to update your biography:

  1. On your profile page, hover over your biography and then click the Edit box that appears.
  2. Enter your biography and then click OK.

Social Media Links

Follow these steps to update your social media links:

  1. On your profile page, hover over the social media icon that you want to update and then click the Edit box that appears.
  2. Enter your social media link and then click OK.
