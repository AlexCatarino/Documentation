Follow these steps to log in to your account:

  1. In the top navigation bar, click Sign In.

    2. The Sign In to QuantConnect page displays.

  2. If you signed up with your social network, click Sign In with Google or Sign In with Facebook and then follow the prompts.

    3. If you do not have two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled on your account, the Algorithm Lab displays.

  3. If you signed up with your email address, follow these steps:
    1. Enter your email address and password.
    2. Click Sign In.

    If you do not have 2FA enabled on your account, the Algorithm Lab displays.

  4. If you have 2FA enabled on your account, in the Please Enter 2FA Code field, enter the authentication code from the Google Authenticator app on your mobile device and then click Sign In.
