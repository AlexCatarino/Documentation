Activate

Follow these steps to activate two-factor authentification:

  1. On the Your Account page, in the Security section, click Activate.
  2. Enter your email address and then click Continue.
  3. Follow the steps in the pop-up window and then click Activate Two Factor.

Deactivate

Follow these steps to deactivate two-factor authentification:

  1. On the Your Account page, in the Security section, click Deactivate.
  2. Enter your email address and then click Continue.
