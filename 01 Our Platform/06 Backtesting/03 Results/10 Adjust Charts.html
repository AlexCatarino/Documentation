Toggle Chart Displays

Open the backtest results page and then, in the Select Chart section, click the name of a chart to display and hide the chart.

Toggle Series Displays

Open the backtest results page and then click the name of a series at the top of a chart to display and hide the series.

Adjust Display Period

On the backtest results page, you can apply zoom to charts and scroll through their display period.

Zoom

Zoom in and out of a time series chart by either:

  • Clicking the 1m, 3m, 1y, or All period in the top-right corner of the chart.
  • Clicking a point on the chart and drag your mouse horizontally to highlight a specific period of time in the chart.

If you adjust the zoom on a chart, it affects all of the charts.

Scroll

After zooming in on a chart, slide the horizontal bar at the bottom of the chart to adjust the time frame that's displayed.

Organize Chart Layout

On the backtest results page, you can resize and move charts to organize the page.

Resize

Hover over the bottom-right corner of a chart to resize it. When the resize cursor appears, hold the left mouse button and then drag to the desired size.

Move

Click, hold, and drag the title of a chart to move it.

Refresh Charts

Open the backtest results page and then, in the Select Chart section, click the reset icon to refresh the charts. Refreshing the charts resets the zoom level on all the charts. If you refresh the charts while a backtest is executing, only the data that was seen by the Lean engine after you refreshed the charts is displayed.
