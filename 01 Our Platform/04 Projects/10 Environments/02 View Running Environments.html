When you open a project, a new environment starts running. The Projects page displays all of running environments for your account. To view the page, log in to the Algorithm Lab and then, in the left navigation menu, click Projects.

To open one of the environments, click the project name.

To stop the environments, click the Stop icon next to a project name.

The left navigation bar of the Algorithm Lab also shows the running environments underneath Projects. The orange environment represents the environment that's currently open. The gray environments represents the environments that are currently minimized.
