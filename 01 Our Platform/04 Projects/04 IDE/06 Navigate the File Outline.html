The Outline section in the Explorer panel makes it easier to navigate your files. The section shows the name of classes, members, and functions defined throughout the file. Click one of the names in the the Outline section to jump your cursor to the respective definition in the file.

Follow these steps to open the Outline:

  1. Open a project.
  2. In the right navigation menu, if there is no Explorer icon, right-click the right navigation bar and then click Explorer.
  3. Click the Explorer icon.
  4. In the Explorer panel, if there is no Outline section, click the three dots icon in the top-right corner of the Explorer panel and then click Outline.
