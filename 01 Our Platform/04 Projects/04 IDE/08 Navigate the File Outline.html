The Outline section in the Explorer panel is an easy way to navigate your files. The section shows the name of classes, members, and functions defined throughout the file. Click one of the names to jump your cursor to the respective definition in the file.

Follow these steps to open the Outline:

  1. Open a project.
  2. In the right navigation menu, click the Explorer icon.
  3. In the Explorer panel, if there is no Outline section, click the three dots icon in the top-right corner of the Explorer panel and then click Outline.
