The Resources panel shows the all of the backtesting, research, and live trading nodes within your organization.

The panel displays the following information for each node:

Column Description
Node The node name and model.
In Use By The owner and name of the project using the node.

If you have stop node permissions in your organization, click the stop button next to a node to stop it.

By default, we select the best node available in your clusters when you launch a backtest or research notebook. To use a specific node, click the checkbox next to a node in the panel.

Follow these steps to open the Resources panel:

  1. Open a project.
  2. In the right navigation menu, if there is no Resources icon, right-click the right navigation bar and then click Resources.
  3. Click the Resources icon.
