You must add a new organization to your account before you can edit its profile.

Follow these steps to update the organization's image and description:

  1. On the organization homepage, click the organization image.
  2. Click Choose file, select a file from your device, and then click Open.

    3. Your organization image must be in GIF, JPG or PNG format and less than 1MB in size.

  3. Click Save.

    4. "Photo Uploaded" displays.

  4. Hover over the organization description and then click the pencil icon that appears.
  5. Enter the new organization description and then click Save Changes.

    6. "Organization Short Description Updated Successfully" displays.
