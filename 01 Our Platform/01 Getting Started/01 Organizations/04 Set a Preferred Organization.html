You must add a new organization to your account before you can set it as your preferred organization.

Follow these steps to set the new organization as your preferred one:

  1. In the top navigation bar, click Connected as: organizationName.
  2. In the Switch Organization panel, select the radio button under the Preferred column that corresponds to the new organization.

    3. "Preferred organization selected" displays. Refresh the page to connect as your preferred organization.
