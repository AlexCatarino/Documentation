You must create a new project before you can migrate it. You must also be the owner of multiple organizations and be currently connected to one of them to migrate projects.

Follow these steps to migrate your project:

  1. In the project panel, click Migrate Project.
  2. Click the name of the organization to which you want to migrate the project and then click Migrate.

Migrating a project creates a copy of the project and stores it in an organization that you own but to which you are not currently connected.
