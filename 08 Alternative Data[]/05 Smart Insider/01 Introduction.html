QuantConnect hosts company buyback intentions and transactions data provided by Smart Insider. The data is rich with details surrounding buyback events and intentions.

Smart Insider data has two distinct data sets that you can use in your algorithm. One of them is Intentions, which is an announcement that establishes the intention to buyback shares of the company. Intentions are events that come before the Transaction event, which is the buyback event itself and the details surrounding the execution of the buyback, To access intentions and transactions in your algorithm, you can use the following classes:

  • $[T,QuantConnect.Data.Custom.SmartInsider.SmartInsiderIntention]
  • $[T,QuantConnect.Data.Custom.SmartInsider.SmartInsiderTransaction]

Because both of those classes derive from $[T,QuantConnect.Data.Custom.SmartInsider.SmartInsiderEvent], Transactions and Intentions both share the same set of fields. You can click on the red links above to view the available fields.
