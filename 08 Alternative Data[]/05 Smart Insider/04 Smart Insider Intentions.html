Smart Insider Intentions
EventType

SmartInsiderEventType

Describes how the buyback will take place. Possible values are:

  • Authorization - The board's authority to repurchase
  • Intention - Notification of the board that shares will be repurchased
  • Transaction - Repurchase transactions that have been actioned
  • UpwardsRevision - Unknown
  • DownwardsRevision - Unknown
  • RevisedDetails - Unknown

Execution

SmartInsiderExecution

Describes how the transaction will be executed. Possible values are:

  • Market - Took place via the open market
  • TenderOffer - Via a companywide tender offer to all shareholders
  • OffMarket - Under a specific agreement between the issuer and shareholder
intention.Execution
ExecutionEntity

SmartInsiderExecutionEntity

Describes which entity carried out the transaction. Possible values are:

  • Issuer - Intention to buyback via the issuer
  • Subsidiary - Intention to buyback via a subsidiary of the issuer
  • Broker - Intention to buyback via a broker
  • EmployerBenefitTrust - Unknown
  • EmployeeBenefitTrust - Intention to transfer shares to employees as part of remunerative plans
  • ThirdParty - Intention to buyback via an undisclosed 3rd party, which is likely to be a broker
intention.ExecutionEntity
ExecutionHolding

SmartInsiderExecutionHolding

Describes what will be done with those shares following repurchase. Possible values are:

  • Treasury - Will be held in treasury until they are sold back to the market
  • Cancellation - Will be immediately cancelled
  • Trust - Will be held in trust, generally to cover employee remunerative plans
  • SatisfyEmployeeTax - Unknown
  • NotReported - Unknown
  • Error - Unknown
