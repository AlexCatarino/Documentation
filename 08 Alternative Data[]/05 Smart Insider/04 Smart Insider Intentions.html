Smart Insider Intentions
BuybackType

string

Describes how the buyback will take place. Possible values are:

  • Authorisation - The board's authority to repurchase
  • Intention - Notification of the board that shares will be repurchased
  • Transaction - Repurchase transactions that have been actioned

intention.BuybackType
LastUpdate

DateTime

The date when a transaction is updated after it has been reported

intention.LastUpdate
LastIDsUpdate

DateTime

Date that company identifiers were changed. Can be a name, Ticker Symbol, or ISIN change.

intention.LastIDsUpdate
CompanyName

string

Company name. PLC is always excluded.

intention.CompanyName
PreviousResultsAnnouncementDate

DateTime

Announcement date of last results, this will be the end date of the last "Close Period"

intention.PreviousResultsAnnouncementDate
NextResultsAnnouncementsDate

DateTime

Announcement date of next results, this will be the end date of the next "Close Period"

intention.NextResultsAnnouncementDate
SecurityDescription

string

Type of security. Does not contain nominal value.

intention.SecurityDescription
AnnouncementDate

DateTime

Date Transaction was entered into Smart Insider systems. Where a transaction is after the London market close (usually 4:30 P.M.) this will be stated as the next day.

intention.AnnouncementDate
TimeReleased

DateTime

Time the announcement first appeared on a Regulatory News Service or other disclosure system and became available to the market, time stated is local market time.

intention.TimeReleased
TimeProcessed

DateTime

Time the transaction was entered into Smart Insider systems and appeared on their website, time stated is local to London, UK

intention.TimeProcessed
TimeReleasedUtc

DateTime

Time the announcement first appeared on a Regulatory News Service or other disclosure system and became available to the market. Time stated is GMT standard (UTC).

intention.TimeReleasedUtc
TimeProcessedUtc

DateTime

Time the transaction was entered into Smart Insider systems and appeared on their website. Time stated is GMT standard (UTC).

intention.TimeProcessedUtc
AnnouncedIn

string

Market in which the transaction was announced, this can reference more than one country.

intention.AnnouncedIn
IntentionVia

string

Describes how the transaction was executed. Possible values are:

  • Authorisation - Will take place via the open market place
  • Intention - Will take place via a companywide tender offer to all shareholders
  • Transaction - Will take place under a specific agreement between the issuer and shareholder

intention.IntentionVia
IntentionBy

string

Describes which entity carried out the transaction. Possible values are:

  • Issuer - Intention to buyback via the issuer
  • Subsidiary - Intention to buyback via a subsidiary of the issuer
  • Employee Benefit Trust - Intention to transfer shares to employees as part of remunerative plans
  • Independent 3rd Party - Intention to buyback via an undisclosed 3rd party, which is likely to be a broker

intention.IntentionBy
BuybackIntentionHoldingType

string

Describes what will be done with those shares following repurchase. Possible values are:

  • In Treasury - Will be held in treasury until they are sold back to the market
  • For Cancellation - Will be immediately cancelled
  • In Trust - Will be held in trust, generally to cover employee remunerative plans

intention.BuybackIntentionHoldingType
