QuantConnect hosts Economics Calendar and Indicator data provided by Trading Economics for 28 countries since 2013. Trading Economics data is divided into datasets by country and indicator. The data is relatively sparse with the most frequent sets being updated monthly.

There are two primary Trading Economics data formats supported in QuantConnect: $[TradingEconomicsCalendar, T:QuantConnect.Data.Custom.TradingEconomics.TradingEconomicsCalendar] and the $[TradingEconomicsIndicator, T:QuantConnect.Data.Custom.TradingEconomics.TradingEconomicsIndicator].
