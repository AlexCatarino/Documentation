QuantConnect hosts Economics Calendar data provided by Trading Economics for 28 countries since 2013. Trading Economics data is divided into datasets by country and indicator. The data is relatively sparse with the most frequent sets being updated monthly.

The Trading Economics data format supported in QuantConnect is the $[TradingEconomicsCalendar, T:QuantConnect.Data.Custom.TradingEconomics.TradingEconomicsCalendar] class. Click the red-type links () to show all of the available properties.
