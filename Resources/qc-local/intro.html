QuantConnect Local is the official VSCode plugin for QuantConnect. This extension enables you to seamlessly developing quant strategies on-premise and in QuantConnect Cloud, getting the best of both environments. With QuantConnect Local, you can harness your local version control, autocomplete, and coding tools with the full power of a scalable cloud at your finger tips. We intend to keep complete feature parity with our cloud environment, allowing you to harness cloud or local datasets to power on-premise quantitative research.
