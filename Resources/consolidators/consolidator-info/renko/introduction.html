Most Renko consolidators aggregate bars based on a fixed price movement. The RenkoConsolidator produces Renko bars by their traditional definition. A RenkoConsolidator with a bar size of $1 produces a new bar that spans $1 every time an asset closes $1 above or below the close of the previous bar. The open and close price of every bar is an interger mulitple of the bar size. If the price jumps multiple dollars in a single tick, the RenkoConsolidator produces multiple $1 bars in a single time step.
