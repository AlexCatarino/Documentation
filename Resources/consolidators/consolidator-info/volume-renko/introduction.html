Volume Renko consolidators aggregate bars based on a fixed trading volume. A VolumeRenkoConsolidator with a bar size of 10,000 produces a new bar every time 10,000 units of the security trades in the market. If the trading volume of a single time step exceeds two step sizes (i.e. >20,000), the VolumeRenkoConsolidator produces multiple bars in a single time step.
