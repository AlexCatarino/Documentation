QuantConnect.Orders.OrderStatus - Fill status of the order class.
Member Value Description
New 0 New order pre-submission to the order processor.
Submitted 1 Order submitted to the market
PartiallyFilled 2 Partially filled, In Market Order.
Filled 3 Completed, Filled, In Market Order.
Canceled 5 Order cancelled before it was filled
None 6 No Order State Yet
Invalid 7 Order invalidated before it hit the market (e.g. insufficient capital)..
CancelPending 8 Order waiting for confirmation of cancellation
UpdateSubmitted 9 Order update submitted to the market
