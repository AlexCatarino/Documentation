The following table describes the DataNormalizationMode class selector methods:

QuantConnect.DataNormalizationMode - Specifies how data is normalized before being sent into an algorithm
Enum Value Description
0 Raw The raw price with dividends added to cash book
1 Adjusted The adjusted prices with splits and dividends factored in
2 SplitAdjusted The adjusted prices with only splits factored in, dividends paid out to the cash book
3 TotalReturn The split adjusted price plus dividends
4 ForwardPanamaCanal Eliminates price jumps between two consecutive contracts, adding a factor based on the difference of their prices. First contract is the true one, factor 0
5 BackwardsPanamaCanal Eliminates price jumps between two consecutive contracts, adding a factor based on the difference of their prices. Last contract is the true one, factor 0
6 BackwardsRatio Eliminates price jumps between two consecutive contracts, multiplying the prices by their ratio. Last contract is the true one, factor 1
