The following table describes the CandleSettingType class selector methods:

QuantConnect.Indicators.CandlestickPatterns.CandleSettingType - Types of candlestick settings
Enum Value Description
0 BodyLong Real body is long when it's longer than the average of the 10 previous candles' real body
1 BodyVeryLong Real body is very long when it's longer than 3 times the average of the 10 previous candles' real body
2 BodyShort Real body is short when it's shorter than the average of the 10 previous candles' real bodies
3 BodyDoji Real body is like doji's body when it's shorter than 10% the average of the 10 previous candles' high-low range
4 ShadowLong Shadow is long when it's longer than the real body
5 ShadowVeryLong Shadow is very long when it's longer than 2 times the real body
6 ShadowShort Shadow is short when it's shorter than half the average of the 10 previous candles' sum of shadows
7 ShadowVeryShort Shadow is very short when it's shorter than 10% the average of the 10 previous candles' high-low range
8 Near When measuring distance between parts of candles or width of gaps "near" means "<= 20% of the average of the 5 previous candles' high-low range"
9 Far When measuring distance between parts of candles or width of gaps "far" means ">= 60% of the average of the 5 previous candles' high-low range"
10 Equal When measuring distance between parts of candles or width of gaps "equal" means "<= 5% of the average of the 5 previous candles' high-low range"
