The following table describes the DataMappingMode class selector methods:

QuantConnect.DataMappingMode - Continuous contracts mapping modes
Enum Value Description
0 LastTradingDay The contract maps on the previous day of expiration of the front month.
1 FirstDayMonth The contract maps on the first date of the delivery month of the front month. If the contract expires prior to this date, then it rolls on the contract's last trading date instead. For example Crude Oil WTI (CL) 'DEC 2021 CLZ1' contract expires on Nov 19 2021, so mapping date will be it's expiration dateAnother example Corn 'DEC 2021 ZCZ1' contract expires on Dec 14 2021, so mapping date will be Dec 1st
2 OpenInterest The contract maps when the back month contract has a higher volume that the current front month.
