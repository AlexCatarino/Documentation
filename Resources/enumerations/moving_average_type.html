Enum Value Description
0 Simple An unweighted, arithmetic mean
1 Exponential The standard exponential moving average, using a smoothing factor of 2/(n+1)
2 Wilders An exponential moving average, using a smoothing factor of 1/n and simple moving average as seeding
3 LinearWeightedMovingAverage A weighted moving average type
4 DoubleExponential The double exponential moving average
5 TripleExponential The triple exponential moving average
6 Triangular The triangular moving average
7 T3 The T3 moving average
8 Kama The Kaufman Adaptive Moving Average
9 Hull The Hull Moving Average
10 Alma The Arnaud Legoux Moving Average
