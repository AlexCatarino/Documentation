Open interest is the number of outstanding contracts that haven't been settled. It provides a measure of investor interest and the market liquidity, so it's a popular metric to use for contract selection. OpenInterest objects represent the open interest of contracts. They have the following properties:

Open interest is calculated once per day.

To get the latest open interest value, use the OpenInterest property of the Option or OptionContract.
