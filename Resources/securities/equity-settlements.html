If you trade with a margin account, trades settle immediately. In backtests, the default settlement rule for Equity cash trades is T+3 at 8 AM Eastern Standard Time (EST). For example, if you sell on Monday, the trade settles on Thursday at 8 AM. For more information about settlement modeling, see Settlement.

In live trading, cash trades generally settle at T+2. We plan to update the backtest settlement rules to T+2 so it matches live trading behavior. To track our progress, subscribe to GitHub Issue #6184.
