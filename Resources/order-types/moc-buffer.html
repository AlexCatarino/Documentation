By default, you must place MOC orders at least 15.5 minutes before the close, but some exchanges let you submit them closer to the market closing time. To adjust the buffer period that's required, set the MarketOnCloseOrder.SubmissionTimeBufferMarketOnCloseOrder.SUBMISSION_TIME_BUFFER property.

Orders.MarketOnCloseOrder.SubmissionTimeBuffer = TimeSpan.FromMinutes(10);
MarketOnCloseOrder.SUBMISSION_TIME_BUFFER = timedelta(minutes=10)
