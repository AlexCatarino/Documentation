Pattern Day Trading

Pattern day traders must maintain minimum equity of $25,000 in their margin account to continue trading Equtiies. You are classified as pattern day trader if all the following statements are true:

  • You reside in the United States.
  • You trade Equities in a margin account.
  • You execute 4+ intraday trades within 5 business days.
  • Your intraday trades represents more than 6% of your total trades.

For more information about the pattern day trading, see Am I a Pattern Day Trader?on the FINRA website.
