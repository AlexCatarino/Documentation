-To find the balance of a currency in the cash book, use self.Portfolio.Cashbook[].Amount. For example, self.Portfolio.Cashbook["USD"].Amount or self.Portfolio.Cashbook["BTC"].Amount
-To find the balance of a currency, denominated in your account currency, use self.Portfolio.Cashbook[].ValueInAccountCurrency
-To track these balances over time, you can use a dictionary, RollingWindow, or DataFrame (python).
