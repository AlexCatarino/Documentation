US Futures by AlgoSeek is for Futures data including price, volume, open interest, and expiry. The data covers the 189 most liquid contracts, starting from May 2009, and is delivered on a tick, second, or minute frequency. This dataset is created by monitoring the trading activity on the CFE, CME, CBOT, NYMEX, ICE, SGX, HKFE, India, and COMEX markets.
