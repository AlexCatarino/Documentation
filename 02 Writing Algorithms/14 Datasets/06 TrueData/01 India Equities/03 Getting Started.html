The following snippet demonstrates how to request data from the India Equities dataset:

from QuantConnect.DataSource import *

# Indian Rupee must be in the Cashbook to trade Indian Equities.
self.SetAccountCurrency("INR")  # or
self.SetCash("INR", 100000)

self.equity = self.AddEquity("YESBANK", Resolution.Daily, Market.India).Symbol
