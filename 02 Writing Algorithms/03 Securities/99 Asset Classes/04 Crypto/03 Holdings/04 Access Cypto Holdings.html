The cashbook stores the quantity of your Crypto holdings. To access your Crypto holdings, index the CashBook with the currency ticker.

var btcQuantity = Portfolio.CashBook["BTC"].Amount;
var btcValue = Portfolio.CashBook["BTC"].ValueInAccountCurrency;
btc_quantity = self.Portfolio.CashBook['BTC'].Amount
btc_value = self.Portfolio.CashBook['BTC'].ValueInAccountCurrency

To access the virtual pair of your Crypto trades, index the Portfolio object with the pair Symbol.

var securityHolding = Portfolio[_btcUsdSymbol];
security_holding  = self.Portfolio[self.btc_usd_symbol]
