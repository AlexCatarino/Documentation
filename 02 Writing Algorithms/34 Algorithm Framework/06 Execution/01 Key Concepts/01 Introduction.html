The Execution Model is primarily concerned with efficiently executing trades. It seeks to find the optimal price to fill orders and manages the orders. It receives a $[PortfolioTarget,T:QuantConnect.Algorithm.Framework.Portfolio.IPortfolioTarget] array from the Risk Management Model and uses it to place trades in the market, seeking to reach the units indicated in the Portfolio Target.

When the targets arrive at the Execution Model, they have already been risk-adjusted by the Risk Management Model. Like all models, the Execution Model only receives updates to the portfolio target share counts. You will not receive all the targets at once.
