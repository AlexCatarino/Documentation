The Universe Selection model creates Universe objects, which select the assets for your algorithm.

In live trading, the securities in your brokerage account are added to your user-defined universe. If your universe selection model does not select these securities, they are not removed from your user-defined universe.

# --
---- You can see these securities during the OnWarmUpFinished event handler
---- When you liquidate the positions, you can removed them from the user-defined universe. (if you call RemoveSecurity, it automatically liquidates the position)
