The LiquidETFUniverse adds various ETFs for the following categories at their inception date:

  • Energy
  • Metals
  • S&P 500 Sectors
  • Technology
  • US Treasury
  • Volatility

self.AddUniverseSelection(LiquidETFUniverse())
