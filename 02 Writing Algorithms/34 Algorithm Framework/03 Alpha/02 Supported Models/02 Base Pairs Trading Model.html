The BasePairsTradingAlphaModel analyzes every possible pair combination from securities selected by the universe selection model. The model generates alternating long ratio/short ratio insights emitted as a group. It calculates the mean ratio between the two securities by dividing their historical prices and taking the 500-period EMA of the quotient.

The model accepts the following arguments:
-lookback: Lookback period of the analysis; Default 1
-resolution: Analysis resolution; Default Resolution.Daily
-threshold: The percent [0, 100] deviation of the ratio from the mean before emitting an insight; Default 1 
// In Initialize
AddAlpha(new BasePairsTradingAlphaModel(lookback, resolution, threshold));
