There are two ways to request historical data in your algorithms, direct historical data requests and indirect algorithm warm up. A direct historical data request fetches any historical data you request at any time for a given Symbol. This gives you the most control to apply the data as you need.

Below we'll explore these two options and how you can use them in your algorithm.
