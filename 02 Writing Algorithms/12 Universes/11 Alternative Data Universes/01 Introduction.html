What is an Alternative Data Universes
- A universe you select based on a linked alternative dataset

Why use an alternative data universe
- If you incorporate an alternative dataset into your algorithm and only trade securities that are included in the dataset, you might as well reduce your universe size to the securities that are included in the alt dataset.
   - Removes unnecessary subscriptions, speeds up your algorithm.
   - If you don't use an alternative data universe when you trade off of alt data, there is a chance that the universe you select doesn't contain any securities that are in the alt dataset, making your algorithm no place any trades.
