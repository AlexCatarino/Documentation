A butterfly spread consists of four Option contracts with the same expiration, but three different strike prices. The following table describes the four variations of butterfly spreads:

Name Strategy
Long butterfly spread with calls Buy 1 ITM call, sell 2 ATM call, buy 1 OTM call
Long butterfly spread with puts Buy 1 ITM put, sell 2 ATM put, buy 1 OTM put
Short butterfly spread with calls Sell 1 ITM call, buy 2 ATM call, sell 1 OTM call
Short butterfly spread with puts Buy 1 ITM put, sell 2 ATM put, buy 1 OTM put

The ITM strike and OTM strike should be the same distance from the ATM strike. The butterfly spread strategy profits from marginal price changes in the underlying Equity. This tutorial uses the Long Butterfly Spread as an example.
