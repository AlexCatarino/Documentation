Long Call Butterfly Spread
----------------------------
What is a long call butterfly spread:
-Buy 1 ITM call Option at strike S_1, sell 2 ATM call Options, buy 1 OTM call Option at strike S_2 > S_1


What is the max profit/loss:
-Max profit: ATM strike price - S_1 - premiums - commissions
-Max loss: premiums paid + commissions

-Risk of early assignment since you sell contracts
