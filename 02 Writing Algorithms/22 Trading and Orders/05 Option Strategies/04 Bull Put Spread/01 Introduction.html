What is a Bull Put Spread:
-Buy X put Option contracts at strike S_1 with expiration E. Sell X put Option contracts at S_2 > S_1 at expiration E.

What is the max profit/loss:
-Max profit: net premium from entering the trade, occurs when the stock closes above S_2 at expiration E.
-Profit is limited if the stock goes far above S_2
-Max loss = S_2 - S_1 - premiumFromOpeningTheTrade X contractMultipler

- Since you sell an option contract, there is a risk of early assignment.
