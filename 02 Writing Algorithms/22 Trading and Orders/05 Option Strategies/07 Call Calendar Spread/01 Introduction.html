What is a call calendar spread:
-Sell X call options with strike S and expiration E_1. Buy X call options with strike S and expiration E_2 > E_1.
-The strike should be as close to the underlying price as possible
-AKA inter-delivery, intra-market, time spread, or horizontal spreads

What is the max profit/loss:
Max profit: No cap on profit
-Max loss: The amount paid to open the strategy

-Since you sell a call option, there is a risk of early assignment
