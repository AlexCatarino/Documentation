What is a bear call spread:
-Buy X call Options at strike S_1 with expiry E. Sell X call Options at strike S_2 < S_1 with expiry E.
-AKA Short Call Spread

What's the max profit/loss:
-Max Profit: The credit you receive from opening the trade.
-Max loss : "the difference between the two strikes reduced by the amount received, or credited, when the trade is initiated" (source: investopedia)
-If the stock price goes below the lower call Option strike price, no additional profit can be made.
-Limited-risk, limited-reward strategy
