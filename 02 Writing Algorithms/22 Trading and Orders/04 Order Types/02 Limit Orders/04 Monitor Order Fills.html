Limit orders fill when the security price passes the limit price. To monitor the fills of your market order, save a reference to the OrderTicket.

// Buy 10 shares of XLK at $140
var ticket = LimitOrder("XLK", 10, 140);
Debug($"Quantity filled: {ticket.QuantityFilled}; Fill price: {ticket.AverageFillPrice}");
# Buy 10 shares of XLK at $140
ticket = self.LimitOrder("XLK", 10, 140)
self.Debug(f"Quantity filled: {ticket.QuantityFilled}; Fill price: {ticket.AverageFillPrice}")

For more information about order tickets, see Order Tickets.
