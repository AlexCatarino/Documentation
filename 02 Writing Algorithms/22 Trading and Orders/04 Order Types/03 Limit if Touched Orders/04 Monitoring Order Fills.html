Limit if touched orders fill when the security price touches the trigger price and is at least as favorable as the limit price. To monitor the fills of your order, save a reference to the OrderTicket.

// Once SPY trades down to $425, place a limit order to buy 10 shares at $415
var ticket = LimitIfTouchedOrder("SPY", 10, 425, 415);
Debug($"Quantity filled: {ticket.QuantityFilled}; Fill price: {ticket.AverageFillPrice}");
# Once SPY trades down to $425, place a limit order to buy 10 shares at $415
ticket = self.LimitOrder("SPY", 10, 425, 415)
self.Debug(f"Quantity filled: {ticket.QuantityFilled}; Fill price: {ticket.AverageFillPrice}")

For more information about order tickets, see Order Tickets.
