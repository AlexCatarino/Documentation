What is a LIT order?
-Limit if Touched (LIT) orders are instructions to place a limit order once an asset touches a specific price level.
-They are opposite to stop limit orders in the fact that when buying (selling), the trigger price for a LIT order is placed below (above) the current market price.

Why use a LIT order?
-To delay placing your limit order until a price level is reached.
