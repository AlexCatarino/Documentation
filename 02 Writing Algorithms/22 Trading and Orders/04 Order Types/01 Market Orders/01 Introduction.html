What are market orders:
-Market orders are instructions to trade a specific number of units, regardless of the fill price.

Why use market orders:
-If your highest priority is to be filled as fast as possible, use market orders. If the order book of the security you're trading has sufficient liquidity when the brokerage receives your order, market orders offer guaranteed execution. If there is not enough liquidity by the brokerage, you will get partial fills.

Why not to use market orders:
-Market order can cause a lot of market impact and slippage, leading to a worse fill price than the current bid/ask when you submitted the order.
