To send a market order, you must provide a symbol and quantity. If you do not have sufficient capital for the order, it is rejected. By default, market orders are synchronous and fill immediately. 

var marketTicket = MarketOrder("IBM", 100);
var buyTicket = Buy("AAPL", 10);
var sellTicket = Sell("TSLA", 25);
var orderTicket = Order("SPY", 20);

market_ticket = self.MarketOrder("IBM", 100)
buy_ticket = self.Buy("AAPL", 10)
sell_ticket = self.Sell("TSLA", 25)
order_ticket = self.Order("SPY", 20)

You can provide a tag and order properties to the Order and MarketOrder methods.

var marketTicket = MarketOrder(symbol, quantity, tag: tag, orderProperties: orderProperties);
market_ticket = self.MarketOrder(symbol, quantity, tag=tag, orderProperties=order_properties)
