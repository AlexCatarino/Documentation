What are MOO orders?
-Market On Open orders are filled at the official opening auction price for the security.

Why use MOO orders?
-If you use daily data (to make your algorithm have speedy execution), all of your orders will be MOO orders.
-If you want to use the closing price in your algorithm to make trading decisions, the earliest you can trade (without extended market hours) is at the market open with MOO orders.

Why not use MOO orders?
-Some markets are open 24/7 so you can't use MOO orders (crypto)
