Stop market orders fill as market orders when the security price passes the stop price. To monitor the fills of your order, save a reference to the order ticket.

// When XLK trades up to $180, buy 10 shares
var ticket = StopMarketOrder("XLK", 10, 180);
Debug($"Quantity filled: {ticket.QuantityFilled}; Fill price: {ticket.AverageFillPrice}");
# When XLK trades up to $180, buy 10 shares
ticket = self.StopMarketOrder("XLK", 10, 180)
self.Debug(f"Quantity filled: {ticket.QuantityFilled}; Fill price: {ticket.AverageFillPrice}")
