- When you place a stop market buy (sell) order, set the stop price above (below) the current market price

To send a stop market order, you must provide a symbol, quantity, and stop price. If you do not have sufficient capital for the order, it is rejected.

// When Bitcoin trades up to $100,000, buy 1 Bitcoin
var buyTicket = StopMarketOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 100000);

// When Bitcoin trades down to $40,000, sell 1 Bitcoin
var sellTicket = StopMarketOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 40000);
# When Bitcoin trades up to $100,000, buy 1 Bitcoin
buy_ticket = self.StopMarketOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 100000)

# When Bitcoin trades down to $40,000, sell 1 Bitcoin
sell_ticket = self.StopMarketOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 40000)

You can provide a tag and order properties to the StopMarketOrder method.

StopMarketOrder(symbol, quantity, stopPrice, tag, orderProperties);
self.StopMarketOrder(symbol, quantity, stopPrice, tag, orderProperties)

To place a sell stop market order, set the stop price below the current bid price.
