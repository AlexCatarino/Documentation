Follow these steps to implement the Short Iron Condor strategy.

  1. In the Initialize method, set the start date, end date, cash, and Option universe.
    2. def Initialize(self):
    self.SetStartDate(2017, 2, 1)
    self.SetEndDate(2017, 3, 31)
    self.SetCash(300000)

    option = self.AddOption("GOOG", Resolution.Minute)
    self.symbol = option.Symbol
    option.SetFilter(-10, 10, timedelta(0), timedelta(30))
  2. Break the candidate contracts into the call and put options.
    3. def OnData(self, data):
    # avoid extra orders
    if self.Portfolio.Invested: return

    # Get the OptionChain of the self.symbol
    chain = data.OptionChains.get(self.symbol, None)
    if not chain: return

    # filter the call and put options from the contracts
    call = [i for i in chain if i.Right == 0]
    put = [i for i in chain if i.Right == 1]
  3. Sort the call and put options according to their strike prices. option.SetFilter(-10, 10, timedelta(0), timedelta(30)) helps us choose 21 call options and 21 put options which expire within 30 days from now. Then for the call options, the first 10 contracts are in the money and the last 10 contracts are out of the money. The middle one is an at the money option. For the put options, the first 10 contracts are out of the money and the last 10 contracts are in the money.
