What is a LIT order?
-Limit if Touched (LIT) orders are instructions to place a limit order once an asset touches a specific price level.
-When you place a buy (sell) LIT order, set the trigger price below (above) the current market price. In effect, they are opposite to stop limit orders.

Why use a LIT order?
-To delay placing your limit order until a price level is reached.
