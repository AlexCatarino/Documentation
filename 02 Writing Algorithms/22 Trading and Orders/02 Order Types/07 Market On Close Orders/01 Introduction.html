What are MOC orders?
-Market On Close orders are filled at the official closing price for the security.

Why use MOC orders?
-If you researched your strategy using the closing prices as the fill price, your algorithm should trade at the market close.
-It's the earliest point you can trade in the day that has a lot of liquidity. Premarket liquidity is low.

Why not use MOC orders?
-There is a chance that your order doesn't fill completely and you'll have to wait until the next day to complete your trade, or fill the remaining quantity of your trade outside of regular trading hours
