When are options exercised?
-Our platform lets you trade American style Options, so you can exercise Option contracts before the expiration date.
-You would only want to exercise an option if it's in-the-money, otherwise you would lose money. If your call option contract is out-of-the-money, you can buy the underlying shares cheaper in the stock market than you would buy them for if you exercised the option.

How do I exercise an Option?
-

To exercise an Option, call the ExerciseOption method with the Option contract Symbol and a quantity. If you do not have sufficient capital for the order, it is rejected. By default, Option exercise orders are synchronous and fill immediately. 

var ticket = ExerciseOption(contractSymbol, quantity);
ticket = self.ExerciseOption(contract_symbol, quantity)

You can provide a tag and order properties to the ExerciseOption method.

ExerciseOption(symbol, quantity, tag: tag, orderProperties: orderProperties);
self.ExerciseOption(symbol, quantity, tag=tag, orderProperties=order_properties)
