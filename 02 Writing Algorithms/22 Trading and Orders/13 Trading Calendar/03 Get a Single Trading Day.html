To get the trading events of the current day, call the GetTradingDay method with no arguments.

tradingDay = TradingCalendar.GetTradingDay();
trading_day = TradingCalendar.GetTradingDay()

To get the trading events of a specific day, pass a DateTimedatetime object to the GetTradingDay method.

tradingDay = TradingCalendar.GetTradingDay(new DateTime(2022, 6, 1));
trading_day = TradingCalendar.GetTradingDay(datetime(2022, 6, 1))
