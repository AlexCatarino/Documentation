To get all the trading events across a range of dates, pass start and end DateTimedatetime objects to the GetTradingDay method.

var startDate = new DateTime(2022, 6, 1);
var endDate = new DateTime(2022, 7, 1);
var tradingDays = TradingCalendar.GetTradingDay(startDate, endDate);
start_date = datetime(2022, 6, 1)
end_date = datetime(2022, 7, 1)
trading_days = TradingCalendar.GetTradingDay(start_date, end_date)

To get specific trading events across a range of dates, pass a TradingDayType enumeration member and the dates to the GetTradingDay method.

var tradingDays = TradingCalendar.GetTradingDay(TradingDayType.OptionExpiration, startDate, endDate);
trading_days = TradingCalendar.GetTradingDay(TradingDayType.OptionExpiration, start_date, end_date)
