An indicator universe uses technical indicators to determine the constituents of the universe. A common example is to select a universe of securities that are above their 200-day simple moving average. You can incorporate indicators into any of the types of universes in the Universes chapter. To create an indicator universe, define a SymbolData class that contains the selection indicators and then define a universe that updates the SymbolData objects and selects the correponding symbols based on their indicator values.
