(The type of data it receives)
- Data Point
      - Accept an IndicatorDataPoint
      - Examples: SimpleMovingAverage, ExponentialMovingAverageTests, Momentum
- Bar
     - Updated with a quotebar or tradebar
     - Since Forex and CFD don't have TradeBars for indicators, they use Bar indicators
     - Candlestick patterns are bar indicators
     - Examples: WilliamsPercentR, Stochastics, AverageTrueRange
- TradeBar
    - TradeBar indicators accept a TradeBar and use volume info
    - Examples: Beta, AdvanceDeclineIndicator, VolumeWeightedAveragePriceIndicator
