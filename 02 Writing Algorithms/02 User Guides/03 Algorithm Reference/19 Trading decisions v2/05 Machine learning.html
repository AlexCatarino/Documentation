What is ML:
- "Machine learning is the study of computer algorithms that can improve automatically through experience and by the use of data. It is seen as a part of artificial intelligence." // Wikipedia definition
- ML finds patterns by imitates biological processes (reinforcement learning, neural pathways, etc)

Why use ML:
- ML models are commonly used in quant finance to optimize portfolios, make trading signals, and manage risk
- ML models can find relationships in datasets that humans struggle to find, are subtle, or are too complex (ex: deep learning)
- ML models can be intuitive (ex: linear regression) and describe how variables interact to changes in other variables
    - More advanced topic than indicators, so there is less researchers/competition
