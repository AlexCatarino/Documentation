The US Energy Information Administration dataset enables researchers to monitor national and international oil production and consumption in their trading strategies. Examples include:<\/p>

  • Trading petroleum companies when there is a change in net imports of petroleum products<\/li>
  • Trading country ETFs when there is a change in the country's net import of resources<\/li>
  • Adjusting exposure to vehicle manufacturer stocks when the supply of gasoline is higher\/lower than historical levels<\/li> <\/ul>
