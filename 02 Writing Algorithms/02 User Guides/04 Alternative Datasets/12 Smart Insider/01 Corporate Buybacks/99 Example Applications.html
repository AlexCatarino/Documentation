Smart Insider Corporate Buybacks enables researchers to design strategies using information on company buyback programs. Examples include:<\/p>

  • Buying securities when the company announces an upcoming share buyback on the premise that the reduction in supply (shares outstanding) will drive up the remaining shares price<\/li>
  • Buying securities when the company executes an upcoming share buyback on the premise that the reduction in supply (shares outstanding) will drive up the remaining shares price<\/li> <\/ul>
