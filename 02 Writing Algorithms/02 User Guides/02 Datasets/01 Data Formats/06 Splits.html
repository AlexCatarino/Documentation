- What are splits? Increase or reduction of the number of shares to change the stock's liquidity. - Splits have a type of Warning and SplitOccurred - In backtesting, they are streamed to your algorithm in a Slice object that is emitted at midnight - It's information is used by the engine to adjust the quantity of your positions accordingly ("SplitOccurred") - If the quantity is not a valid lot size, the remaining value is credited to your account currency.
