Margin call model What is the margin call model -The model responsible for picking which orders should be executed during a margin call Why is there a margin call model: -To ensure the portfolio stays within margin limits specified by the brokerage. -To execute synchronous orders to bring the account within margin requirements. -To liquidate the portfolio immediately before the portfolio gets sub zero if there is a margin call, -To scan the portfolio and the updated data for a potential margin call situation which may get the holdings below zero What margin call models are supported: -DefaultMarginCallModel: orders the generated margin call orders by the unrealized profit (losers first) and executes each order synchronously until we're within the margin requirements -You can create your own
