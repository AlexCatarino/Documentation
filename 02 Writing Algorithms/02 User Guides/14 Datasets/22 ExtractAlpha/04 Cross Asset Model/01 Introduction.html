Cross Asset Model by ExtractAlpha is a stock selection model scoring algorithm designed to capture the information contained in Options market prices and volumes. Since the Options market has a higher proportion of institutional traders than the Equities market, the Options market is composed of investors who are more informed and information-driven on average.

The data covers a dynamic universe of over 3,000 US Equities, starting in July 2005, and is delivered on a daily frequency. This dataset is created by feature engineering on the Options market put-call spread, volatility skewness, and volume.

This dataset depends on the US Equity Security Master dataset because the US Equity Security Master dataset contains information on splits, dividends, and symbol changes.
