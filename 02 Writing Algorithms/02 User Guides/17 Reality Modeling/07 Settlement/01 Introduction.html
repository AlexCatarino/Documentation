Settlement model What is the settlement model: -The model responsible for applying cash settlement rules -After a trade has occurred, the brokerage needs to apply the change to the funds in your account Why is there a settlement model: -To apply cash settlement rules -The most common settlement type is immediate - where the funds are available for trading immediately. -US Equities trading with cash accounts is typically settled 3 days after the transaction occurred. What are the supported settlement models: -AccountCurrencyImmediateSettlementModel: This model converts the amount to the account currency and applies cash settlement immediately -DelayedSettlementModel: This model applies cash settlement after T+N days -ImmediateSettlementModel: This model applies cash settlement immediately -You can create your own
