The default brokerage model is the DefaultBrokerageModel, but LEAN has many other brokerage models you can use in your algorithms. To check the security level models the DefaultBrokerageModel assigns to securities in your algorithm, see the following pages:

  • Fill
  • Slippage
  • Fee
  • Buying Power
  • Settlement
  • Short availability
  • Option models
    • Pricing
    • Volatility
    • Exercise

To check the margin call model that the DefaultBrokerageModel uses, see Margin Calls.
