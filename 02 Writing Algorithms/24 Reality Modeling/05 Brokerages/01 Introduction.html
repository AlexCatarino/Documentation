What is a brokerage?
-Brokerages supply a connection to the exchanges so that you can automate orders using LEAN.

What are brokerage models?
- Brokerage models combine together all of the models relevant for a brokerage as a set. If you set the appropriate brokerage model, the fee models and supported order types will be appropriately set in your algorithm.


Why are there brokerage models?
-to simulate the live-trading behavior of a brokerage in backtesting
-To validate your orders before sending them to the real brokerage in live trading, this avoids invalid orders reaching the real brokerage.
