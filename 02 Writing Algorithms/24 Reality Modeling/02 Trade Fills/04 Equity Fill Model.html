- If you are trading Equities, the default model to use for filling trades in backtests is the EquityFillModel
- The EquityFillModel fills orders at the following prices (before applying slippage) and assumes the trades fill completley
    - Market orders
         - Filled at close price (close of QuoteBar if available; incorporating bid/ask spread)
     - Limit orders
         - Buy orders filled when lowPrice < limitPrice, filled at min(highPrice, limitPrice)
         - Sell orders filled when highPrice > limitPrice, filled at max(lowPrice, limitPrice)
         - The lowPrice and highPrice is from the QuoteBar, if it's available
     - LimitIfTouched orders
         - Buy orders triggered when lowPrice <= triggerPrice, filled when bidPrice <= limitPrice
         - Sell orders triggered when highPrice >= triggerPrice, filled when askPrice >= limitPrice
         - The lowPrice and highPrice is from the QuoteBar, if it's available
    - StopMarket orders
         - Filled when the high/low breaks through the stop price
         - Buy orders filled at max(stopPrice, closePrice + slippage)
         - Sell orders filled at min(stopPrice, closePrice - slippage)
         - The lowPrice and highPrice is from the QuoteBar, if it's available
    - StopLimit orders
         - Converted to limit order when high/low breaks through the stop price, limit filled when close price breaks through limitPrice
         - Buy orders filled at min(highPrice, limitPrice)
         - Sell orders filled at max(lowPrice, limitPrice)
         - High, low, close are from the QuoteBar, if it's available
