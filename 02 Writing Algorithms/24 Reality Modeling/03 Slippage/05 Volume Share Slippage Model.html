-The VolumeShareSlippageModel calculates the slippage of each order by multiplying the price impact constant by the square of the ratio of the order to the total volume.
-CFD, Forex, and Crypto data often doesn't report volume. If there is no volume reported for these asset classes, the model returns zero slippage
-If the bar volume is zero, the slippage percent is volumeLimit^2 * priceImpact.
-If the bar volume > 0, the slippage percent is
    min(abs(orderQuantity) / barVolume, volumeLimit)^2 * priceImpact
new VolumeShareSlippageModel(volumeLimit, priceImpact);
