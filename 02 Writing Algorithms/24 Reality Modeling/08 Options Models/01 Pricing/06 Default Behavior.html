The default Option pricing model is the BjerksundStensland for American Options or BlackScholes for European Options.

To turn off the Option price model, use the CurrentPriceOptionPriceModel. This model sets the Greeks to 0, sets the implied volaility to 0, and sets the theoretical price to the current price.

option.PriceModel = new CurrentPriceOptionPriceModel();
option.PriceModel = CurrentPriceOptionPriceModel()
