As your data reader reads your custom data file, the data points are included in the Slice passed to your algorithm's OnData method. To collect the custom data, use the Symbol of your custom data subscription. You can access the Value and custom properties of your custom data class. To access the custom properties, pass the property name to the GetProperty method.
