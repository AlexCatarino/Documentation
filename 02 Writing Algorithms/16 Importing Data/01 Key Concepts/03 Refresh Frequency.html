What resolution should you set?
- Match the resolution of your custom dataset
- The max reasonable resolute is every minute. Anything more frequent than minute will be very slow to execute.

What frequency should I sample my custom data source?
- If you are sampling a continuous waveform into discrete time steps, Nyquist frequency states that you must sample the waveform at least twice the frequency of the waveform to avoid aliasing.
- Aliasing is where you can't construct the original waveform from the samples because the samples were taken too slowly.
- For example, if your continuous dataset occurs on a daily basis, you must sample it atleast every 12 hours.
