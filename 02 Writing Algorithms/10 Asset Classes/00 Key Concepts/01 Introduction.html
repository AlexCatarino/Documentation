-Used to inform trading decisions
-Data is sourced from data providers
-All datasets are listed in the Datasets page (link)
-Differences between data providers (TradingView vs QC data is sometimes plotted differently)
    - Different timestamps (start or end of the period)
-Datasets are imported into your algorithm by creating a subscription
   - self.AddEquity, self.AddCrypto, self.AddData
   - Data is streamed into OnData/Update
         - One by one to avoid look-ahead bias and match live trading
   - Data is used to update the algorithm state: positions and portfolio value, and to fill the orders placed by your algorithm (in backtesting and optimization modes).  
   - Can perform history requests on the data subscription
