What is shorting:
-Taking a negative position; Selling before you buy to close your position
-The trade occurs in reverse order relative to a regular buy->sell order

Why short:
-To bet that a security's price will decrease in the future

How is shorting done:
-You need to borrow shares from another investor before you can sell them on the market. When you buy-to-cover, the shares you buy go back to the original investor.

Risks of shorting:
-Margin calls; complete liquidation of your portfolio;
-Incurring borrowing costs
