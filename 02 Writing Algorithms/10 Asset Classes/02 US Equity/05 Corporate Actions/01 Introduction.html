-If there is a split/dividend, you should reset and warm-up any indicators you have in your algorithm and any objects that are storing historical data. If you don't refresh them, they will contain discontinuous data from the split/dividend adjustment. This is only for if you are live trading or if you are backtesting with some other data normalization than Adjusted.
