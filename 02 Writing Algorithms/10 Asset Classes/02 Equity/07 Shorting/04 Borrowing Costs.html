What are borrowing costs:
-The fee to borrow shares to short. You must pay the investor that provided you the opportunity to short with their shares.

Why are borrowing costs important:
-If you don't consider borrowing costs in your backtests,
   - You may be placing trades when it's not economically viable to do so.
   - You may deploy a strategy live that had inflated backtest performance
   - Your backtesting results can be misleading

How to estimate borrowing costs:
-Currently borrowing costs are not modeled.
