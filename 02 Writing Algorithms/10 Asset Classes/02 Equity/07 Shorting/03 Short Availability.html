What is short availability:
-The number of shares that are available to be borrowed for short selling (comes from the brokerage).

Why is short availability important:
-Without knowing the short availability, you could have a strategy that performs well in backtesting but doesn't trade in live mode
-If you assume that your short orders are successful in backtests, your backtesting results likely won't represent the true performance of actually trading the strategy during the backtest period you test.

How to get short availability:
-The US Equities Short Availability dataset has historical and live access to short availability
