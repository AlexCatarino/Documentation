-Point values have no Period because they occur at a singular point in time
-Examples: ticks, open interest (link to OpenInterest), news releases, balance sheet figures

Tick data represents a single trade or quote made on the market. It is a discrete event that does not have a period of time attached to it. These events are emitted as soon as they arrive to LEAN.

$[Tick, T:QuantConnect.Data.Market.Tick] objects have the same value of Time and EndTime because they have no period. They represent instantaneous point values and cannot be filled forward.
