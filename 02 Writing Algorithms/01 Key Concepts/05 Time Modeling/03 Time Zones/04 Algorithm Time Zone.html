Algorithm time is accessed from the self.TimeTime property of QCAlgorithm. Algorithm time defaults to New York timezone for UTC-4 in summer, and UTC-5 in winter. This can be configured by passing an accepted time zone name into the SetTimeZone() method. A full list of time zone names can be found on Wikipedia. 

# In initialize method:
self.SetTimeZone("America/New_York")

// In initialize method:
SetTimeZone("America/New_York");

The result of setting a timezone is saved to your algorithm.TimeZone property. In addition QCAlgorithm maintains UTC time under the UtcTime property: 

# UTC Conversion of Algorithm Time
self.UtcTime

// UTC Conversion of Algorithm Time
UtcTime;

Both Time and UtcTime are timezone-less objects. Their state is maintained by LEAN to be consistent.
