What:
Timezone of the dataset

Why:
-Data timezones are tracked so that you receive the correct data at the right point in time in your algorithm. You can have multiple datasets in your algorithm from different timezones and they will be mapped to the algorithm time.
-Datasets can have different timezones because the timezone is up to the data provider, where they are located, where the data is collected.

How:
-To find the timezone of a dataset, see the listing page in the Dataset Market
