Before you can use the CLI to download data from QuantConnect's Data Library, you must add the data to your QuantConnect account. At the moment you can add Forex and CFD data to your account for free, and in the future you'll also be able to purchase data of other asset types through QuantConnect's Data Library.

To add data to your QuantConnect account, visit QuantConnect.com/data while logged in, browse to the data file you want to add to your account and click on the "Add to Cart" button on the bottom of the data file's page. After adding the data file to your cart, click on the "Cart" button in the top-right, read and accept the terms and conditions and click on "Proceed to Checkout" to start the checkout process. On the Checkout page make sure that the displayed data is correct and click on "Checkout" to finish adding the data to your account.
