Before you can use the CLI to download data from QuantConnect's Data Library, you must add the data to your QuantConnect account. At the moment you can add Forex and CFD data to your account for free, and in the future, you'll also be able to purchase data of other asset types through QuantConnect's Data Library. Follow these steps to add data to your QuantConnect account:

  1. Go to the Data Explorer while logged in.
  2. Navigate to the data file that you want to add to your account.
  3. Click on "Add to Cart" on the bottom of the data file's page.
  4. Click on the "Cart" button in the top-right.
  5. Read and accept the terms and conditions.
  6. Click "Proceed to Checkout" to start the checkout process.
  7. Ensure the displayed data is correct on the Checkout page.
  8. Click "Checkout" to finish adding the data to your account.
