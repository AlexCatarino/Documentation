The recommended way to source LEAN-compatible data for local usage is by purchasing and downloading it from QuantConnect's Data Library. This data is curated and maintained by QuantConnect and is easy to download with the CLI or by using the ApiDataProvider in LEAN.

Data charges are deducted from your organization's QCC balance. You can purchase QCC by clicking "Purchase Credit" on the bottom of your organization's billing page.

Recent data

Depending on your organization's tier your access to recent data may be restricted:

  • The Free tier is unable to download the most recent 72 hours of tick, second, and minute data.
  • The Researcher tier is unable to download the most recent 24 hours of tick, second, and minute data.
  • The Team, Trading Firm, and Institution tiers have no restrictions.
