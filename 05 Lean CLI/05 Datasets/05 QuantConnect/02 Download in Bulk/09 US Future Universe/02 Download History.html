To unlock local access to the US Future Universe dataset, open the Pricing page of your organization and subscribe to the US Future Universe History by QuantConnect data package. If you don't already subscribe to the US Futures Security Master by QuantConnect data package, subscribe to it too. You need billing permissions to change the organization's subscriptions.

After you subscribe to local access, to download the US Future Universe data, open a terminal in your organization workspace and then run:

$ lean data download --dataset "US Future Universe" --data-type "bulk" --start "20250403" --end "20250403"

To download the US Futures Security Master, run:

$ lean data download --dataset "US Futures Security Master"
