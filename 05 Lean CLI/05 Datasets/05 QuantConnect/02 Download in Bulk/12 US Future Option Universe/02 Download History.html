To unlock local access to the US Future Option Universe dataset, open the Pricing page of your organization and subscribe to the the following data packages:

  1. US Future Option Universe History by QuantConnect
  2. US Futures Security Master by QuantConnect
  3. US Future Universe History by QuantConnect

You need billing permissions to change the organization's subscriptions.

After you subscribe to local access, to download the US Future Option Universe data, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. On the CLI tab of the dataset listing, use the CLI Command Generator to generate your download command and then copy it.
  3. Open a terminal in your organization workspace and then run the command from the CLI Command Generator.

To download the US Futures Security Master, run:

$ lean data download --dataset "US Futures Security Master"
