Download the US Future Option Universe dataset in bulk to get the full dataset without any selection bias. The bulk dataset packages contains the available Future Option contracts, their daily prices, and volume for every trading day.

The US Future Option Universe dataset depends on the following datasets:

  • US Futures Security Master - information on symbol changes of the contracts.
  • US Future Universe - the universe of underlying Futures contracts.

To use the CLI, you must be a member in an organization on a paid tier.
