To unlock local access to the US Index Option Universe dataset, open the Pricing page of your organization and subscribe to the US Index Option Universe History by QuantConnect data package. You need billing permissions to change the organization's subscriptions.

After you subscribe to local access, to download the US Equity Option Universe data, open a terminal in your organization workspace and then run:

$ lean data download --dataset "US Index Option Universe" --data-type "bulk" --start "20250403" --end "20250403"
