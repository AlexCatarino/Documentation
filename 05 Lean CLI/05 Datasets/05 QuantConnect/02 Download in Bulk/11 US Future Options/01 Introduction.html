Download the US Future Options dataset in bulk to get the full dataset without any selection bias. The bulk dataset packages contain trade, quote, and open interest data for every ticker and trading day.

The US Future Options dataset depends on the following datasets:

  • US Futures - it contains data on the underlying Futures contracts.
  • US Futures Security Master - it contains information to construct continuous Futures.
  • US Future Universe - it contains the available US Future contracts, their daily trading volume, and Open Interest.

To use the CLI, you must be a member in an organization on a paid tier.
