To gain access to the entire US ETF Constituents dataset, open the Pricing page of your organization and subscribe to the US ETF Constituents History by QuantConnect dataset. You need billing permissions to change the organization's subscriptions.

After you subscribe to the US ETF Constituents History by QuantConnect dataset, follow these steps to download it:

  1. Log in to the Algorithm Lab.
  2. On the CLI tab of the dataset listing, use the CLI Command Generator to generate your download command and then copy it.

    3. The Ticker field is irrelevant for bulk downloads.

  3. Open a terminal in your organization workspace and then run the command from the CLI Command Generator.
