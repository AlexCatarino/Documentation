To view the asset classes that are available for your brokerage, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Brokerages page.
  2. Click the name of your brokerage.
  3. In the table of contents, click Asset Classes.

The brokerage data providers serve raw data. To get adjusted Equity data in local deployments, download the US Equity Security Master. To get continuous Futures data in local deployments, download the US Futures Security Master.
