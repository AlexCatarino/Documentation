What are fund projects
- “Our Fund Projects listing lets you learn the target alpha performance criteria from funds active in the QuantConnect ecosystem to guide your research. Eliminate the guess work from building an Alpha and tailor your strategy building to sophisticated clients.”

Why would I use fund projects:
- To see what funds in the market are looking for in terms of strategies
- To list your fund, to request certain alphas to be listed in the market, to motivate the community to write algorithms you want

What’s included in this page:
- Fund name
- Description of the strategies the fund is looking for
- Required backtest performance
- Alpha allocations currently owned by the fund
- Fund AUM
