What are upgrades:
- Upgrading to the latest version of the alpha, after the author has published code updates
-If there is an update to the alpha and you want to deploy it, you need to stop the alpha, delete the project, and get a new copy from the Submissions & Bids page

Why are there upgrades:
-The author may fix a bug, adjust some parameters, or add new logic/components
-When you license an alpha, you have the right to deploy the latest version of the alpha. Not the version that was running when you licensed the alpha.
