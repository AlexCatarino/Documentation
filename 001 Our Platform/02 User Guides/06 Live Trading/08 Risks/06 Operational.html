What are operational risks:
-Risks within a fund that relate to the business operations
   - Business risks
   - IT risks
   - external regulatory risks
   - changes to the law
   - Trading infrastructure risks
   - Employee risks (fraud, quitting)

Why are operational risks a risk:
-They are a result of the nature of a trading business, having employees, changing laws/regulations

How to address operational risks:
- Stay informed on regulatory/law changes
- Use open-source trading infrastructure (LEAN) that's maintained by experts
- Only bring on employees into your trading business that you trust and have signed appropriate documentation to protect the trading firm
- Use co-located servers so you don't have to worry about maintaining the hardware (or internet outages, etc)
