What is the holdings tab:
-A widget to show the current portfolio, including
   - symbol, avg price, quantity, market price, market value, unrealized profit on the position
-A place to manually add security subscriptions
-A place to see the current cash book of the algorithm

Why is there a holdings tab:
-To help you see your portfolio's current positions
-To manually create orders for securities and liquidate positions
-To help you manually add security subscriptions
-To display your algorithms's cashbook
-To give you an overview of your algorithm's current state
