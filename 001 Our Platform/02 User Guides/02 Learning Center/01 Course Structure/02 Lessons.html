What are course lessons:
- Lessons contain tasks, the first task is a video
- Lessons are made up of videos, reading, and coding exercises

Why are there lessons:
- To break up the process into into digestible tasks
- Each lesson builds on an understanding of the API from the lesson before it, so completing them in the order listed can feel intuitive
- The introductory video walks you through the process of completing the lesson (tasks) before you go about coding it yourself
