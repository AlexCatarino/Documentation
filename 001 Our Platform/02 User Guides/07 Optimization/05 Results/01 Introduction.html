What are optimization results:
- The results of each backtest the optimizer performed
- Besides just the equity curve of the backtests, 'objective' values are reported for each backtest that's run

Why do I need optimization results:
- To see how the changes in parameter values affects the performance of the strategy
- To see how stable/sensitive the parameter values are
- To take a closer look at individual backtests

How(?):
- Results are stored in the same "Results" tab as individual backtests. You'll know it's a optimization because of the '>>' symbol. Normal backtests have the '>' symbol. (show image?)
