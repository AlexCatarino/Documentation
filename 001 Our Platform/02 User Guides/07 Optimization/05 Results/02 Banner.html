What is the banner:
-A section at the top of the page that shows
   - Number of backtests that have started, completed, failed, are currently running, and are in queue.
   - The average amount of time to complete one of the backtests
   - The total runtime of running the optimizer
   - The amount of QCC that was used to perform the optimization

Why is there a banner:
-To present the optimization results in real-time as the backtests run on our servers
