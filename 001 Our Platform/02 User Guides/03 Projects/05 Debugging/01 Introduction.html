What is debugging: Debugging is the process of diagnosing and resolving errors in your code
Why use debugging:
      - Required to have an error-free project
      - Developers spend a large portion of their time debugging code that they have written
      - Errors are particularly costly in quant trading software (incorrect trades, downtime, faulty risk management logic, etc)
      - LEAN is under constant development, you may need to update your code in response to these changes, may break your algorithm
